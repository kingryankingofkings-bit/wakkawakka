# Lemon8 Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for Lemon8, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. Lemon8 In-App Photo Editor
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: A comprehensive image-editing tool that allows creators to crop, adjust contrast, apply filters, and tune color curves. It provides mobile creators with all the standard tools needed to optimize photo aesthetics before publishing.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Built-in Image Editor. Tap the "+" icon at the bottom of the navigation bar, choose a photo from the gallery, and select from the "Filter" or "Adjust" menus at the bottom of the interface.

### 2. Lemon8 Creative Layout Templates
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Pre-styled visual layouts specifically optimized for categories like Fashion, Beauty, Travel, and Food. These templates help creators design aesthetically pleasing, magazine-like posts with minimal manual effort.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Design Layout Presets. In the post editing interface, select the "Templates" tab at the bottom panel and browse through pre-configured styles, then tap one to apply it to your selected photo.

### 3. Lemon8 Font Styling Overlays
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: An overlay tool that enables creators to add customizable text titles and descriptions directly on their images. It offers a variety of fonts, colors, borders, and shadows to highlight key aspects of the post.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Text Formatting System. In the post editing screen, tap the "Text" tool, type your text, choose a font family, adjust background borders, and drag the text block to position it.

### 4. Lemon8 Sticker Library
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: A library of icons, arrows, pointers, and seasonal graphics that creators can place onto images. These stickers help draw viewers' attention to specific product details or design elements within a photo.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Graphic Overlay Asset. Tap the "Stickers" icon in the post creator toolbar, browse or search for a sticker (e.g., pointing hand), and tap to place it, dragging the corners to resize.

### 5. Lemon8 Multi-Photo Carousel
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows creators to upload up to 10 photos in a single post, creating swipeable slide carousels. This feature enables sharing structured storytelling collections, product reviews, or step-by-step guides.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Image Carousel Post. In the image upload gallery, select the "Multi-select" checkbox, tap up to 10 photos in your preferred order, and tap "Next" to edit them collectively.

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. Lemon8 For You Recommendation Feed
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A personalized home feed that uses machine learning algorithms to serve content tailored to the user's interests. It dynamically adjusts recommendation streams based on the user's historical interaction and viewing patterns.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Algorithmic Interest Stream. Open the app and tap the "For You" header at the top of the main home screen to scroll through personalized posts.

### 7. Lemon8 Category Navigation Tabs
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Horizontal navigation menus that divide the platform's feeds into interest topics like Fashion, Beauty, Food, Travel, and Pets. Users can tap these tabs to explore content in specific niches without relying on search queries.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Topical Directory Tabs. Swipe horizontally on the top navigation bar of the homepage or tap on tabs like "Fashion" or "Food" to view content specifically tagged under that vertical.

### 8. Lemon8 Keyword Search Console
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A search interface with auto-suggestions that allows users to find posts, accounts, and popular hashtags. It provides real-time search results to help users discover relevant inspiration and creator profiles.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Directory Search Interface. Tap the magnifying glass icon at the top-right corner of the home page, type search keywords into the field, and view filtered results divided into "Posts" and "Users."

### 9. Lemon8 Trending Topic Hashtag Directory
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A search-page listing that aggregates current popular hashtags and topic campaigns run by the community. Clicking on any hashtag opens a dedicated feed displaying all related posts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Hot Topics Finder. Tap the search icon, and scroll down to the "Trending" section to view a list of popular hashtags (e.g., `#ootd` or `#travelguide`), clicking on any tag to open its dedicated post feed.

### 10. Lemon8 Post Collections
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A bookmarking feature that lets users save inspiring posts and organize them into categorized, themed folders. These collections are saved privately in the user's profile for future reference.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Content Bookmarking folders. Tap the bookmark banner icon at the bottom of any post to save it, then tap "Add to Collection" to select or create a themed folder.

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. Lemon8 Follow System
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Subscribes a user to a creator's profile, prioritizing their posts in the "Following" tab of the homepage. It helps users build a curated feed of their favorite influencers and friends.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Creator Feed Subscription. Tap the "Follow" button next to a creator's name on their post, or visit their profile page and tap the blue "Follow" button.

### 12. Lemon8 Post Comments
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: A public messaging thread below posts where users can write comments and engage with the creator or other fans. It allows for detailed Q&A about products, travel tips, or styling suggestions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Public Discussion Feed. Tap the speech bubble icon on the bottom panel of a post to open the comments, type your message in the text field, and tap the upload arrow.

### 13. Lemon8 Heart Reactions
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows viewers to show appreciation for a post by double-tapping the image or clicking the heart icon. It provides direct engagement feedback that influences the recommendation algorithm.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Media Likes Indicator. Tap the heart icon at the bottom-left of a post feed item, or double-tap anywhere on the main image to light up the heart in pink.

### 14. Lemon8 Direct Link Content Sharing
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Generates direct links, QR codes, or custom cards to share Lemon8 posts across external messaging and social apps. It allows users to easily share inspirational content with friends who are not on the platform.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Cross-Platform Sharing. Tap the arrow share icon in the bottom-right corner of a post, and select "Copy Link" or choose from direct app shortcuts like WhatsApp or Instagram.

### 15. Lemon8 Template Application
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows users to reuse the exact visual templates and layouts designed by other creators in their own posts. It promotes community layout sharing and makes aesthetic post creation accessible to everyone.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Design Layout Sharing. When viewing a post that uses a Lemon8 template, tap the template tag button on the screen to load the template directly into your post editor.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. Lemon8 Direct Messaging
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Provides private direct messaging channels for users to send one-on-one text messages to each other. It facilitates private networking and communication between creators and followers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Private Chat Window. Go to the Notifications tab, tap the chat bubble icon in the top-right corner to open the inbox, select a contact, and begin typing.

### 17. Lemon8 Photo Sharing in DM
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows sending images directly from the device camera roll inside private chat threads. It enables users to share inspiration photos and product ideas privately.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Private Image Sharing. Open a direct message chat room, tap the "+" or image icon next to the chat text bar, select a photo from your gallery, and tap "Send."

### 18. Lemon8 Post Card Sharing
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows sharing Lemon8 post cards directly to a friend's private message inbox from the post view. It lets users share ideas and recommendations without leaving the in-app chat interface.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Direct Message Sharing. Tap the share arrow on any Lemon8 post, search or select a friend from the direct message sharing list, write a note, and tap "Send."

### 19. Lemon8 Direct Message Notifications Toggle
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: An alert mute setting that enables users to turn notifications off for individual chat threads or the entire DM system. It helps users manage their notifications and reduce distractions during busy hours.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Message Alert Customization. Open a chat thread, tap the three dots in the top-right corner, and toggle the "Mute Notifications" switch to active.

### 20. Lemon8 Chat Block and Report
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: A safety control to block unwanted users from sending direct messages and report malicious conversations to the help desk. It ensures user safety by preventing harassment and filtering spam.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Message Safety Control. In the top-right menu of a chat thread, click the three dots, select "Block" to prevent further contact, or click "Report" to submit the thread for audit.

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. Lemon8 Retail Product Tagging
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows creators to tag specific items in photos, linking them directly to external retail web stores. It provides viewers with direct shopping access to items featured in fashion and home decor posts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Shoppable Retail Links. In the post editing screen, tap the "Tag" option, choose "Product Link," input the retail URL (such as Amazon or Zara), name the item, and position the tag.

### 22. Lemon8 Affiliate Storefront Links
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows verified creators to link Amazon storefronts, LTK shops, or personal blogs directly in their profile bio. It helps creators monetize their influence by directing traffic to their affiliate hubs.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Bio URL Links. Navigate to Profile > Edit Profile, paste your affiliate or storefront link into the designated "Website" field, and click "Save."

### 23. Lemon8 Sponsored Content Flag
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A regulatory setting that enables creators to flag specific posts as sponsored collaborations or paid partnerships. It ensures legal compliance and transparency regarding paid promotions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Paid Promotion Disclosure. On the final publishing screen of a post, scroll to "Advanced Settings" and toggle "Paid Partnership / Sponsored" to on before posting.

### 24. Lemon8 ByteDance Brand Campaigns Portal
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: An in-app campaign center that connects popular creators with public relations agencies for sponsored content requests. It allows creators to apply for paid campaigns directly within the platform.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Sponsor Campaign Hub. Go to Settings > Creator Tools, tap "Brand Campaigns Portal," and review the lists of active campaigns, submitting your profile to apply.

### 25. Lemon8 Product Showcase Carousel
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A dedicated display carousel on a creator's profile page containing catalog listings of partnered brand products. It creates a personalized storefront for creators to recommend and sell curated items.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Storefront Display. Enabled in settings under "Profile Shop Settings." Once active, select products from your synced catalog to display in the profile tab.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. Lemon8 Creator Center Analytics
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A dashboard that provides creators with overall metrics on post views, profile visits, shares, likes, and comment counts. It offers valuable insights into which posts are driving the most engagement.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Creator Analytics Suite. Navigate to Profile > Settings (gear icon), tap "Creator Center," and select "Analytics" to view performance charts.

### 27. Lemon8 Individual Post Performance metrics
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Displays detailed statistics for individual posts, showing traffic sources, views, saves, and watch-time ratios. It helps creators identify their most successful content and optimize future uploads.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Post Statistics Inspector. Open any of your own published posts, tap the "View Insights" link at the bottom of the post card, and review the detailed analytics report.

### 28. Lemon8 Follower Demographics Dashboard
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Visualizes follower demographic profiles, showing gender splits, age distributions, and location percentages. It helps creators tailor their content and schedule posts based on their target audience.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audience Demographics. In the Creator Center Analytics page, click the "Followers" tab to view interactive charts displaying audience segmentation.

### 29. Lemon8 Creator Academy Tutorials
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A resource center with tutorials, guides, and trends to help creators improve photo quality and caption writing. It provides creators with regular updates on what styles are trending.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Creator Education Hub. Open the Creator Center and select "Creator Academy" to browse tutorials categorized by photography, layout design, and niche topics.

### 30. Lemon8 Draft Post Vault
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Allows creators to save, edit, and organize multiple draft posts, retaining edits, stickers, and links until they are ready to publish. It provides a convenient workspace for creators working on campaigns or content batches.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Content Drafting Manager. When editing a post, click the "Save Draft" button at the bottom-left of the final creation page. Access drafts via the "+" post creation screen under "Drafts."

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. Lemon8 Private Account Mode
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Sets the account to private, requiring manually approving or declining new follower requests. It limits profile and post visibility to approved followers only.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Account Privacy Settings. Navigate to Profile > Settings > Privacy, and toggle the "Private Account" option to active.

### 32. Lemon8 Comment Filtering System
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Allows users to filter and auto-hide public comments that contain specific keywords or offensive phrases. It helps creators maintain a positive and safe community environment under their posts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Comment Blocklist. Go to Settings > Privacy > Comment Filters, input custom keywords separated by commas, and tap "Save."

### 33. Lemon8 Post Visibility Control
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Enables setting the visibility of individual posts to "Public," "Friends Only," or "Private" when publishing or after. It gives users control over who can view specific uploads.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Selective Content Access. On the final post creation screen (or by clicking the three dots on a published post), select "Who can view this post" and choose the preferred visibility level.

### 34. Lemon8 Direct Message Permissions
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Restricts who can initiate direct message conversations with the user, helping prevent spam and unwanted contacts. Users can restrict incoming DMs to only people they follow.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: DM Access Control. Navigate to Settings > Privacy > Who can send me DMs, and select between "Everyone," "Followers," or "No One."

### 35. Lemon8 Content Reporting Tool
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: An interface to report posts, comments, or accounts that violate platform guidelines to moderators. It helps keep the platform safe by routing reported content to review teams.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Abuse Reporting System. Tap the three dots in the top-right corner of any post or profile, tap "Report," select the violation reason from the checklist, and submit.

## Category 8: Notifications & Time Management

### 36. Lemon8 Push Notifications Preferences
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Allows users to customize exactly which events trigger mobile push notifications, including likes, comments, and mentions. It helps users reduce screen time and manage in-app distractions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Push Notification Muting. Go to Settings > Notifications, and toggle the checkboxes next to individual items like "Likes," "Comments," or "Direct Messages."

### 37. Lemon8 Screen Time Limits
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: An in-app digital wellness tool that tracks daily app usage and locks the app with a passcode once the limit is reached. It encourages healthy usage habits and helps users manage their time.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Usage Limit Scheduler. Go to Settings > Digital Wellbeing > Screen Time Management, set a daily limit (e.g., 60 minutes), and enter a 4-digit passcode to enable the lock.

### 38. Lemon8 In-App Alert Bell
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: A dedicated notifications center inside the app listing all user comments, mentions, likes, and system alerts. It organizes all account activity in a single feed.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Alert Center Feed. Tap the "Inbox" icon (bell) in the bottom navigation bar to view your categorized activity logs.

### 39. Lemon8 Notification Pause Scheduling
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: A scheduler that automatically mutes all push notifications during specified hours of the day. It allows users to set daily quiet times during sleep or work.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Silent Hours Scheduler. Navigate to Settings > Notifications > Do Not Disturb Schedule, toggle it on, and configure your daily start and end quiet times.

### 40. Lemon8 Weekly Activity Report
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: An automated notification that summarizes weekly screen time, average daily use, and engagement rates on the app. It provides users with insight into their consumption habits.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Digital Wellbeing Summary. Access Settings > Digital Wellbeing, and toggle "Weekly Reports" to receive a summary push alert every Sunday morning.

## Category 9: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 41. Lemon8 Login via TikTok API
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Programmatic login API that allows developers of third-party apps to authenticate Lemon8 users using ByteDance/TikTok account credentials. It simplifies registration for external portals and partners.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Federated Identity API. Access the ByteDance Open Developer portal, integrate the TikTok Login SDK, configure redirect URIs, and request user authorization scopes for Lemon8 authentication.

### 42. Lemon8 Content Share SDK
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A developer SDK enabling third-party photo editors and travel apps to upload images and text directly to Lemon8 from their apps. It streamlines content workflows by bypassing local device storage.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Share Integration API. Download the Lemon8 Share SDK for iOS/Android from the developer site, link it to your app's sharing menu, and implement the share callback request methods.

### 43. Lemon8 Web OAuth 2.0 Authentication
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: The secure OAuth protocol interface utilized by partner platforms to verify user identity and request user profile permissions. It protects credentials while sharing access permissions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Secure Token Exchange. Register your web portal on the developer dashboard, call the OAuth endpoint `https://open.lemon8-app.com/oauth/authorize` with a Client ID, and capture the authorization code.

### 44. Lemon8 Brand Portal Webhooks
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Webhook APIs that deliver real-time JSON callback notifications to developer servers when brand campaigns or review requests are updated. It allows marketing agencies to sync campaign statuses instantly.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Event Subscription Webhooks. In the Brand Portal developer console, register an active HTTPS endpoint URL and select event subscriptions like `campaign.status_changed` to capture JSON callbacks.

### 45. Lemon8 Feed Aggregation API
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A RESTful JSON API used by ByteDance partners to pull public trending posts and display them in external widgets. It enables displaying curated lifestyle feeds programmatically on external websites.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Public Content API. Authenticate your API requests using a developer key, and execute GET queries to the endpoint `https://api.lemon8-app.com/v1/discovery/trending` to fetch JSON lists of trending posts.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. Lemon8 Account Linking System
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows users to link their Lemon8 profile with TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube accounts for cross-platform sharing. It makes cross-platform promotion and verification much easier for creators.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Social Connection Manager. Go to Settings > Edit Profile, tap "Linked Accounts," select the social platform, and follow the redirect prompts to authenticate.

### 47. Lemon8 Password Management Portal
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Security portal for setting, updating, or resetting the account password. It helps users protect their accounts from unauthorized logins and update expired passwords.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Credential Configuration. Navigate to Settings > Security, select "Password," enter your old password followed by the new password, and click "Submit."

### 48. Lemon8 Contact Verification Settings
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows adding and verifying phone numbers or email addresses to secure the account and facilitate recovery. It provides recovery points to prevent permanent account lockouts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Verification Settings Editor. Open Settings > Account and Security, tap "Phone" or "Email," input your details, enter the SMS/Email verification code sent, and click "Verify."

### 49. Lemon8 Account Deactivation System
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: A settings option that allows users to permanently delete their account and purge all data after a 30-day grace period. It ensures compliance with user privacy policies and data protection regulations.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Data Purge Console. Navigate to Settings > Account and Security > Delete Account, confirm the warning terms, enter your verification code, and tap "Confirm Deletion."

### 50. Lemon8 Language and Region Settings
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Configures the app display language and changes the content curation region for localized posts. It allows users to customize their interface and feed recommendations based on language and geography.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Localization Manager. Access Settings > App Settings > App Language, select your preferred language, and adjust "Content Region" to choose your location.
