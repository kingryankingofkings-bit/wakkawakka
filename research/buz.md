# Buz Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for Buz, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. One-Tap Voice Note Recorder

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows users to record and send voice messages instantly by holding a single button on the screen, capturing high-quality audio formats.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Push-to-Record. Press and hold the main microphone button on the screen, speak your message, and release to send the audio note.

### 2. Voice-to-Text Transcription Tool

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Automatically transcribes spoken voice messages into text overlays in real-time, aiding accessibility and readability.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Speech-to-Text. Under a received voice message, tap the "Show transcript" button, and the app will generate and show the text version of the audio.

### 3. Voice Filter & Effect Changer

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Applies digital audio effects (e.g., echo, pitch adjustment, robotic tone) to recorded voice clips before they are sent.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Modification. While holding the microphone button, slide your finger up to the wave icon, choose an effect (e.g., "Deep Voice," "Chipmunk"), and record.

### 4. Microphone Sensitivity Tuning

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Adjusts background noise suppression levels and microphone input sensitivity to filter out wind, traffic, or chatter.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Sound Levelizer. Go to Audio Settings, drag the sensitivity slider to establish a high or low input volume threshold, and toggle noise cancellation.

### 5. Voice Note Trim / Edit Tool

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows users to crop or trim silent or unwanted portions from their recorded voice messages before publishing or sending.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Trimmer. Double-tap a recorded voice note preview, drag the yellow start and end markers to crop the audio timeline, and click "Send."

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. Voice Status Search

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Permits users to search for active contacts or channels based on their active voice status icons (e.g., "Studying," "Gym," "Do Not Disturb").
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Status Directories. Open the search page, select the status filters, or type keyword cues to filter users showing matching voice statuses.

### 7. Nearby Voice Channels

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Discovers local public voice rooms or walkie-talkie circles based on GPS location data.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Geo-Location Rooms. Go to the Discover tab, select "Voice Rooms Nearby," grant location permissions, and join active nearby chatrooms.

### 8. Global Voice Channel Directory

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A public directory cataloging open voice streams and walkie-talkie groups categorized by language, topic, or region.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Public Directories. Go to the Discover tab, browse interest categories (e.g., Gaming, Music, English Practice), and select a room card to listen in.

### 9. Voice Note Tagging & Folder Search

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Organizes saved voice notes using tags (e.g., `#work`, `#ideas`), allowing users to search and play saved audio notes quickly.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Saved Audio Search. Under saved recordings, click a voice clip, add custom tags, and use the search bar to locate specific tags.

### 10. Discover Friends via Contacts Sync

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Matches users with contacts in their address book who have registered Buz accounts, suggesting them as friends.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Address Book Matching. Go to Add Friends, select "Sync Contacts," authorize contact list access, and tap "Add" next to matching users.

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. Walkie-Talkie Group Channel

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: A live voice channel where group members can broadcast live audio to all connected members in real-time.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Live Group Channels. Open a group chat, tap "Start Live Session," and members can tap the talk button to speak to the group.

### 12. Live Voice Listener List

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Displays the profile avatars of all users who are currently listening to a live voice channel or walkie-talkie stream.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audience Indicators. Open the active channel dashboard, view the participant list, and check who has their microphone enabled or is strictly listening.

### 13. Emoji Voice Reactions

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows listeners to send floating emoji reactions that trigger short sound effects while a user is speaking in a live channel.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Reactions. Tap the emoji reaction bar during a live talk, select a reaction, and floating icons and audio triggers will broadcast.

### 14. Co-Host / Channel Administrator Delegation

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Enables channel creators to delegate administrative permissions to other group members to moderate live audio streams.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Leadership Assignment. In Channel settings, select the members list, tap a member name, and select "Make Admin" to grant moderator capabilities.

### 15. Shared Audio Playlist

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Permits group members to upload MP3 audio files to a shared channel queue, playing music or audio synchronously.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Collaborative Audio. Open the channel playlist tab, click "Add Audio," upload an MP3 file, and queue the track for group listening.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. Instant Walkie-Talkie (Push-to-Talk) DM

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: A direct messaging function that immediately streams and plays voice messages out loud on the recipient's phone if they have the app active.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Live PTT Streaming. Hold the PTT button in a direct message window to stream audio to the contact's speaker.

### 17. Offline Voice Messaging Queue

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Saves recorded voice notes locally when offline and automatically delivers them when a cellular or Wi-Fi connection is restored.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Offline Syncing. If cellular signal is lost, record your message. The app queues the file and uploads it automatically when online.

### 18. Auto-Play Voice Sequence

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Plays multiple unread voice messages in a conversation consecutively without requiring the user to press play on each one.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Hands-free Audio Play. Open a chat with unread messages, tap the first voice message bubble, and the app will play all unread audio notes in order.

### 19. Whisper Mode (Low-volume PTT)

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Automatically routes voice playback from the external speaker to the earpiece speaker when the phone is held to the ear.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Proximity Audio Routing. Bring the phone to your ear while listening to a walkie-talkie message; the proximity sensor will route sound to the earpiece.

### 20. Audio Read Receipts

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Displays a visual indicator showing whether the recipient has played a sent voice message.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Listening Receipts. Look for the headphone icon next to your sent voice bubble; it will turn blue once the recipient has played the audio file.

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. Premium Voice Filters Pack

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Offers access to premium audio modulation and character voice synthesis packs for purchase.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Storefront. Open the Voice Shop, select a filter package (e.g., "Sci-Fi Voice Pack"), and complete the payment using mobile store billing.

### 22. Paid Channel Subscription

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows channel creators to charge a monthly subscription fee for access to exclusive live voice rooms or audio archives.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Channel Monetization. Go to Channel Settings > Monetization, set the subscription price, and link a Stripe account for payouts.

### 23. Virtual Gifting during Live Audio

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows listeners to buy and send virtual stickers and gifts to speakers in live rooms, which hosts can exchange for cash payouts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Virtual Donations. Open the gift panel during a broadcast, select a gift (e.g., "Golden Microphone"), and send it to the speaker using purchased coins.

### 24. Ad-Free Subscription Tier

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A monthly platform subscription plan that removes banner ads and audio promos from the app experience.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Platform Subscriptions. Go to Profile Settings > Upgrade, select the monthly ad-free subscription option, and complete the transaction.

### 25. Sponsored Audio Ads

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Short sponsored audio ads inserted between voice note playbacks in public interest channels.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Sound Ads. Advertisers use the Buz Ads Portal to upload short audio spots and bid on placement frequencies across public voice categories.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. Audio Creator Stats Dashboard

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Displays analytics for voice channels, including total listening hours, peak listeners, and listener country distributions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Channel Metrics. Open Channel Tools > Analytics to view dashboards showing listening duration, visitor numbers, and geographic metrics.

### 27. Live Room Audience Retention Tracker

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Displays retention charts showing when listeners joined or left during a live voice broadcast.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Engagement Analytics. Navigate to Live Room Analytics, observing the line graph representing user counts mapped against the stream timeline.

### 28. Voice Note Download Utility

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Allows users and creators to export and download voice messages to their devices as high-quality WAV files.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Media Export. Long-press a voice message bubble, click "Save as File," choose a destination directory on your device, and save.

### 29. Audio Transcription Export

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Exports the text transcriptions of conversations and voice chats into text or spreadsheet files.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Text Exports. Under Chat Settings, click "Export Transcripts," select the date range of conversations, and download the resulting TXT file.

### 30. Creator Audio Promo Codes

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Allows premium channel hosts to generate promotional codes offering free or discounted trial memberships to their channels.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Promo Campaigns. Go to Channel settings > Promotions, click "Generate Codes," define the validity period and discount percentage, and export code lists.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. Incognito Voice Profile (Voice Masking)

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Scrambles or alters voice pitch automatically in public channels to protect a user's real voice identity.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Voice Masking. Go to Settings > Privacy settings, toggle on "Voice Masking for Public Channels," and select a default filter style.

### 32. Block List Management

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Prevents blocked users from sending walkie-talkie voice calls or messaging you on the platform.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Contact Block. Open the user profile card of the target contact, tap the menu settings icon, and select "Block user" to stop communications.

### 33. Auto-Delete Voice History

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Automatically deletes voice message files from the app interface and device storage immediately after playback.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Self-Destructing Audio. Go to Settings > Chat Privacy, and toggle on "Auto-delete played voice messages" to purge audio files from cache.

### 34. Channel Moderation Filter

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Allows channel hosts to mute, kick, or ban members who post inappropriate or disruptive audio.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Speaker Moderation. Tap the participant icon in a live room, click a username, and select "Mute microphone" or "Ban from channel."

### 35. Safety Reporting Tool

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Allows users to flag abusive, explicit, or threatening audio messages for review by Buz moderation teams.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Flagging. Long-press the offending voice message bubble, tap "Report," select the violation category, and submit the audio clip for inspection.

## Category 8: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 36. Buz Voice Webhook Integration

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Delivers real-time HTTP callbacks to external developer endpoints when new voice messages are received and transcribed.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Developer Webhooks. Register a callback URL in the Buz developer settings dashboard, and select the triggers (e.g., `message.transcribed`) to receive JSON payloads.

### 37. Buz SDK for Games

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A mobile game SDK that allows developers to integrate Buz's instant voice walkie-talkie communication into external mobile games.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: In-Game Voice API. Import the Buz iOS or Android SDK, retrieve an app key, and integrate the PTT commands in game UI hooks.

### 38. Siri / Google Assistant Voice Shortcuts

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Integrates with iOS Siri and Google Assistant, allowing users to send voice walkie-talkie messages using OS voice commands.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Voice Assistant Shortcuts. Navigate to Settings > Shortcuts, select "Buz Voice Connect," write a verbal trigger phrase, and link it to a specific friend.

### 39. External Microphone Bluetooth Integration

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Integrates with Bluetooth headsets, utilizing headset hardware buttons to activate and record push-to-talk messages.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Bluetooth Drivers. Connect a Bluetooth headset, navigate to Audio Input settings, select the connected headset, and toggle "Hardware button PTT."

### 40. Smart Watch Walkie-Talkie App

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A dedicated app for Apple Watch and WearOS that lets users send and play voice messages directly from their smartwatch.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Watch Complications. Install the Buz companion app on your smartwatch, link your account, and tap the watch face microphone to talk.

## Category 9: Notifications & Time Management

### 41. PTT Push Alert Tone

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Allows users to select custom alert ringtones and walkie-talkie chirp sounds for incoming live voice requests.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Notification Soundboard. Open Settings > Sound alerts, click the "Incoming walkie-talkie alert" option, select a sound clip, and tap "Save."

### 42. Scheduled Quiet Time

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Configures a schedule during which incoming walkie-talkie audio will not play out loud, automatically routing alerts to silent notifications.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Silence Schedule. Open settings, tap Quiet Hours, toggle on, and choose your sleep times (e.g., 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM) to mute audio output.

### 43. Voice Message Read Alerts

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Sends a push notification to your device as soon as a recipient finishes listening to a voice note you sent.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Listen Notifications. Go to Settings > Notifications, and toggle on "Message listened alerts" to receive notifications on playback.

### 44. Alert Snoozing

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Allows users to temporarily mute notifications and live audio playback from a busy chat or channel.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chat Muting. Tap the top bar of a chatroom, click "Mute," and select a snooze duration (e.g., "Snooze for 1 hour" or "Snooze for 8 hours").

### 45. Channel Join/Leave Notifications

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Toggles notification alerts when users enter or exit a live walkie-talkie stream.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Participant Alerts. In Channel Settings, toggle off the "Announce join/leave" switch to silence membership change announcements during broadcasts.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. Phone Number Registration & Verification

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Registers and secures accounts using a mobile phone number validated by an SMS one-time password.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Phone Verification login. Enter your mobile number on the signup page, input the 6-digit OTP code received by SMS, and verify.

### 47. Federated OAuth Sign-In (Google & Apple)

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows users to log in securely using Google or Apple accounts, integrating authentication APIs.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Federated Login. Tap "Continue with Google" or "Continue with Apple" on the welcome screen, and verify to set up profile details.

### 48. Account Nickname & Profile Picture Customization

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows users to configure their public display nickname, avatar image, and brief bio status line.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Editing. Tap the Profile avatar, edit the display name text field, select a new avatar photo, write a bio note, and tap save.

### 49. Request Data Export (GDPR)

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows users to download a ZIP archive containing all their registered profile details, active channels, and audio transcripts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Data Retrieval. Go to Settings > Privacy > Download Data, click the request button, and wait for the download link to be emailed.

### 50. Account Permanent Deletion

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: A self-service portal to permanently delete a user account and purge all audio recordings and data from servers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Permanent Deletion. Go to Settings > Account settings, select "Delete Account," type your password, and click the confirmation button to delete all data.
