# WhatsApp Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for WhatsApp, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. Status Updates

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows users to share text, photo, video, and voice note updates that disappear after 24 hours.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Ephemeral Status. Tap the camera icon in the Updates tab to capture/select media, or the pencil icon for text, and share to Status.

### 2. Disappearing Messages

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Automatically deletes messages in a chat after a pre-selected duration of 24 hours, 7 days, or 90 days.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Ephemeral Chats. Open chat settings, tap "Disappearing messages," and select the default message timer.

### 3. View-Once Media

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Enables photos and videos to be viewed only once by the recipient, deleting automatically after being opened.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Single-view Media. Tap the '1' icon in the caption box when sending a photo or video to enable View-Once mode.

### 4. In-App Photo Editor

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Provides tools to crop, add text, draw, apply filters, and pixelate sensitive parts of photos before sending.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Built-in Media Editor. Tap the camera icon or attach a photo in a chat, then use the toolbar at the top of the editing screen to customize.

### 5. Voice Note Waveform Preview

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Displays a visual waveform of a voice message as it is recorded or played back, allowing users to pause and preview before sending.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Recorder. Swipe up on the microphone icon in a chat to lock the recording, view the waveform preview, and play or delete before sending.

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. Updates Tab Feed

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Consolidates Status updates from contacts and channels followed by the user into a dedicated tab for browsing.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Status Feed. Tap the "Updates" tab at the bottom navigation bar to view vertical lists of channels and horizontal Status previews.

### 7. Channel Search Directory

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Allows users to discover and subscribe to public broadcast channels by category, region, or popularity.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Directory Browsing. Tap "Find Channels" in the Updates tab, filter by region/category, or search keywords, and tap "+" to follow.

### 8. Deep Chat Search

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Enables searching for specific keywords, messages, links, media, or documents within an individual chat or across all chats.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chat Query Tool. Tap the search icon at the top of the Chats list to query all chats, or tap a chat's header and select "Search" for deep contextual queries.

### 9. Pin Chats

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Keeps important individual or group chats at the top of the chats list for quick and easy access.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Priority Chats. Tap and hold a chat in the list (on mobile) or swipe right, then tap the pin icon to secure it at the top (maximum of 3 chats).

### 10. Chat Archiving

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Moves inactive or low-priority chats out of the main chats list into an Archived folder without deleting them.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Folder Storage. Long-press a chat and select the Archive icon (box with down arrow). Access archived chats by tapping "Archived" at the top of the Chats list.

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. Group Chats

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Creates shared chat rooms where up to 1024 members can exchange messages, media, and participate in calls.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Group Collaboration. Tap the new chat icon, select "New Group," add participants from contacts, enter a group name, and tap "Create."

### 12. Communities Hub

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Links multiple related group chats under a single umbrella community, facilitating organized announcements and discussions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Organizational Portal. Tap the Communities tab, select "New Community," set the name, and link existing groups or create new sub-groups.

### 13. Group Call Screen Sharing

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows users to share their mobile or desktop screen with participants during a live WhatsApp video call.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Collaborative Video. During a video call, tap the screen sharing icon (a phone with an arrow) to broadcast your device's display.

### 14. Message Reactions

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Lets users react to messages using any emoji, avoiding unnecessary clutter in conversations.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Emoji Feedback. Long-press a message and select one of the default emojis, or tap the "+" icon to choose any emoji from the keyboard.

### 15. In-Chat Polls

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows users to create multiple-choice polls within individual or group chats to collect quick feedback.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Survey Utility. Tap the attachment icon (paperclip/plus), select "Poll," input the question and options, and tap send.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. Hands-Free Voice Notes

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows users to record voice messages without holding down the microphone button.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Hands-Free Recording. Tap and hold the microphone icon in a chat, then swipe up towards the padlock icon to lock the recording in hands-free mode.

### 17. Instant Video Notes

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows users to record and send circular, short-form video messages up to 60 seconds directly in a chat.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Round Video Messages. Tap the microphone icon to toggle to video mode, then long-press or swipe up to record a hands-free circular video message.

### 18. Live Location Sharing

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Shares a user's real-time physical location with a chat for a set duration of 15 minutes, 1 hour, or 8 hours.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Geo-tracking. Tap the attachment icon, select "Location," tap "Share live location," choose the duration, and tap send.

### 19. Contact Cards

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Shares a contact's phone number, name, and profile details directly into a chat.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: VCard Share. Tap the attachment icon, select "Contact," choose one or more contacts from your address book, and send as a card.

### 20. Document Sharing

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Sends documents and files of various formats (PDF, DOCX, ZIP) up to 2GB in size directly through a chat.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: File Transfer. Tap the attachment icon, select "Document," browse local files or cloud storage, and choose the file to transmit.

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. Business Catalog Showcase

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows businesses to display their products and services directly on their WhatsApp Business profile with prices, descriptions, and links.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Virtual Storefront. In WhatsApp Business settings, go to Business tools > Catalog, tap "Add new item," upload photos, enter details, and save.

### 22. In-Chat Payments

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Enables users to send and receive money securely directly within a WhatsApp chat using linked bank accounts or payment gateways.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Peer-to-Peer Payments. Tap the attachment icon or the currency symbol in a chat, enter the amount, authenticate with a PIN/biometrics, and send.

### 23. E-Commerce Cart

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows customers to browse a business's catalog, add multiple items to a shopping cart, and send the order as a single message.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Conversational Cart. When viewing a business catalog, tap "Add to cart" on items, then tap the cart icon to review and send the selected order.

### 24. Quick Reply Business Templates

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Enables businesses to save and reuse frequent messages to answer common customer inquiries rapidly.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Response Automation. In Business settings, go to Quick replies. Set keyboard shortcuts (e.g. "/thanks") that expand into full messages.

### 25. Click-to-WhatsApp Ads

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows businesses to run ads on Facebook or Instagram that open a WhatsApp chat window with the business when clicked.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Ad Integration. Created via Meta Ads Manager. Select "WhatsApp" as the messaging app destination to link social media ads directly to chats.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. Business Profile Details

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Provides businesses with an enhanced profile page listing operational hours, address, website, email, and business description.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Business Registry. Configure in Business settings > Business profile. Fill in fields to display professional credentials to customers.

### 27. Broadcast Lists

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Allows users to send a single message to multiple contacts simultaneously without creating a group chat, delivered as individual messages.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Bulk Notification. Tap "Broadcast Lists" (iOS) or the menu icon and "New broadcast" (Android), select contacts, and send messages.

### 28. WhatsApp Business API

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Connects medium and large businesses with customers at scale using automated backend integrations, custom CRM platforms, and chatbots.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Scale Developer Tool. Configured via the Meta Developer Portal. Integrates with existing customer service databases and ERP systems.

### 29. Messaging Statistics

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Displays basic statistics on the number of messages sent, delivered, read, and received.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Metrics Dashboard. In WhatsApp Business, go to Settings > Business tools > Stats to view analytical counts of chat interactions.

### 30. Automated Greetings

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Automatically sends a greeting message to customers when they first message the business or after 14 days of inactivity.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Greeting Automation. In Business settings > Greeting message, toggle the message on, customize the text, and select the recipients.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. End-to-End Encryption

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Secures all text, voice, media, and call data so that only the sender and recipient can read or listen to them.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Security Protocol. Activated automatically by default for all chats. Tap a contact's name in a chat, then tap "Encryption" to verify the 60-digit security code.

### 32. Chat Lock

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Secures specific chat threads behind a device passcode, face ID, or fingerprint lock, keeping them hidden from the main chats list.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Locked Chats Folder. Tap the contact or group name in a chat, select "Chat lock," toggle it on, and authenticate. Access via the hidden "Locked Chats" folder.

### 33. Biometric Screen Lock

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Requires Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode to open the WhatsApp application on the device.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: App Lock. Go to Settings > Privacy > App Lock / Screen Lock, enable the toggle, and select the timeout duration (e.g. immediately or after 1 minute).

### 34. Silence Unknown Callers

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Automatically silences calls from numbers not saved in the contact list, listing them in the Calls tab.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Spam Prevention. Navigate to Settings > Privacy > Calls, and toggle "Silence unknown callers" to on.

### 35. View-Once Screenshot Blocking

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Prevents recipients from taking screenshots or screen recordings of media sent using the "View-Once" feature.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: DRM Protection. Enforced automatically by the platform; attempting to capture a screenshot displays a blocked message screen.

## Category 8: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 36. Cloud API Integration

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Allows developers to host the WhatsApp Business API on Meta's servers, facilitating quick setups and messaging capabilities.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Serverless API. Set up on the Meta Developers Platform by adding the WhatsApp product to an app and generating access tokens.

### 37. Developer Webhooks

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Receives real-time HTTP POST notifications of inbound messages, delivery statuses, and user interactions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Event Listener. Configure the callback URL and verification token in the Webhooks section of the Meta Developer Console.

### 38. Custom wa.me Links

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Generates a short URL that allows users or customers to open a chat directly with a specific phone number.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chat URL. Format the link as `https://wa.me/<number>` where `<number>` is the full phone number in international format.

### 39. Embedded Signup Flow

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Integrates an onboard portal on developer websites to allow clients to register their phone numbers for WhatsApp API.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Partner Onboarding. Add the JavaScript SDK and Meta login buttons to initiate the WhatsApp Business Account setup.

### 40. Contact Sync API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Synchronizes the device's address book with WhatsApp to identify which contacts are active users.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Sync Engine. Request address book permissions on mobile OS, then make secure hashing queries to WhatsApp's servers.

## Category 9: Notifications & Time Management

### 41. Custom Group Tones

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Sets unique notification sounds and vibration patterns for specific group or individual chats.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Customization. Tap the group/contact name, select "Custom notifications," check "Use custom notifications," and select the tone.

### 42. Chat Mute Options

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Mutes notification sounds and alerts for specific chats for 8 hours, 1 week, or Always.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Alert Suppression. Long-press a chat in the chats list, tap the speaker icon at the top (or swipe on iOS), and select the mute duration.

### 43. High Priority Previews

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Displays notification previews at the top of the device screen, showing the sender and message snippet even when the device is locked.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Push Settings. Go to Settings > Notifications, and toggle "Use high priority notifications" to control preview displays.

### 44. DND Mode Silence

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Automatically silences incoming voice and video calls when the device is set to Do Not Disturb mode.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: OS Integration. Inherits settings from the Android or iOS Do Not Disturb configuration without requiring in-app setup.

### 45. Notification Vibrate Toggles

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Configures the vibration pattern length (Default, Short, Long, or Off) for new incoming chat notifications.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Haptic Feedback. Go to Settings > Notifications > Vibrate, and select the desired haptic feedback duration.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. Change Number Wizard

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Transfers account details, groups, and settings from an old phone number to a new phone number.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Account Migration. Go to Settings > Account > Change number, enter the old and new phone numbers, and verify via SMS.

### 47. Passkey Logins

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows users to log back into their WhatsApp account using biometric authentication (face or fingerprint) or device lock pin.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Passwordless Auth. Go to Settings > Account > Passkeys, and tap "Create passkey" to link the account to the device's key store.

### 48. Two-Step Verification

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Requires a custom 6-digit PIN when registering the phone number with WhatsApp again to prevent unauthorized access.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Two-Factor Verification. Go to Settings > Account > Two-step verification, tap "Enable," set the PIN, and add an email address.

### 49. Request Account Info

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Generates a downloadable report of account information, settings, and profile details (excluding messages).
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: GDPR Data Request. Go to Settings > Account > Request account info, tap "Request report," and download the ZIP file once ready (typically 3 days).

### 50. Encrypted Cloud Backups

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Encrypts chat histories stored in iCloud or Google Drive with a custom password or 64-digit key.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Secure Backup. Go to Settings > Chats > Chat backup > End-to-end encrypted backup, tap "Turn on," and set the password.
