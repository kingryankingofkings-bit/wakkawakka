# TikTok Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for TikTok, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. Green Screen Effect

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Replaces the video background with an image or video from your device's library in real time during camera capture.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Real-time Chroma Keying. Open the TikTok camera, tap "Effects" on the left of the record button, search and select the green screen icon, select an image/video from your gallery, and record.

### 2. Adjust Clips (Multi-track Editing)

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Provides an in-app timeline interface to split, trim, speed up, or reorder individual video clips and audio tracks.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Multi-track Timeline. After recording or uploading multiple clips, tap the "Adjust clips" or scissor icon in the right-hand menu, split individual clips, or drag to reorder.

### 3. Voice Effects & Filters

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Apply audio distortion effects to recorded voice clips post-recording.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Voice Modulation. Record a video, tap the "Audio Editing" or voice icon in the right-side editing menu, select a voice filter (e.g., "Joey" or "Robot"), and tap save.

### 4. Stitch

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows users to trim and integrate up to 5 seconds of footage from another creator's video into their own video.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Clip Insertion. Tap the "Share" button on a Stitch-enabled video, tap the "Stitch" icon at the bottom, drag the crop handles to select a portion, tap "Next," and record your contribution.

### 5. Duet

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Places your recorded video side-by-side with another creator's video in a split-screen format, syncing playback.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Split-screen Video Recording. Tap the "Share" button on a Duet-enabled video, select "Duet," choose your preferred layout (e.g., Left & Right, Picture in Picture), and tap the record button to film.

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. For You Page (FYP) Algorithm

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Algorithmic feed that surfaces videos based on user watch history, comments, and real-time interest profiles.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Machine-Learning Feed. Launch the TikTok app on your device, and the "For You" tab will be selected by default at the top of the main home screen. Swipe up vertically on the screen to view the next recommended video in the algorithmic stream.

### 7. Refresh For You Feed (Algorithm Reset)

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Resets your For You Page recommendations to the default state, clearing historical interest tracking completely.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Feed Reset Switch. Go to Settings and Privacy > Content Preferences > Refresh your For You feed, tap "Refresh," and confirm to clear interests.

### 8. STEM Feed

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A specialized secondary feed containing strictly educational videos about Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Curated Content Feeds. Go to Settings > Content Preferences > STEM feed, and toggle it to active to display a "STEM" tab on your Home screen.

### 9. Custom Search Filters

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Refines search query results by upload date (e.g., past 24 hours, past week) and popularity (most liked).
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Search Result Filtering. Type your query in the search bar, tap the filter adjustment icon (two sliders) next to the search bar, select "This week" and "Most liked," and tap "Apply."

### 10. Search Trends & Auto-Suggest

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Displays trending topics, music tracks, and hashtags with dynamic autocomplete suggestions in the search tab.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Trending Topic Discovery. Tap the search bar on the top right to view automated search trend boxes, and type terms to see real-time search suggestions.

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. Video Replies to Comments

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Creates a new video post that highlights and overlays a specific comment from a previous video as a sticker.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Video Comment Stickers. Open the comments of your video, tap the comment you want to address, tap the red video camera icon next to it, record your response, and place the floating comment sticker on the screen.

### 12. Repost Button

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Recommends a video to your mutual friends' For You feeds without saving or re-uploading the video yourself.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Peer-to-Peer Feed Sharing. Tap the "Share" button (arrow icon) on any video on your For You Page, and tap the yellow "Repost" button.

### 13. TikTok LIVE Q&A

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows creators to toggle a dedicated Q&A panel during LIVE broadcasts, letting viewers submit questions that the host can select and display on-screen to answer in real-time.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Live Stream Q&A. In the LIVE setup screen or during a live broadcast, tap the "Q&A" button. Viewers submit questions via the Q&A icon in the chat. The creator can browse submitted questions, select one to pin to the live screen for the audience, and answer it live.

### 14. Video Playlists (Collections)

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Categorizes public videos on a creator's profile into themed playlists displayed at the top of their grid.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Playlist Sorting. On your profile page, tap "Sort videos into playlists" (available to eligible accounts), name the playlist, select the relevant videos, and save.

### 15. Profile View History

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows you to see which users have viewed your profile in the past 30 days, and makes your profile views visible to them.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Visitor Tracking. Tap the footprints icon at the top of your profile page, tap the gear icon, and toggle "Profile view history" to on.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. In-App Group Chats

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Creates a private group message board within TikTok for sharing and discussing videos with up to 32 mutual followers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Group Chat Rooms. Navigate to the Inbox tab, tap the chat bubble icon in the top right, select multiple friends from your mutuals list, and tap "Start group chat."

### 17. Quick Send (Hold-to-Share)

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Instantly shares a video with a specific friend with a single long-press action.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Instant Message Sharing. Long-press on the middle of the screen while watching a video, and tap a contact's profile icon on the radial menu.

### 18. DM Read Receipts

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Shows indicators in direct message threads to let you know if a mutual follower has viewed your message.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Read Receipt Settings. Go to Settings > Privacy > Direct Messages. Toggle "Read status" to active to see when messages are read.

### 19. Giphy Integration in DMs

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows users to search and send animated GIFs directly within the messaging interface.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: GIF Keyboard. Open a direct chat, tap the emoji smiley face on the keyboard bar, select the "GIF" tab, enter a search term, and tap the desired GIF to send.

### 20. TikTok Direct Message Voice Notes

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows users to record and send private audio messages to their mutual followers directly within a chat thread.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Voice Messages. Open the Inbox tab, tap on a chat thread, press and hold the microphone icon next to the chat bar to record a voice message, and release to send.

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. Creator Rewards Program

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Pays eligible creators based on the high-quality views and engagement of original videos that are longer than 60 seconds.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Video Payout Systems. Navigate to Profile > Settings > Creator Tools > Creator Rewards Program. If you meet the criteria (10k+ followers, 100k+ views in 30 days), tap "Apply."

### 22. TikTok Creator Subscriptions

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Enables eligible creators to offer recurring monthly paid subscriptions in exchange for exclusive perks, such as custom subscriber badges, exclusive emotes, subscriber-only chat mode during streams, and subscriber-only video content.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: TikTok Creator Paywalls. Navigate to Profile > Settings and Privacy > Creator Tools > Subscription. If eligible, set up a monthly subscription price tier, customize badges/emotes, and publish exclusive videos or toggle subscriber-only chat.

### 23. LIVE Gifts & Diamonds

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows LIVE streamers to collect virtual gifts from viewers, which accumulate as Diamonds and can be withdrawn as cash.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Streamer Virtual Tips. Start a LIVE broadcast, and viewers will see a "Gift" icon at the bottom of their screen. Viewers buy Coins to send gifts.

### 24. Creator Marketplace

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: The official platform connecting brands with TikTok creators for sponsored campaigns and collaboration deals.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Sponsored Deal Directories. Go to Settings > Creator Tools > Creator Marketplace, register your profile, set baseline rates, and select content niches.

### 25. TikTok Shop Storefront Integration

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows brands and registered sellers to exhibit products directly on their profile under a shopping tab.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: E-Commerce Shop Integration. Set up a TikTok Shop Seller account via Seller Center, link your official TikTok account, and upload inventory to create a shopping bag tab on your profile.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. Creator Analytics Portal

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Displays detailed metrics on account growth, profile views, video view trends, and specific content performance parameters.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Page Metrics Console. Open Settings and Privacy > Creator Tools > Analytics to access charts detailing video views, profile views, likes, comments, shares, and follower growth patterns.

### 27. LIVE Analytics Dashboard

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Tracks real-time and historical LIVE streaming metrics including total play time, viewer retention, diamond earnings, and new subscriber growth.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Stream Performance Insights. Go to Creator Tools > LIVE Center > LIVE Analytics to view comprehensive summaries of broadcast length, peak viewer counts, and earnings breakdowns.

### 28. Web Content Scheduler

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Allows creators to schedule video posts in advance via the TikTok desktop web portal.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: TikTok Web Publisher. Log in to your TikTok account on a desktop web browser, click the upload icon, select your video file, write your caption, toggle "Schedule video," choose the target publish date and time, and click schedule.

### 29. Promote Tool

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Allows creators and businesses to pay to boost video reach, choose specific engagement goals, and define target audience segments.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: In-App Advertising Campaigns. Tap the three-dot menu on any of your public videos, select "Promote," choose a goal (e.g., more video views, website visits, or followers), configure the budget and duration, and select your target demographics.

### 30. TikTok Creative Center Trends

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Allows creators and businesses to search and analyze trending music tracks, hashtags, creators, and popular videos in real time.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Trend Research Database. Access the portal at `ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter/trends` via a web browser to filter trending audio and keywords by region, industry, and time range.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. Filter Video Keywords

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Excludes videos containing specified tags or keywords in captions from showing up on your feeds.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Feed Content Filtering. Navigate to Settings > Content Preferences > Filter video keywords, tap "Add keyword," type restricted words, and select target feeds.

### 32. Family Pairing

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Connects a parent's TikTok account to their teen's account to remotely manage screen time, DMs, and content filters.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Parental Control Linking. Open Settings > Family Pairing on both devices. Scan the QR code displayed on the parent's device using the teen's phone.

### 33. Bulk Comment Management

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Allows creators to delete, report, or block multiple accounts in bulk (up to 100 at once) from their comment section.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Mass Comment Moderation. Long-press a comment, tap "Manage multiple comments," select up to 100 comments, and select "Delete," "Report," or "Block."

### 34. Filter Comment Keywords

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Automatically hides comments on your videos if they contain specific keywords you define.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Custom Moderation Filter lists. Navigate to Creator Tools > Settings > Comments. Toggle "Filter keywords" to active, tap "Add keywords," and type your terms.

### 35. DM Permission Control

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Restricts incoming direct messages based on relationship status to prevent unsolicited messages.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Inbox Gating. Go to Settings > Privacy > Direct Messages. Select from: Everyone, Suggested Friends, or No One.

## Category 8: Notifications & Time Management

### 36. Screen Time Breaks & Daily Limits

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Limits app usage by locking the app or displaying a fullscreen reminder after reaching a custom time threshold.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Screen Time Limiters. Go to Settings > Screen Time > Daily Screen Time, set a limit (e.g., 60 minutes), and establish a passcode to lock the app.

### 37. Notification Category Mutes

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Toggle notifications individually for likes, comments, live events, and video recommendations.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Custom Push Alerts. Go to Settings > Push Notifications, and toggle the individual sliders off/on for likes, comments, or direct messages.

### 38. Sleep Reminders

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Alerts users to close the app at bedtime and mutes push notifications during their sleeping window.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Sleep Breaks. Go to Settings > Screen Time > Sleep Reminders, set your sleep time, and enable notifications to turn off during this window.

### 39. Push Notifications Scheduling

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Restricts push notifications from being delivered to the device during set hours of the day.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Notification Time Gating. Navigate to Settings > Push Notifications > Notification Schedule, and select hours to pause notifications (e.g., 9:00 PM to 7:00 AM).

### 40. Screen Time Dashboard

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Displays a comprehensive dashboard showing total daily and nightly app usage, open frequencies, and break histories.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Usage Metrics tracking. Navigate to Settings > Screen Time. Review visual bar charts detailing total screen hours per day/week.

## Category 9: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 41. TikTok Login API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: OAuth login enabling users to sign in to third-party applications securely using their TikTok credentials.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: OAuth Sign-In. Connect via developer SDKs using the platform's OAuth endpoints to verify user profiles.

### 42. TikTok Display API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Enables developers to securely fetch and display a user's public TikTok profile information, video feeds, and media metadata on third-party websites or applications after obtaining user consent.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: External Video Feeds. Register an application on the TikTok Developer Portal, request approval for the "Display API" scope, implement the OAuth authorization flow, and call the `/video/list/` or `/user/info/` endpoints to retrieve and render the user's video list or profile details on external platforms.

### 43. TikTok Content Posting API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Allows third-party developer platforms (e.g., Hootsuite, Sprout Social) to schedule and publish video posts programmatically.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Direct Posting APIs. Authorized partners configure developer API keys and submit video binaries and captions to the content posting endpoint.

### 44. TikTok Research API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Programmatic access for academic researchers to fetch public TikTok account information, video metadata, comments, and query performance data.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Academic Research Queries. Register the research project on the TikTok Developer portal, request access to the Research API, obtain developer credentials, and send queries to the endpoint https://open.tiktokapis.com/v2/research/video/query/ to download public post logs for social science research.

### 45. TikTok Webhooks API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Delivers real-time HTTP POST events to external server endpoints when users perform specific actions, such as video uploads or profile updates.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Webhook Server Subscriptions. Configured in the TikTok Developer Console by registering a secure endpoint URL, validating it via challenge-response, and selecting event scopes like video posting alerts.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. Two-Step Verification (SMS/Email)

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Protects account login via secondary verification codes sent to external phone numbers or email addresses.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Two-Step Login Security. Navigate to Settings > Security > 2-Step Verification, select SMS, Email, or Authenticator App, and verify your credentials.

### 47. Device Session Manager & Remote Logout

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Displays all devices currently logged into your account and provides instant remote sign-out.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Remote Session Termination. Go to Settings > Security > Manage devices, review active devices, and tap the trash can icon to log out unrecognized logins.

### 48. Verification Badge Application Portal

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: In-app portal to apply for verification checkmarks to verify public profiles and brand identities.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Verification. Navigate to Settings > Account > Request verification, upload credentials or business licenses, and submit.

### 49. Download Your Data

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Request a downloadable archive containing all your video history, comments, draft metadata, and profile settings.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: GDPR Data Access. Settings > Privacy > Download your data. Select TXT or JSON format, and request the zip file.

### 50. Switch to Business Account

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Toggles account type from personal to professional to access advanced commercial tools, page statistics, and auto-publishing.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Account Type Toggling. Navigate to Settings > Account > Switch to Business Account, select your business category, and complete onboarding.
