# BeReal Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for BeReal, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. BeReal Dual Camera Photography
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Captures front-facing and rear-facing photos simultaneously when the user takes their daily post. It ensures that the viewer sees both what the creator is looking at and their immediate reaction.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Dual Capture Interface. When the daily notification triggers, tap the camera button, align your shot, and tap the shutter button to take both photos at the same time.

### 2. BeReal RealMojis Selfie Capture
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows users to take specific selfie reactions to represent emojis (e.g., thumbs up, laughing face) for reacting to friends' posts. These custom reactions are saved in the user's reaction drawer to be used repeatedly.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Selfie Reaction Creator. Tap the emoji reaction button on a friend's post, choose a reaction type, line up your face in the circular frame, and capture your custom RealMoji.

### 3. BeReal Late Posting Marker
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Permits users to post their daily BeReal after the initial two-minute window has expired, attaching a timestamp showing how late they were. It ensures that users can still share their day even if they missed the immediate alert.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Late Upload Annotation. If you miss the prompt, open the app at any time later in the day and tap the "Post a Late BeReal" prompt to capture and publish your post.

### 4. BeReal Retake Tracker
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Counts the number of times a user re-shoots their daily photo before publishing it, displaying the count to their friends. It promotes visual authenticity by showing if a post was carefully staged or captured on the first try.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Creative Authenticity Analytics. Retakes are logged automatically when you delete a draft shot and click the shutter button again. The count is visible to friends when they tap the three dots on your published post.

### 5. BeReal Video BeReal Clips
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: An option that records a short, three-second video clip leading up to the static daily photo capture. It captures a quick snippet of live action to add motion and depth to the daily post.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Live Photo Recording. In the camera screen, tap the "Video" toggle in the top corner to activate video mode, then hold the shutter button to capture the motion.

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. BeReal Friends Feed
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: The main chronological feed showcasing the daily BeReal posts shared by a user's approved friends list. To encourage participation, a user must publish their own daily post before they can view their friends' uploads.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Private chronological feed. Open the application, and the default screen will display the "My Friends" feed tab. To unlock your friends' posts, you must first publish your own daily post.

### 7. BeReal Memories Calendar Archive
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A private grid calendar showing a chronological visual archive of all the user's past daily BeReal posts. It acts as a personal photo journal, allowing users to look back on what they did on any specific day.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Historic Archive Index. Tap your profile icon in the top-right corner of the app, and select the "Memories" calendar grid to click on specific dates and view past uploads.

### 8. BeReal Global Discovery Directory
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A public feed tab listing daily posts shared by users worldwide who have set their visibility settings to public. It allows users to peek into the daily lives of people from different cultures across the globe.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Public Post Search. From the main feed, swipe left or tap the "Discovery" tab at the top of the interface to browse through public uploads from around the world.

### 9. BeReal Discovery Geolocation Map
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: An interactive map view displaying the location pins of public BeReal posts globally, helping users find content in specific regions. Users can click on geographical pins to explore local posts in different cities.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Location Directory. In the "Discovery" tab, tap the map icon in the top corner to load a global map, and pinch-to-zoom to click pins and view local posts.

### 10. BeReal RealMojis Notification Widget
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A mobile home screen widget displaying friends' latest BeReal photos in real-time without needing to open the app. It updates automatically when friends upload new content, keeping users connected directly from their home screens.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Home Screen Widget. Add the BeReal widget to your device's home screen via system settings. The widget automatically updates its visual content whenever a friend publishes a new post.

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. BeReal RealMojis Reactions
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Enables reacting to a friend's daily post using a custom pre-recorded selfie RealMoji instead of standard keyboard characters. It creates a personalized way of responding to friends' posts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Custom Selfie Reactions. Tap the emoji smiley icon in the bottom-right corner of a friend's post, choose one of your pre-recorded RealMojis, and tap to apply it.

### 12. BeReal Post Comments
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: A private commenting section under a friend's BeReal post that is only visible to the user and their shared friends. It provides a secure space for friends to discuss their daily activities and plan hangouts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Private Conversation Threads. Click the "Add Comment" button at the bottom of a friend's post, write your comment, and press enter to post.

### 13. BeReal Contact Sync Friend Discovery
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Scans the user's mobile device contact list to find and send friend invitations to people they know. It makes building a network fast by identifying which contacts already have active accounts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Contact Book Matching. Tap the "Add Friends" icon (two silhouettes) on the top-left of the feed, click "Find Contacts," and grant permission to see recommendations.

### 14. BeReal Cross-Platform Post Export
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows users to share their daily BeReal to external applications such as Instagram Stories or WhatsApp chats. It helps users cross-promote their content and share daily moments with their other social networks.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Media Export Service. Open your published BeReal, tap the share icon (arrow or three dots), select "Share to other apps," and choose your preferred destination.

### 15. BeReal RealMoji Customization Panel
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: A manager within the reaction menu that allows users to delete and re-record their saved RealMojis. It gives users the flexibility to refresh their reaction selfies whenever they want.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Reaction Management. Long-press on any of your saved circular RealMojis inside the reaction tray, click the "Delete" trash can icon, and capture a new selfie reaction.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. BeReal Direct Chat System
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: A direct, private text messaging feature that allows friends to chat privately inside the application. It provides an integrated chat workspace to continue conversations started in comment threads.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Private Chat Rooms. Open a friend's profile page or tap their username on a post, and click the chat bubble icon "RealChat" to open a messaging thread.

### 17. BeReal Private Photo Sharing
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows users to send private, one-off photos to friends directly inside a private chat window. These photos are captured using the front and rear cameras to maintain the platform's signature dual style.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chat Camera Utility. Open a private chat, tap the camera icon next to the text input box, capture a photo using the front/rear cameras, and tap "Send."

### 18. BeReal RealMojis in Chat
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows users to send their pre-saved RealMoji selfie reactions as direct, standalone chat messages. It makes messaging more expressive by letting users respond with their actual facial expressions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chat Selfie Icons. In a private chat thread, tap the emoji smiley face inside the chat box, select a RealMoji, and tap to send it as a message bubble.

### 19. BeReal RealChat Notification Mute
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: A setting that silences notifications for a specific private chat thread, reducing incoming alerts. It helps users manage busy conversations without leaving the chat room entirely.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Thread Mute Control. Inside a private chat room, tap the contact's name at the top of the screen, select "Mute Notifications," and choose the mute setting.

### 20. BeReal Chat Message Deletion
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Enables users to delete specific text or photo messages from the chat view for both themselves and the recipient. It allows retracting accidental messages or clearing sensitive chats permanently.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Ephemeral Message Removal. In a chat thread, tap and hold the message bubble you want to remove, and click "Delete for everyone" in the pop-up options.

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. BeReal RealBrands Official Subscriptions
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows users to search for and subscribe to verified official brand accounts, celebrities, and creators. Subscribing adds these public profiles to a secondary tab within the user's daily feed.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Brand Following Portal. Open the search menu, type a verified brand username (e.g., "Adidas"), and tap the "Subscribe" button to add them to your feed.

### 22. BeReal Sponsored Behind-The-Scenes Campaigns
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A promotional placement tool that enables official brand partners to post behind-the-scenes content in the Discovery feed. It allows brands to engage with users in the same candid format as normal profiles.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Branded Campaigns. Sponsored posts appear directly in the Discovery feed. Users can tap the brand's profile card to view details or promotional offers.

### 23. BeReal Branded Product Tagging
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows official brand accounts to tag products in their daily BeReal posts, routing users to their digital shop. Viewers can tap these tags to explore product specifications and purchase details.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Product Link Tags. Verified brand accounts can click "Add Tags" when posting, search their product catalog, and place a tag. Viewers tap the tag to open the shop page.

### 24. BeReal Promotional Feed Placement
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Interstitial advertising slots inserted between friends' posts on the home feed. These placements are labeled clearly and allow users to discover products while catching up with friends.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Ad Integration. Promoted posts are inserted dynamically by the system. Users can interact by rating them or dismiss them by tapping "Hide Ad."

### 25. BeReal In-App Digital Item Purchase Shop
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: An in-app storefront created by Voodoo that allows users to purchase premium frames, visual themes, or emoji packs. It provides a non-intrusive monetization model that lets users customize their profile aesthetics.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Digital Microtransactions. Navigate to settings, select "Digital Shop," browse premium aesthetic skins or visual frames, and complete the transaction using Apple Pay or Google Pay.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. BeReal RealBrands Analytics Dashboard
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: An analytics control panel for verified brand profiles displaying overall views, shares, and reaction rates. It provides brands with numerical insight into their campaign effectiveness and community reach.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Brand Analytics Suite. Log in to your verified brand account, navigate to profile settings, and click "Insights" to view engagement charts.

### 27. BeReal RealBrands Follower Growth Tracker
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A tracking widget showing weekly statistics for new subscribers, retention rates, and unfollow trends. It helps creators and brand marketing teams monitor their audience growth over time.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Follower Growth Metrics. In the Insights tab, select the "Subscribers" header to view charts displaying weekly user acquisition.

### 28. BeReal Post Engagement Statistics
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Displays detailed statistics on how many subscribers reacted to a post and which RealMoji emojis were used most. It allows brand coordinators to analyze which styles of content resonate best with their target audience.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Post Interaction Analytics. Open any of your brand's published posts, tap the "View Statistics" link, and review the detailed breakdown.

### 29. BeReal Campaign Scheduling Console
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: An interface that enables verified brand accounts to schedule promotional posts to align with campaign timelines. It provides marketing coordinators with a tool to manage and queue content in advance.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Brand Post Scheduler. In the brand creator panel, select "Campaigns," draft a post, click "Schedule," and set the release time window.

### 30. BeReal Audience Location Demographics
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Aggregates demographic location data showing where the brand's active followers are located globally. It helps brands customize their posting schedules and product offerings for specific geographic regions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Geographical Demographics. In the Insights console, tap "Audience," and scroll down to the "Locations" map to view regions with high subscriber concentrations.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. BeReal Post Audience Selector
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Enables users to set their daily post visibility to either "Friends Only" or "Discovery" (Public) before uploading. It allows users to keep their daily lives private or share them globally.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audience Privacy Controls. When taking your BeReal, tap the privacy icon at the bottom of the screen to choose between "My Friends Only" or "Discovery" before tapping send.

### 32. BeReal Geolocation Toggle
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Allows users to enable or disable sharing their precise GPS coordinates on their daily post. Disabling location removes the map pin while keeping the photo viewable.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Precise Location Control. On the post preview screen, tap the location indicator icon to toggle it to "Location Off" or "Location On" before publishing.

### 33. BeReal User Blocking
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Blocks a user, preventing them from finding your account, viewing your posts, or sending you chat requests. It is a critical safety tool to stop harassment and enforce personal boundaries.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Access Restrictions. Go to the user's profile page, tap the three dots in the top-right corner, and select "Block User."

### 34. BeReal Content Reporting System
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: An in-app flag tool that allows users to report posts, comments, or RealMojis that violate guidelines. Reported content is flagged and sent directly to review teams for moderation.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Abuse Reporting System. Tap the three dots on the post you want to report, select "Report," choose the violation type from the list, and submit.

### 35. BeReal Friend List Hiding
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: A privacy setting that hides the user's friend list from other users on the platform. It helps protect user privacy and prevents strangers from mining contact lists.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Contact Privacy Control. Navigate to Settings > Privacy, and toggle the "Hide Friend List" option to active.

## Category 8: Notifications & Time Management

### 36. BeReal Simultaneous Daily Push Alert
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: The daily push notification sent simultaneously to all users in a specific region, prompting them to post within two minutes. It creates a shared daily moment where everyone posts their current status together.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Regional Notification Alert. Triggered automatically at a randomized time daily. Tap the notification banner to launch the camera and start the two-minute countdown.

### 37. BeReal Region Selection Settings
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Configures the geographic region of the user's account to align the daily notification with local time. It ensures that the notification triggers during daytime hours for the user's current location.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Time Zone Customization. Open Settings > Time Zone, select your current continent/region, and click "Save" to align daily notifications with local daytime.

### 38. BeReal Notification Snooze Settings
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: A setting that silences go-live push notifications during work hours or personal events. It allows users to prevent interruptions without permanently disabling app notifications.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: App Alerts Silencing. Navigate to device system settings, select BeReal > Notifications, and customize your alert schedule.

### 39. BeReal Live Feed Updates Pause
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: A control that pauses feed synchronization, reducing battery usage and screen time. It lets users manual refresh the feed only when they choose.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Sync Toggle. Go to Settings > Preferences, and toggle "Background Refresh" to off.

### 40. BeReal Custom Alert Tone Selection
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Allows users to select custom audio alert tones for BeReal notifications on their mobile devices. It lets users personalize the daily notification alert to stand out from other app sounds.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Alert Audio Personalization. Navigate to Settings > Notifications > Ringtone, and select your preferred audio tone.

## Category 9: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 41. BeReal Spotify API Integration
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A programmatic linkage that enables the BeReal app to check the user's Spotify active playback and share music details on their posts. It displays the song title, artist, and album art directly on the daily photo.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Spotify Integration API. Open Settings > Integrations, tap "Link Spotify," log in to your Spotify account, and grant the application permissions to query your currently playing track details.

### 42. BeReal Apple Music API Integration
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Programmatic API integration that fetches details of the user's currently playing track from Apple Music to display on daily posts. It allows viewers to click the track to listen to a preview or save it to their library.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Apple Music API. Go to Settings > Integrations, tap "Link Apple Music," and authorize the app to access your music activity permissions on your device.

### 43. BeReal OAuth 2.0 Web Authentication
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A secure OAuth identity verification endpoint that allows third-party services to authenticate users using their BeReal login details. It simplifies registration on developer portals and partner sites.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Federated Identity API. Access the BeReal Developer Portal, register your app, redirect users to `https://auth.bereal.team/oauth/authorize`, and exchange authorization codes for access tokens.

### 44. BeReal RealMojis Web SDK
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A developer SDK that allows integrating BeReal custom RealMoji selfie reactions into third-party blogging platforms or comment widgets. It extends the platform's unique reaction format to external websites.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: RealMoji Integration SDK. Download the SDK package from the developer site, embed it in your web app, and use methods like `BeRealRealMoji.load()` to display user reaction icons.

### 45. BeReal Post Published Webhook
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Webhook integration that sends real-time HTTPS POST callbacks with JSON payloads to developer servers when a user uploads their daily post. It allows developers to trigger external automations or update stats immediately.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Event Notification Webhook. Register a webhook callback URL in your developer dashboard, select the event `post.published`, and write a server script to capture the JSON notification metadata.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. BeReal Phone Number Sign-in Verification
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: A passwordless authentication system that verifies user identity by sending a secure SMS verification code. It speeds up sign-in while maintaining high account security.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Secure Login Verification. Enter your registered phone number on the login screen, receive the 6-digit SMS verification code, and type it into the app to log in.

### 47. BeReal Contact Sync Control
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: A settings control that enables or disables syncing contacts, with options to delete previously synced data. It gives users complete control over how their phone contact books are used by the platform.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Sync Settings Manager. Open Settings > Privacy > Contact Syncing, and toggle the switch to active or tap "Delete Synced Contacts" to purge stored data.

### 48. BeReal Account Deactivation Request
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: An in-app account deletion tool that initiates the process of permanently deleting all user data and media. It satisfies privacy regulations by allowing users to wipe their profiles completely.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Permanent Data Deletion. Navigate to Settings > Account > Delete Account, select a reason, confirm your password, and submit the deletion request.

### 49. BeReal Data Archive Export Request
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows users to request a downloadable archive containing all their account history, comments, and media. It provides data portability, packaging all historic BeReal photos into a secure ZIP file.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Personal Data Download. Go to Settings > Privacy > Download My Data, tap "Request Export," and wait for the download link to be emailed.

### 50. BeReal Active Sessions Manager
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Displays a history of active devices, IP addresses, and locations that are logged in, with options to log out. It allows users to detect suspicious access and terminate rogue sessions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Security Session Auditing. Navigate to Settings > Security > Active Sessions, review the list of devices, and click "Terminate Session" to log out any unrecognized device.
