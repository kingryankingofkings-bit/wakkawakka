# LinkedIn Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for LinkedIn, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. LinkedIn Articles Editor
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: A long-form publishing tool that allows users to write blog-style articles directly on the platform. It supports adding header images, styling text with markdown, and embedding hyperlinks or multimedia elements.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Professional Blog Editor. Click the "Write article" button in the post composer on your desktop homepage, enter your header and text, and click publish.

### 2. LinkedIn Carousel Posts
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows users to post multiple images or PDF slides that display as a swipeable carousel in the feed. This format is widely used for sharing slide decks, step-by-step guides, and graphics.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Document-Based Slider. Click the "Share document" icon in the post composer, upload your PDF or presentation file, add a caption, and post it to create a carousel.

### 3. LinkedIn Video Uploads & Captions
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: A native video uploader supporting professional and educational video sharing. Creators can upload custom SRT subtitle files to ensure that videos are accessible when played without sound.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Native Professional Video. Select the video icon in the post composer, upload your video file, click "Select caption file" to add your SRT track, and post.

### 4. LinkedIn Post Composer Templates
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Preset visual backgrounds and text layouts designed to make short posts stand out. Users can choose from various color schemes, layouts, and pre-formatted text headers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Graphic Post Creator. Open the post composer, select "Create a template," choose your desired background layout, write your text, and click done.

### 5. LinkedIn Newsletter Creator
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows creators to group their articles under a subscription newsletter feed. Subscribers receive automatic email and in-app notifications whenever a new issue is published.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Professional Newsletter Hub. Tap the "Write article" button, click the "Create newsletter" option in the article editor header, set your title and publishing frequency, and save.

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. LinkedIn Advanced People Search
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A search tool that allows users to find professional profiles using specific filters. Users can refine searches by industry, current or past employer, geographic location, and connection degree.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Targeted Profile Finder. Type a name or role in the search bar, click search, click "All Filters" at the top of the page, apply your filter criteria, and view results.

### 7. LinkedIn Job Search Engine
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A comprehensive job board listing opportunities across industries. Users can filter listings by remote status, experience level, salary range, and application type.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Career Board Search. Click the "Jobs" icon in the navigation bar, enter keywords and locations, click search, and use the filter pills at the top to refine results.

### 8. LinkedIn Skill Assessment Directory
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A directory of standardized tests that allow users to verify their technical and professional skills. Passing an assessment adds a verification badge to the user's profile.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Skill Verification Portal. Open your profile page, scroll down to the "Skills" section, click "Demonstrate skills," select "Take skill assessment," and complete the test.

### 9. LinkedIn Hashtag Following
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Allows users to follow specific hashtag topic channels to customize their feed. Following hashtags ensures that relevant professional discussions appear on the user's homepage.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Topic Feed Customizer. Search for a hashtag (e.g., `#tech`) in the search bar, select it from the results, and click the blue "Follow" button.

### 10. LinkedIn Saved Items
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A bookmarking system where users can store saved jobs, articles, and posts. Saved items are grouped in a private portal for easy reference.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Private Bookmark Hub. Click the bookmark ribbon or three-dot menu on any job post or article, select "Save," and access them via the "My Items" section in the left sidebar.

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. LinkedIn Connections System
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Defines professional relationships using three degrees of connection. This helps users map their professional network and understand how they are connected to other members.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Professional Network Mapper. Visit a member's profile page and click the "Connect" button to invite them to your network, or click "Follow" to view their public posts.

### 12. LinkedIn Recommendations and Endorsements
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Features that allow connections to validate a user's skills and write reviews of their professional experience. These testimonials are displayed publicly on the user's profile.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Peer Reference Panel. Scroll to the "Recommendations" section on a colleague's profile, click "Recommend [Name]," write your review, and submit it for their approval.

### 13. LinkedIn Groups Directory
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Niche discussion forums where professionals can share ideas, ask questions, and network. Groups are organized around specific industries, interests, or alumni networks.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Professional Forums. Click the "Groups" link under the "Work" menu in the navigation bar, search for your industry topic, and click "Request to join."

### 14. LinkedIn InMail Messaging
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: A premium messaging feature that allows users to send messages directly to people they are not connected with. This is widely used by recruiters and sales professionals for outreach.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Out-of-Network Messages. Open the profile of a member outside your network, click the "InMail" button on their card, write your message, and click send.

### 15. LinkedIn Events & Live Audio
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Virtual hubs that host live audio panel discussions or video broadcasts. Users can RSVP to events, invite connections, and participate in live chat during the broadcast.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Virtual Event Builder. Click the "+" button next to "Events" in the left sidebar of your home feed, enter the event details, choose the format, and click "Save."

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. LinkedIn Message Inboxes
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: A divided messaging panel that separates conversation threads into Focused and Other tabs. This keeps important professional chats organized while filtering out marketing pitches and spam.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Messaging Tab Separator. Click the "Messaging" icon in the navigation bar, and select either the "Focused" or "Other" tab to view organized conversations.

### 17. LinkedIn Smart Replies
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: AI-generated quick reply options displayed at the bottom of the chat interface. These replies are customized based on the context of the incoming message to help users respond quickly.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Automated Response Panel. Open an incoming message thread, select one of the suggested text bubbles at the bottom, edit the text if desired, and click send.

### 18. LinkedIn Direct Video Meetings
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Enables users to start video calls directly from messaging threads. The system integrates with Microsoft Teams, Zoom, or LinkedIn's native video conferencing tool.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: In-Chat Video Call. Open a message thread, click the video camera icon in the top right, select your conferencing provider, and send the meeting link.

### 19. LinkedIn Attachment Sharing
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows users to send resumes, PDFs, photos, and spreadsheets directly within direct messages. This streamlines the application and networking process.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: In-Chat Document Exchange. Inside a message thread, click the paperclip or paper icon next to the text entry box, select the file from your device, and click send.

### 20. LinkedIn Chat Emojis and GIPHY Integration
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Integrates emojis and animated GIFs into messaging threads to make conversations more engaging. Users can quickly search and add visual elements to express ideas.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Visual Chat Attachments. In any LinkedIn chat window, select the smiley face icon or the GIF button next to the input area, search for your item in the panel, and hit send.

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. LinkedIn Premium Subscriptions
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Paid subscription tiers offering expanded profile views, additional search filters, and InMail credits. Tiers include Premium Career, Premium Business, and Sales Navigator.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Paid Membership Manager. Click your profile icon, select "Premium Subscription Settings," choose your subscription level, and complete the payment information.

### 22. LinkedIn Ads Manager Campaign Portal
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: An advertising dashboard where businesses set budgets and create sponsored content, message ads, or dynamic text ads. Campaigns can be configured to target specific professional cohorts based on company size, job seniority, and industry fields.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: B2B Advertising Portal. Navigate to `linkedin.com/campaignmanager`, link your company page, create an ad account, set up your daily budgets, and select target audiences.

### 23. LinkedIn Talent Solutions
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: An enterprise recruiter platform that allows companies to search, track, and contact potential candidates. It provides advanced search filters and candidate tracking tools.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Recruiter Console. Access the Talent Solutions dashboard at `talent.linkedin.com`, log in with your recruiter credentials, and set up your candidate searches.

### 24. LinkedIn Learning Subscriptions
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A subscription-based learning portal offering thousands of professional video courses. Users can complete courses to earn certificates that display on their profiles.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Educational Video Library. Access LinkedIn Learning via the "Work" menu in the navigation bar, search for courses, subscribe, and complete video tutorials.

### 25. LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A lead generation platform offering advanced lead search filters, lead recommendations, and CRM integrations. It helps sales professionals identify and contact key decision makers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Sales Leads Manager. Open Sales Navigator from the top navigation bar, set your lead search filters, save target accounts, and track updates.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. LinkedIn Profile Analytics Dashboard
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: An analytics dashboard showing weekly profile views, search appearances, and post impressions. This dashboard helps creators monitor their personal reach and engagement.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Reach Dashboard. Navigate to your main profile page, click on the "Analytics" or "Dashboard" section below your intro card, and view your performance metrics.

### 27. LinkedIn Creator Mode
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A profile setting that highlights your content and lists your followed topics on your profile card. It changes the default action on your profile from "Connect" to "Follow."
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Content Creator Layout. Open your profile page, click the "Creator Mode" option under the "Resources" section, select your topic hashtags, and toggle the switch to "On."

### 28. LinkedIn Company Page Analytics
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A metrics dashboard for business page admins that tracks follower demographics, visitor trends, and update engagement. This dashboard helps companies measure the effectiveness of their brand updates.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Corporate Page Tracker. Navigate to your Organization Page admin center, click the Analytics dropdown, and select visitors, followers, or posts to view detailed graphs.

### 29. LinkedIn Ad Campaign Reporting
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: An advertising metrics dashboard tracking conversion rates, return on ad spend, and demographic data. This dashboard helps advertisers optimize campaigns and measure audience responses.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Ads Dashboard. Log in to Campaign Manager, select your account, and navigate to the "Performance" or "Demographics" reporting tabs to inspect campaign data.

### 30. LinkedIn Employee Advocacy Tools
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A management tool that allows companies to curate content for employees to share on their personal feeds. This helps companies extend their reach through their employees' networks.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Curated Content Portal. Open your company page admin menu, click "Employee Advocacy," add content suggestions to the portal, and invite employees to share them.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. LinkedIn Private Profile Viewing Mode
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: A privacy setting that hides your identity when visiting other member profiles. When active, the profile owner only sees that an anonymous member viewed their profile.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Anonymous Viewing Toggle. Go to Settings & Privacy > Visibility > Profile viewing options, select "Private mode," and click save to activate.

### 32. LinkedIn Connection Visibility Controls
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Controls whether your connections can see your list of connections. Restricting this setting prevents other members from browsing your professional network.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Network Privacy Settings. Go to Settings & Privacy > Visibility > Connections, and toggle the switch next to "Who can see your connections" to your preference.

### 33. LinkedIn Data Sharing Preferences
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Allows users to manage how LinkedIn shares profile data with third-party partners. Users can opt out of data sharing for research, advertising, and job match systems.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Third-Party Data Controls. Open Settings & Privacy > Data privacy > Data sharing and personalization, and toggle off the data sharing switches.

### 34. LinkedIn Blocking & Reporting
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Allows users to block accounts and report profiles for violating community guidelines. Blocking prevents the user from viewing your profile, sending messages, or connecting.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Restrictions. Click the "More" button on a member's profile page, select "Report / Block" from the dropdown menu, select your reason, and confirm.

### 35. LinkedIn Active Session Auditing
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: A security dashboard showing all devices, browsers, and locations currently logged into the account. Users can audit login history and close active sessions remotely.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Session Security Manager. Go to Settings & Privacy > Sign-in and security > Where you're signed in, review the list of active sessions, and click "End session" to log them out.

## Category 8: Notifications & Time Management

### 36. LinkedIn Notification Settings Hub
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: A central hub where users can manage alerts for job openings, connections, messaging, and network updates. Users can customize alerts for push notifications, emails, and in-app notifications.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Notification Manager. Go to Settings & Privacy > Notifications, select the category you want to modify, and toggle the switches to adjust frequency.

### 37. LinkedIn Job Alert Frequency
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Allows users to customize the frequency of email and push alerts for saved job search criteria. Users can set alerts to arrive daily or weekly.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Career Alert Settings. Click the "Jobs" icon in the navigation bar, click "Job Alerts," select the alert you want to edit, and adjust the frequency dropdown.

### 38. LinkedIn Mute Conversation
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Mutes notifications for individual message threads without deleting the conversation. This prevents push and email alerts from interrupting your work.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Inbox Conversation Muter. Tap the three dots next to the search bar inside any active chat session, and select 'Mute conversation' to silence future notifications.

### 39. LinkedIn Daily Digest Emails
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Bundles network updates and news alerts into a single daily email digest. This reduces the number of individual email notifications sent to your inbox.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Email Digest Toggle. Open Settings & Privacy > Notifications, select "Email Frequency," and toggle on the "Daily Digest" option.

### 40. LinkedIn Feed Preference Customization
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Toggles feed sorting between Top (algorithmic recommendations) and Recent (chronological updates). This allows users to control how content is organized on their homepage.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Feed Sort Toggle. Click the dropdown arrow next to "Sort by" under the post composer on your desktop homepage, and select "Top" or "Recent."

## Category 9: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 41. LinkedIn Share API
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A REST-based API that enables developers to automate sharing updates, links, and media to a member's professional feed. This interface is widely integrated with content management systems and corporate marketing tools.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: B2B Share API. Register a developer project in the LinkedIn Developer Portal, request the Share on LinkedIn permission, and send JSON POST requests to the `/v2/ugcPosts` endpoint.

### 42. LinkedIn Sign In API
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: An identity integration API based on the OpenID Connect protocol. It allows third-party developers to implement "Sign in with LinkedIn" buttons on their websites to retrieve profile details securely.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: OAuth Login Integration. Configure OAuth redirect URLs in the Developer Portal, and integrate the Sign In API endpoints (`https://www.linkedin.com/oauth/v2/authorization`) into your login flow.

### 43. LinkedIn Marketing Developer Platform API
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A programmatic interface allowing developers to manage ad accounts, campaigns, budgets, and retrieve lead generation data. This API supports integration with enterprise marketing automation systems.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Marketing Automation Endpoint. Request access to the Marketing Developer Platform in the Developer Portal, obtain tokens, and make HTTP requests to the campaign endpoints.

### 44. LinkedIn Learning API
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Allows enterprise organizations to integrate LinkedIn Learning progress, user records, and course catalogs with external learning management systems (LMS). It supports standard LMS protocols like xAPI.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: LMS Integration Interface. Access the LinkedIn Learning admin settings, generate API credentials (client ID and secret), and configure the integration URL in your LMS dashboard.

### 45. LinkedIn Webhooks API
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A real-time HTTP callback system that sends instant notifications to corporate servers when page actions occur. This allows organizations to monitor comments, track company page reactions, and capture lead gen form entries.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: B2B Real-time Webhooks. Register your callback endpoint in the LinkedIn Developer Portal under the Webhooks tab, complete the verification handshake, and subscribe to page events.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. LinkedIn Two-Step Verification
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Provides security for professional accounts by requiring an additional security code at login from unrecognized browsers or mobile devices. This helps prevent unauthorized access to user profile details and business pages.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Credential Security Controls. Go to Settings & Privacy > Sign-in and security, select Two-step verification, click 'Turn on', and choose your preferred verification mode.

### 47. LinkedIn Primary Email Management
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows users to manage the email addresses linked to their account. Users can add backup emails, confirm new addresses, and change their primary contact address for account recovery.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Account Email Settings. Go to Settings & Privacy > Sign-in and security > Email addresses, click "Add email address," confirm it via email link, and click "Make primary."

### 48. LinkedIn Data Archive Download
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Compiles a comprehensive download of a user's professional archive, including connections list, message history, profile page details, and search history. This exports all interactions in structured CSV and HTML formats for personal backup.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Professional Archive Downloader. Open Settings & Privacy, select Data privacy, click on 'Get a copy of your data', choose the specific data buckets, and request the archive link.

### 49. LinkedIn Account Merging Portal
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: A portal that allows users to merge two duplicate LinkedIn accounts. This combines connections and deletes the duplicate account, preserving network integrity.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Merger tool. Go to Settings & Privacy > Account preferences > Account management > Merge accounts, enter the login credentials of the duplicate account, and confirm.

### 50. LinkedIn Account Deactivation
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Initiates a request to temporarily hibernate or permanently delete a LinkedIn account. Permanent deletion removes the profile, connections, and all postings from the platform.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Account Closure Portal. Go to Settings & Privacy > Account preferences > Account management > Close account, select your reason for leaving, enter your password, and confirm the deletion.
