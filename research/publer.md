# Publer Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for Publer, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. Multi-Platform Post Composer

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows users to draft, format, and customize a single post layout to be shared simultaneously across multiple connected social media networks.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Omnichannel Composer. Open the post composition window, select the target social channels (e.g., Facebook, LinkedIn, X), type your copy, and click the network tabs to customize details for each.

### 2. AI Assist Content Generator

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Integrates with artificial intelligence to automatically generate post captions, rewrite copy, or recommend hashtags based on user prompts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: AI Text Generator. Click the AI Assist button within the composer, input a prompt (e.g., "Write a marketing pitch for new shoes"), choose tone options, and insert the generated text.

### 3. Built-in Image & Video Editor (Canva & VistaCreate Integration)

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Integrates Canva and VistaCreate within the Publer editor, allowing users to design and modify visual media without leaving the app.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Visual Media Editor. Tap the image upload icon in the post composer, click "Design on Canva" or "Design on VistaCreate," create or edit graphics in the overlay browser, and save the file directly to the post draft.

### 4. Auto-Scheduling & Queue System

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Automatically queues posts into the next available preset time slot for each social media account, ensuring consistent publishing.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Scheduled Queues. Go to Posting Schedule to set default weekly timeslots. When drafting a post, click "Auto-Schedule" to assign the post to the next empty time slot.

### 5. Bulk Media Uploader & Scheduler

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows uploading up to 500 images, videos, or GIFs at once, automatically creating separate scheduled posts for each file.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Bulk Scheduling. Click the bulk scheduler tab, drag and drop files from local storage, write a default caption template, set the interval between posts (e.g., "1 day apart"), and click schedule.

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. Visual Calendar Planner

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Displays all scheduled, draft, and published posts in an interactive calendar format supporting drag-and-drop rescheduling.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Interactive Calendars. Click the "Calendar" tab, view posts by month, week, or day, and drag individual post cards to different dates or times to reschedule.

### 7. Social Media Feed Search (Inbox)

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Searches through brand mentions, comments, and direct message contents across connected social channels in a single search console.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Search Filters. Open the Unified Inbox, type keywords into the search bar, and filter by platform, message type, or read status.

### 8. RSS Feed Automation Parser

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Automatically imports content from external blog RSS feeds and schedules updates whenever a new article is published.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: RSS Content Sync. Go to RSS Feeds, click "Add RSS feed," paste the XML link, set your post templates with title and link tags, and save to configure auto-posting.

### 9. Content Library Search & Tag Filters

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Allows users to save media assets and templates in a library, using keyword searches and tag filters to locate them.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Asset Repositories. Open the Media Library tab, click "Tags" to filter resources by labels (e.g., `#summercampaign`), or search filenames using the search bar.

### 10. Hashtag Generator & Suggestion Engine

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Recommends popular or trending hashtags based on the caption text entered in the post composer.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Keyword Tagging. Type a post caption, click the hashtag wand icon in the footer toolbar, and select from the suggested tags list to append them to your post.

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. Unified Social Inbox

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Consolidates comments, direct messages, and brand reviews from multiple social channels into a single inbox, allowing users to reply directly.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Community Management. Navigate to the Inbox tab, select an unread customer comment, write a response in the text pane, and click "Send reply."

### 12. Comment Auto-Responder (Auto-Comment on Post)

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows scheduling a follow-up comment to post automatically alongside the main post, useful for sharing links.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Auto-Comments. In the post composer, expand the "Follow-up comment" section, input text or links, and the system will publish this comment as soon as the main post goes live.

### 13. Auto-Follow / Auto-Like Rules

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Automatically engages with target profiles or hashtags based on user-defined interaction rules to grow accounts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Growth Automation. Navigate to Automation Settings, write rules (e.g., "Like posts containing tag #webdev"), set daily action limits, and save.

### 14. Customer Sentiment Tagging

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Uses natural language processing to tag incoming inbox messages as positive, negative, or neutral.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Customer Sentiment Analysis. Within the Inbox, view the automated colored sentiment tag next to messages, and sort queries by sentiment to prioritize critical feedback.

### 15. Link in Bio (Linkin.bio Page Creator)

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: A customizable landing page generator that links social posts to specific product or webpage URLs.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Landing Page Management. Open the Link in Bio tab, select your theme layout, add buttons and custom links, and copy the generated link to place in your Instagram or TikTok profile bios.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. Direct Message Inbox Manager

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Receives, tracks, and manages private direct messages from platforms like Facebook Pages and Instagram Business profiles.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Inbox Direct Messaging. Navigate to the Inbox tab, filter by "Direct Messages," select a conversation thread, write your reply, and send.

### 17. Canned Responses Shortcut

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Stores text templates for frequently asked questions, allowing users to insert them into chat windows with shortcuts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Quick Replies. Go to Inbox Settings > Canned Responses, save templates with keyword shortcut triggers, and click the template icon in chats to paste.

### 18. Message Assigner / Internal Delegation

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Assigns customer direct messages or comment threads to specific team members in the workspace.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Teammate Assignment. In the Unified Inbox, select a message thread, click the "Assign to" dropdown in the top header, select a teammate, and write internal notes.

### 19. Real-Time DM Alerts

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Sends desktop browser notifications or in-app pings as soon as a new direct message is received on any connected channel.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Instant Desktop Notifications. Go to Settings > Notifications, and toggle on "Browser alerts for incoming DMs" to enable real-time message popups.

### 20. Internal Team Chat Notes

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows team members to leave private notes and comments inside customer message threads, invisible to the customer.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Collaborative Notes. Under a client's message bubble in the Inbox, toggle "Internal Note," type your comment, and click save to display it to colleagues.

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. Shopify & WooCommerce Product Linker

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Connects with online stores to tag products directly in social posts, facilitating social selling.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Product Catalog Sync. Go to Settings > Integrations, link your Shopify or WooCommerce store, and click the product tag icon in the post composer to select items from your catalog.

### 22. Publer Affiliate Program Partner

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Provides users with referral links and displays a metrics dashboard tracking sign-ups, clicks, and affiliate payouts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Referral Programs. Access the Affiliate Program page from the dashboard sidebar, copy your unique referral URL, and check the payout transaction log.

### 23. Custom Brand Watermarking

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Automatically overlays a logo file as a watermark on scheduled image or video posts to protect intellectual property.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Automated Watermarks. Go to Settings > Watermarks, upload your PNG logo, set its opacity, size, and corner placement, and save to auto-apply it.

### 24. Client Billing Management

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Provides agency owners with tools to configure workspace plans and invoice clients for managing their social accounts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Client Portals. Access Workspace Settings > Client Invoicing, configure pricing plans, add credit cards, and manage client bills.

### 25. Paid Ad Boost Scheduler

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Schedules Facebook and Instagram ad boosting budgets directly from the scheduling dashboard.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Social Ad Boosting. When scheduling a post, check "Boost Post," select your Meta Ad Account, set budget limits, input targeting details, and save.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. Comprehensive Performance Analytics Dashboard

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Displays charts tracking reach, link clicks, impressions, and engagement metrics over custom date ranges.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Data Analytics. Open the Analytics tab, select the date range from the date picker, choose the social network tab, and view performance charts.

### 27. Automated PDF Report Generator

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Generates and emails PDF reports of social media performance to client or team email addresses on weekly or monthly schedules.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Scheduled Reporting. In the Analytics tab, click "Export," select "PDF Report," configure the scheduled send frequency (e.g., "Monthly"), enter recipient email addresses, and save.

### 28. Best Time to Post Analytics

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Analyzes historical audience engagement to calculate and recommend the most effective hours to post on each platform.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Time Heatmaps. Go to Analytics > Best Times, review the weekly heatmap showing high-engagement slots, and click "Apply to Schedule" to update your queue settings.

### 29. Individual Post Performance Tracker

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Tracks detailed engagement metrics for specific published posts, listing likes, comments, retweets, and clicks.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Post Auditing. Go to Posts > History, click the "Analytics" button under any published post card, and view the metrics popup.

### 30. Competitor Performance Tracker

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Benchmarks engagement levels and posting frequencies of competitor accounts alongside your own.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Competitor Benchmarking. In the Analytics tab, click "Competitors," click "Add Competitor Page," input the public profile URL, and compare metrics.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. Workspace Access Roles & Permissions

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Restricts workspace tool and social account access by assigning roles (Owner, Admin, Editor, Client) with custom permissions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Workspace Security. Go to Workspace Settings > Members, click "Invite Member," enter their email, select their role, and configure permissions.

### 32. Post Approval Workflow

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Requires posts created by junior members to be reviewed and approved by managers before they are scheduled.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Editorial Gatekeeping. Set a team member's role to "Requires Approval." When they draft a post and click "Submit," it is placed in the "Pending Approval" queue.

### 33. Secure API Key Encryption

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Encrypts and stores connected social platform OAuth tokens securely, hiding raw account passwords from the interface.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Token Security. When adding social accounts, Publer uses the official API OAuth authentication screens. The resulting access tokens are encrypted using security protocols.

### 34. Auditing Logs (Activity History)

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Tracks and logs all actions performed in a workspace, noting who created, modified, approved, or deleted posts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Activity History. Navigate to Settings > Activity Log, and browse the chronological list of workspace events filterable by user and date.

### 35. Client Access Sandbox Mode

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Provides clients with a viewing dashboard where they can approve or request changes to posts without editing capabilities.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Client Portals. Invite a client using the "Client" or "Viewer" role settings, allowing them to click "Approve" or leave comments on draft posts.

## Category 8: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 36. Publer REST API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A developer API providing RESTful endpoints to manage workspaces, upload media, schedule posts, and fetch analytics programmatically.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Developer API endpoints. Access the developer console, generate a client access token, and make authenticated HTTPS requests to `/v1/posts` endpoints.

### 37. Browser Extension Scheduler

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A browser extension for Chrome, Firefox, and Safari that allows scheduling text, links, or images from any webpage.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Web Extensions. Install the Publer extension, hover over any web image, click the Publer icon, and schedule it in the pop-up window.

### 38. Bitly & Custom URL Shorteners Integration

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Integrates link shortening tools to automatically compress post links and track click-through numbers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: URL Shortening. Go to Settings > Link Shortener, select your preferred shortener (e.g., Bitly, Rebrandly), enter your API key to authenticate, and toggle auto-shorten.

### 39. Google Drive / Dropbox Import Integration

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Connects cloud storage accounts, allowing users to import photos, videos, and media directly into the Publer library.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Cloud Media Import. Inside the composer or media library, click "Import from Cloud," select Google Drive or Dropbox, log in to authorize, and select files.

### 40. Mastodon & Fediverse API Integration

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Integrates with decentralized Fediverse platform APIs to schedule posts to Mastodon servers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Fediverse Integration. Click "Add Account," select Mastodon, enter your server instance URL, log in to authorize the app, and schedule posts.

## Category 9: Notifications & Time Management

### 41. Failed Post Email & Push Alerts

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Sends immediate email or push alerts to workspace owners when a scheduled post fails to publish.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Error Alerts. The system monitors API publishing statuses and sends alerts (e.g., "Post failed on Facebook due to expired token") with troubleshooting links.

### 42. Weekly Workspace Digest

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Compiles weekly summaries of scheduled posts, upcoming events, inbox metrics, and audience growth into an email.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Notification Summaries. Go to Account Settings > Notifications, and toggle on "Weekly workspace summary email."

### 43. Slack/Discord Channel Alerts

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Sends post status updates and approval requests directly to team Slack or Discord channels.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chat Integrations. Connect your Slack or Discord workspace via the Integrations menu, and configure notifications for events like "Post approved" or "Post failed."

### 44. Snooze Approval Reminders

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Temporarily mutes pending post approval push notifications and emails.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Reminder Snoozing. Go to Notification Settings, select "Snooze approval alerts," choose a snooze duration, and confirm to mute pings.

### 45. Holiday Calendar Notifications

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Integrates a holiday calendar displaying international dates alongside ready-made content templates.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Event Planners. Toggle "Show Holiday Calendar" in the main scheduling calendar view, click on highlighted holiday squares, and click "Use Template" to create a themed post.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. Single Sign-On (SSO) with Google/Facebook

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Integrates federated authentication platforms to allow secure, one-click access using Google or Facebook credentials.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Federated Identity login. On the login screen, click "Log in with Google" or "Log in with Facebook" and complete authorization.

### 47. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA/2FA)

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Secures login by requiring an authentication code from an authenticator app alongside the password.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: MFA Setup. Go to Account Security, toggle "Two-Factor Authentication" to active, scan the QR code with your authenticator app, and enter the verification code.

### 48. Account Deletion & Data Purge

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Self-service portal that permanently deletes the user account, workspace data, and all connected social tokens.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Account Wiping. Go to Settings > Account settings, scroll to the bottom, click "Delete Account," type your password, and click the confirmation button.

### 49. Workspace Migration Tool

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows transferring social channels and historical post records from one workspace to another.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Channel Migration. Navigate to Workspace Settings > Workspaces, select the social accounts to move, click "Transfer," select the destination workspace, and save.

### 50. Invoice billing history & Download

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Provides access to view and download PDF invoices for all past subscription transactions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Billing Records. Go to Settings > Billing, scroll down to the "Billing History" table, and click "Download PDF" next to any invoice row.
