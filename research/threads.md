# Threads Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for Threads, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. Threads Voice Posts

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows users to record voice clips to share as threads, which are automatically transcribed into readable text overlays.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Post Composition. Tap the microphone icon in the thread composer on mobile, hold or tap to record your message, and review the automatic text transcription before sharing.

### 2. Threads Character Expansion

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Provides a generous text limit of 500 characters per individual post, giving users more room for detailed thoughts compared to other microblogging platforms.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Text Post Editor. Open the post composer, type your message, and monitor the circular progress bar in the bottom right corner to track character usage.

### 3. Threads Interactive Polls

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Adds user-facing interactive question polls with up to four customizable options that remain active for 24 hours.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Interactive Poll Creator. Tap the graph icon in the thread composer, type your question, define the choices, and tap post to start gathering responses from followers.

### 4. Threads Photo Grid Layout

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows creators to organize up to 10 photos or videos in a single post using pre-built visual carousel or grid arrangements.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Carousel Media Layouts. Select multiple images from the device gallery inside the post composer, then swipe to choose between standard slideshow, collage, or grid layouts.

### 5. Threads Video Captioning

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Automatically processes and overlays closed captions onto uploaded video files to ensure accessibility and quiet viewing.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Automatic Video Captions. Upload a video in the composer, click the three-dot menu on the video thumbnail, select "Add Captions," and edit the generated text before publishing.

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. Threads Following Feed

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Displays a chronological stream composed strictly of posts from profiles the user actively follows, bypassing the main algorithmic recommendations.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chronological Feed Navigation. Tap the Threads home icon or logo at the top of the mobile screen, and select the "Following" tab to switch views.

### 7. Threads Tagged Topic Search

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Filters search results and clusters public discussions using hyperlinked topic tags, replacing traditional hashtags with inline keywords.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Tagged Topic Discovery. Highlight text or tap the "#" icon in the composer to select a tag topic, or click on a blue topic tag in any feed to open its dedicated search stream.

### 8. Threads For You Feed

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: An algorithmically driven timeline that curates posts, replies, and trends based on your interactions, followed accounts, and overall network engagement.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Algorithmic Recommendations. Access the main home view on the app. By default, the application loads the "For You" feed, which updates dynamically when pulled down.

### 9. Threads Keyword Search

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Searches the entire platform's database for public posts containing specific words or phrases rather than just searching for user account handles.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Content Search. Tap the magnifying glass icon in the navigation bar, type your query into the search field, and toggle the results view between "Profiles" and "Search."

### 10. Threads Hidden Words Feed Filter

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Removes specific posts, threads, and search results containing designated keywords from appearing on the user's discovery feeds.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Feed Keyword Filtering. Access Settings > Privacy > Hidden Words. Toggle "Custom words and phrases" on, and enter the terms you wish to filter out of your views.

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. Threads Repost & Quote

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Shares public posts directly with your followers as a clean repost, or with attached commentary in a quote post format.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Content Amplification. Tap the double-arrow repost icon beneath any thread, then select either "Repost" to share it instantly or "Quote" to open a composer with the original post attached.

### 12. Threads Mention Control

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Manages permission levels for who can tag or mention your profile name in their public threads and replies.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Mention Security. Navigate to Settings > Privacy > Mentions, and select between "Everyone," "People you follow," or "No one."

### 13. Threads Profile Verification Badge

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Imports and displays the blue Meta-verified badge next to your handle to confirm identity authenticity, synced directly with your linked Instagram account.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Syncing Identity Verification. Automatic upon logging in. Manage your subscription and check verification status under Settings > Account > Meta Verified.

### 14. Threads Share to Instagram Stories

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Cross-posts a thread directly onto your Instagram Stories feed, rendering the post as an interactive custom sticker.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Cross-platform Sharing. Tap the paper airplane icon underneath a thread, select "Add to story" from the share options, and edit the sticker in Instagram before posting.

### 15. Threads Follower Syncing

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Replicates your Instagram social network by automatically following or queueing follow requests for the same users when they join Threads.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Social Graph Import. Access Settings > Follow and invite friends. Tap "Follow accounts from Instagram" to browse and follow your existing contacts in bulk.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. Threads Message Button Integration

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Integrates a direct message option on Threads user profiles, routing messages through Meta's Instagram messaging backend.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Direct Contact Initiation. Open any user's profile on Threads and tap the "Message" button at the top. This opens the Instagram app directly into a secure DM chat thread with that user.

### 17. Threads Share to Direct Message

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Shares a specific post directly to a contact's Instagram Direct Message inbox from the Threads user interface.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Message Inbox Sharing. Tap the paper airplane share icon under any thread, select "Send via Instagram," type the recipient's name, and tap "Send" to forward the post.

### 18. Threads Group Messaging Link

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Generates secure invite or content sharing links for specific threads to be pasted directly into group chats on Instagram.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Direct Share Generation. Tap the paper airplane icon under a thread, choose "Copy Link" from the menu, and paste the clipboard URL into any group message thread.

### 19. Threads Contact Quick Share

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Accelerates sharing by listing your top-interacted Instagram direct message contacts directly within the Threads sharing sheet.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Direct Message Quick Send. Long-press the paper airplane icon under any thread to bring up a rapid-sharing tray of your top 5 message contacts, then tap a profile picture to send.

### 20. Threads Thread-to-DM Forwarding

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Forwards a copy of an active public thread directly into a private text conversation thread on Instagram.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Direct Message Forwarding. Tap the paper airplane icon under the thread, select "Send," choose from the list of recent Instagram DM threads, and add an optional message.

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. Threads Branded Content Tag

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows creators to label commercial partnerships in posts, appending an official "Paid partnership" notification tag to the thread header.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Sponsored Post Compliance. When composing a thread, click the three dots, select "Add Paid Partnership Tag," and search for the brand partner's account to link them.

### 22. Threads Meta Verified Subscription

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A monthly paid subscription that grants creators search prominence, enhanced support access, and an identity badge.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Paid Account Verification. Open Settings > Account, tap "Meta Verified," and follow the prompts to complete payment and identity verification.

### 23. Threads Shopping Link Referrals

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Formats e-commerce URLs inside posts to render interactive, rich preview cards that direct users to checkout.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Product Referrals. Paste a valid shopping link (e.g. from an Instagram Shop or external merchant) directly into the composer. The app automatically fetches the product image, title, and link card.

### 24. Threads Creator Marketplace

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Connects brand partners with Threads creators for sponsored campaigns, managed through the central Meta Business portal.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Sponsored Deals Integration. Creators configure their portfolio and collaboration settings in the Meta Creator Portfolio dashboard and sync it with their Threads account.

### 25. Threads Affiliate Link Previews

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Generates metadata-rich product cards when affiliate links are posted, maximizing click-through rates and commission tracking.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Affiliate Marketing. Paste your tracking link in the thread composer. The system checks the destination URL, pulls the product cover photo and price, and renders it as an e-commerce card.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. Threads Account Insights Dashboard

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Provides creators and businesses with a web-based dashboard showing follower counts, impressions, and post engagement rates.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Web Creator Insights. Log into Threads via a desktop browser, click the graph icon in the navigation bar, and view metrics showing post performance over the last 7, 14, or 30 days.

### 27. Threads Post View Count

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Displays a public indicator of total views and impressions on individual threads, helping creators evaluate reach.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Post Reach Auditing. Look at the bottom of any thread post. Next to the repost and reply counts, the view count icon shows the total number of times the post has appeared on screens.

### 28. Threads Follower Demographics

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Breaks down follower distribution data by age group, gender, country, and city to optimize content strategy.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audience Demographics. Open the Threads web dashboard, navigate to "Insights," and click on the "Audience" tab to view breakdown charts.

### 29. Threads Web Editor & Scheduler

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Allows accounts to schedule posts in advance from the desktop web interface to optimize posting schedules.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Scheduled Posting. Log into `threads.net` on desktop, write your post in the composer, click the clock icon, set the date and time, and click "Schedule."

### 30. Threads Profile Analytics Summary

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A mobile summary showing net follower growth, profile visits, and likes over the last 30 days.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Quick Mobile Analytics. Go to your Threads profile, tap the "Insights" button beneath your bio, and swipe through the cards representing views, replies, and net followers.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. Threads Hidden Words List

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Filters out offensive replies containing user-specified words, phrases, or emojis from threads.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Content Blocklists. Navigate to Settings > Privacy > Hidden Words, and add your custom phrases to the blocklist.

### 32. Threads Profile Muting

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Mutes posts and replies from specific accounts without unfollowing or blocking them, hiding their content silently.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Account Muting. Tap the three dots in the top right corner of any post or profile page, and select "Mute" from the dropdown menu.

### 33. Threads Thread Reply Controls

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Restricts who can reply to your thread, letting you limit discussion to specific circles or accounts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Conversation Controls. In the thread composer, tap the "Anyone can reply" text in the bottom left, and choose between "Anyone," "Profiles you follow," or "Only mentioned profiles."

### 34. Threads Sensitive Content Control

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Adjusts the volume of sensitive or mature content recommended to you in search and feed screens.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Content Moderation. Access Settings > Privacy > Sensitive Content, and select either "Standard," "More," or "Less" to adjust the recommendation engine.

### 35. Threads Blocked Accounts List

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Syncs blocklists with Instagram to prevent designated accounts from viewing or finding your profile.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Account Blocklist. Navigate to Settings > Privacy > Blocked. This displays accounts blocked on both Threads and Instagram. Tap "Unblock" to restore access.

## Category 8: Notifications & Time Management

### 36. Threads Notification Pause

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Temporarily halts all push notification alerts from Threads for a designated duration.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Focus Mode Muting. Navigate to Settings > Notifications, toggle "Pause all" on, and select a duration: 15 minutes, 1 hour, 2 hours, 4 hours, or 8 hours.

### 37. Threads Take a Break Reminder

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Prompts a full-screen screen time warning when the user reaches a designated limit of continuous app scrolling.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Usage Reminder. Go to Settings > Account > Take a break. Select a frequency of 10, 20, or 30 minutes, or turn the reminder off.

### 38. Threads Custom Push Categories

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Allows granular configuration of push alerts for individual interaction categories such as likes, replies, and mentions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Alert Filtering. Go to Settings > Notifications > Threads and replies. Customize alerts to "Off," "From people I follow," or "From everyone" for each event.

### 39. Threads Thread Notification Subscriptions

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Subscribes users to receive real-time push notifications for all subsequent replies to a selected post.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Thread Subscriptions. Tap the three dots in the top right of any thread you are reading, and select "Turn on notifications" to receive updates for replies.

### 40. Threads Quiet Mode Syncing

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Automatically aligns Threads notification silencing schedules with the Quiet Mode settings configured in Instagram.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Synced Silent Periods. Access Settings > Notifications > Quiet Mode. Toggle the sync switch to inherit quiet periods from Instagram's profile scheduler.

## Category 9: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 41. Threads Graph API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Programmatic HTTP interface enabling developers to publish text and media, retrieve replies, and manage threads.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: RESTful API Endpoint. Configured under Meta Developer Portal. Integrates OAuth access tokens to call HTTP endpoints (e.g., POST `/v1.0/me/threads`).

### 42. Threads API Webhooks

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Sends real-time HTTPS callback payloads to developer servers when mentions, replies, or changes occur on an authorized account.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Real-time Webhook Alerts. Set up on the Meta Developer Dashboard. Developers register a secure HTTPS endpoint and subscribe to specific feed events (e.g., `threads_replies`).

### 43. Threads OAuth 2.0 Authentication

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Implements secure user-delegated authorization protocol allowing third-party apps to access Threads content.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Federated Authorization. Developers configure OAuth redirect URIs in their app dashboard, directing users to authorize their client and return code tokens.

### 44. Threads API Insights Endpoint

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Programmatic endpoint that allows developers to fetch account-level and post-level metrics for third-party analytics integrations.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Analytics API Query. Make HTTP GET requests to `/v1.0/me/threads_insights` with an access token, passing parameters to filter by metrics like impressions, reach, and follower count.

### 45. Threads Media Publishing Endpoint

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Specialized API resource supporting programmatically scheduled uploads of image and video media files to Threads.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Media Upload Pipeline. Make a POST request to `/v1.0/me/threads` with `media_type` set to `IMAGE` or `VIDEO` and a URL parameter referencing the hosted media.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. Threads Profile Deactivation

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Temporarily hides your Threads profile, threads, replies, and likes without deleting your linked Instagram account.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Temporary Account Deactivation. Navigate to Settings > Account > Deactivate or delete profile, and select "Deactivate profile."

### 47. Threads Account Center Integration

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Centralizes security, logins, and authentication settings via the Meta Account Center dashboard.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Unified Security Hub. Navigate to Settings > Account > Account Center. Manage password resets, active login sessions, and two-factor authentication.

### 48. Threads Account Deletion

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Permanently deletes your Threads profile data, comments, and posts from the network.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Data Purge. Navigate to Settings > Account > Deactivate or delete profile, tap "Delete profile," and enter your account password to confirm.

### 49. Threads Private Profile Toggle

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Restricts profile visibility so only approved followers can view, reply to, or share your threads.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Visibility Toggle. Navigate to Settings > Privacy > Private Profile. Switch the toggle on to require manual follower approval.

### 50. Threads Data Portability Export

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows users to download an archive containing their posts, comments, likes, and profile settings.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Data Download. Navigate to Settings > Account > Account Center > Your information and permissions > Download your information. Select "Threads" and submit the export request.
