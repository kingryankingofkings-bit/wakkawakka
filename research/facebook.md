# Facebook Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for Facebook, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. Disappearing Stories

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Shares photos or short videos that remain visible to friends or followers for exactly 24 hours before disappearing.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Transient Stories. Created by clicking "Create Story" at the top of the Feed. Supports interactive features like text overlays, music stickers, and polls.

### 2. Reels Creation & Audio Sync

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Compiles short-form, portrait-oriented videos with audio overlays, transitions, text effects, and speed controls.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Vertical Video Editor. Created via the Reels tab on mobile. Offers an "Audio Sync" tool that automatically aligns video clips to the rhythm of a selected music track.

### 3. Feeling/Activity Status Post

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Appends a descriptive mood or action emoji to a standard text post (e.g., "feeling excited" or "eating pizza").
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Status Annotations. Triggered by clicking the "Feeling/activity" option in the post composer window. The activity appears next to the poster's name.

### 4. Live Producer Streaming

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Broadcasts high-definition live video feeds to Facebook Pages or Groups using professional external cameras and encoding software.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Professional Live Stream. Accessed on desktop via `facebook.com/live/producer`. Supports stream keys, API inputs, custom graphics, and live commenting.

### 5. Post Background Styling

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Highlights short text posts by placing the text on top of a selected graphic background or gradient.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Text Post Layouts. Activated automatically in the post composer when typing a text post under 130 characters. Users click a colored grid icon to select a design.

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. Feed Preferences (Manage Favorites)

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Allows users to designate up to 30 friends and pages to prioritize their content at the top of their Feed.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Custom Home Feed. Configured in Settings > Feed Preferences > Favorites. Posts from selected favorites are marked with a star icon.

### 7. Chronological Feeds Tab

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A dedicated navigation shortcut that displays posts from friends, groups, and followed pages in a strict reverse-chronological order, bypassing the main algorithmic feed.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chronological Feed Navigation. Accessed by tapping the "Feeds" tab on the mobile app navigation bar or selecting "Feeds" in the left-hand navigation pane on desktop.

### 8. Advanced Search Filters

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Allows users to locate posts, photos, videos, people, pages, and groups on the platform using granular filter criteria such as date, location, and source.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Targeted Search. Enter a query in the search bar, then use the left-hand filter panel (desktop) or top filter pill menu (mobile) to refine results by "Posts you've seen," "Tagged location," or "Date posted."

### 9. Hashtag Topic Feeds

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Aggregates all public posts, articles, and discussion threads containing a specific hashtag into a unified, scrollable topic feed.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Topic Discovery. Click on any blue hyperlinked hashtag (e.g., `#gardening`) in a post caption to open its dedicated feed page. Users can also type `facebook.com/hashtag/TopicName` directly in a browser.

### 10. Watch Video Feed

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A dedicated video discovery tab that aggregates recommended long-form videos, live streams, and reels based on user viewing habits.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Video Discovery Portal. Accessed by clicking the video icon ("Watch") in the shortcut bar. Offers personalized categories such as "Live," "Music," "Following," and "Saved."

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. Facebook Groups & Admin Tools

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows users to create closed or public discussion groups with moderator tools, member lists, and customizable rules.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Community Moderation. Navigate to the main home screen menu, select "Groups," tap the "+" icon or "Create Group" button, fill in group settings (name, privacy level), invite members, and tap "Create."

### 12. Facebook Events Scheduler

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Organizes online or offline events with geographic coordinates, ticket links, invitation lists, and public discussion feeds.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Digital Calendar invites. Click "Create Event" in the events tab, define parameters (time, location, description), invite friends, and manage responses.

### 13. Facebook Pages for Business

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Specialized profiles for brands, public figures, and businesses to build a public following and run targeted advertising campaigns.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Brand Profiles. Navigate to the main menu or Facebook bookmarks section, select "Pages," tap "Create" or "Create New Page," enter page details (name, category, bio), and click "Create Page."

### 14. Custom Member Onboarding Questionnaires

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Prompts prospective members to answer up to three custom questions before admins review their request to join a private group.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Group Onboarding. Configured by Group Admins in Group Settings > Member Questions. Can include multiple-choice or written-answer formats.

### 15. Community Moderation Rules

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Sets up to 10 community rules that display on the group's "About" page, outlining expected behavior and guidelines.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Community Rules. Created under the Admin Tools menu under "Group Rules." These rules can be selected as reasons when deleting posts.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. End-to-End Encrypted Messenger Chats

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Provides private chat rooms that encrypt messages, voice calls, and media, ensuring they cannot be accessed by third parties.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Encrypted Chats. Accessed in Messenger by starting a new conversation and toggling the padlock icon, or by selecting "Secret Conversation" in chat settings.

### 17. Messenger Vanish Mode

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Deletes all viewed messages, photos, links, and voice notes automatically as soon as the recipient reads them and closes the chat window.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Ephemeral Chats. Activated within a mobile Messenger thread by swiping up from the bottom of the screen until the "Vanish Mode" screen displays.

### 18. Messenger Broadcast Channels

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: A public one-to-many messaging tool for creators and public figures to broadcast updates, voice notes, photos, and polls to a large audience of subscribers directly in their inbox.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Channel Broadcasting. Started from a Facebook Page or creator profile by tapping "Create Broadcast Channel" in the settings. Followers receive an invitation notification to join.

### 19. Reaction Customization

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows users to react to specific chat bubbles with a customized selection of emojis.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Emoji Reactions. Activated by long-pressing a message inside Messenger, tapping the "+" icon, and editing the default quick-reaction palette.

### 20. Consolidated Shared Media Viewer

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Groups all media, files, documents, and web links shared in a message thread into a structured library.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Shared Chat Library. Accessed by opening a chat thread, tapping the contact’s name at the top, and selecting "View media, files & links."

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. Localized Marketplace Listing

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows users to post items for sale, listing them in a geographic grid accessible to local buyers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: P2P E-Commerce. Accessed via the Marketplace tab > "Sell." Sellers fill in category, item condition, price, location radius, and upload photos.

### 22. Professional Shops

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Digital storefronts that allow businesses to showcase catalogs and sell products directly through Facebook.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Storefront integration. Set up using Meta Commerce Manager. Supports catalog uploads, inventory management, and direct checkouts via Meta Pay.

### 23. Product Tagging in Media

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows pages to tag specific items from their Shop catalog directly in feed photos, videos, or reels.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Clickable Store tags. Managed during the post composition phase by clicking "Tag Products" and selecting items from the synced store catalog.

### 24. Marketplace Buyer/Seller Ratings

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A feedback system that lets buyers and sellers rate their transaction experience on a 1-5 star scale.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Transaction Reviews. Prompts appear in Messenger after a listing is marked as "Sold" to rate the specific buyer/seller, displaying scores on profiles.

### 25. Shipping and Payment Processing

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Integrates shipping label creation and payment collection through Facebook Marketplace for nationwide delivery.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Marketplace Logistics. Configured by selecting "Shipping and Local Pickup" during listing. Payments are processed via Meta Pay, generating shipping labels.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. Meta Business Suite Insights

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: An analytical workspace that compiles audience reach, trends, growth, and advertising performance across Facebook and Instagram.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Business Analytics. Accessible at `business.facebook.com`. Offers customized reports that track paid vs. organic reach over custom date ranges.

### 27. Facebook Professional Dashboard

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A mobile hub showing real-time statistics on net followers, post impressions, engagement rates, and content reach.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Mobile Analytics Hub. Accessed via the "View Tools" button on your Facebook page profile. Displays a comparison of metrics against the prior 28-day period.

### 28. Detailed Audience Demographics

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Visualizes information regarding the age, gender, geographic cities, and countries of a page's followers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Demographics Visualizer. Located in Meta Business Suite Insights > Audience. Helps brands adjust their post timing and topics based on active regions.

### 29. Content Scheduling Calendar

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A grid calendar displaying scheduled, drafted, and published posts, allowing creators to organize content timelines.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Planner Calendar. Managed in Meta Business Suite > Planner. Users can drag and drop scheduled posts to reschedule them visually.

### 30. Meta Page Access Management

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Allows page owners to assign granular, task-based access permissions (e.g., content creation, messaging, ads management) to team members without sharing password credentials.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Collaborative Page Management. Go to Settings > Page Setup > Page Access. Tap "Add New," input the email address or profile name of the team member, select their permission level (Full control or Partial access), and send the invitation.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. Moderation Assist

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: An automated assistant that screens and hides comments based on rules set by the page owner.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Automated Comment Moderation. Accessed via Facebook Professional Dashboard > Moderation Assist. Rules include hiding comments containing links or profanity.

### 32. Profile Lock

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Restricts access to a user's profile photos, albums, and posts, displaying them only to confirmed friends.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Lockdowns. Enabled on mobile by visiting your profile, clicking the three dots next to "Edit Profile," and selecting "Lock Profile."

### 33. Custom Comment Filters

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Allows page owners to upload a CSV file containing specific offensive words or phrases to be blocked.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Keyword Blocklists. Configured in Page Settings > Privacy > Public Posts > Comment Moderation. Comments containing these terms are hidden automatically.

### 34. Support Inbox

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: A tracking portal where users can monitor reports they have filed and view notices of copyright or community standards violations.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Violation Tracking. Accessed in Settings > Support Inbox. Shows status updates and communication history with Facebook's review team.

### 35. Custom Audience Lists

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Divides Facebook friends into subgroups (e.g., "Close Friends," "Acquaintances") to tailor post visibility.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Granular Share Permissions. Managed under Friends > Custom Lists. When posting, users select the specific list from the audience selector dropdown.

## Category 8: Notifications & Time Management

### 36. Your Time on Facebook (Quiet Mode)

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Tracks app usage statistics and lets users schedule quiet periods where push notifications are muted.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Usage Break Scheduling. Found in Settings > Your Time on Facebook. Users can set a daily reminder limit or schedule "Quiet Mode" to block notifications.

### 37. Custom Notification Controls

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Allows users to customize how they receive updates for tags, comments, group activity, and page updates.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Alert Channel Settings. Managed in Settings > Notifications. Users can turn push, email, and SMS notifications on or off for individual activity categories.

### 38. Daily Time Limits

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Set a daily time threshold that prompts a full-screen block once reached, forcing users to exit.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Screen Limits. Settings > Your Time on Facebook > Set Daily Time Reminder. Choose limits between 15 minutes and 23 hours.

### 39. Notification Snoozing

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Mutes all push notification alerts immediately for a temporary duration (e.g., 8 hours).
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Temporary Muting. Tap and hold a push notification, or navigate to Settings > Notifications, select "Mute notifications," and choose a time.

### 40. Group Notification Settings

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Configures post alerts on a per-group basis to limit feed clutter from hyperactive communities.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Group Alert Frequencies. Inside any Facebook Group, tap "Joined" > "Manage Notifications," and select "All Posts," "Highlights," or "Off."

## Category 9: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 41. Facebook Graph API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Programmatic HTTP interface enabling developers to query, post, and delete profiles, page statistics, posts, and comments.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: RESTful API Endpoint. Configured under Meta Developer Portal. Integrates OAuth access tokens to call HTTP endpoints (e.g., `/v20.0/me`).

### 42. Facebook Login SDK (OAuth)

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Allows developers to add a secure "Log in with Facebook" button to external websites and applications, easing user signup.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Federated Authentication. Developers import the Facebook Login JavaScript or mobile SDKs, linking authentication callbacks to their servers.

### 43. Facebook Webhooks API (Real-time Developer Alerts)

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Delivers real-time HTTPS callbacks to developer servers for events such as lead generation forms, page messages, and feed edits.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Real-time Webhook Alerts. Configured on the Meta App Dashboard under the Webhooks product tab. Developers select a topic (e.g., "Page"), enter their callback URL and verify token, and subscribe to fields.

### 44. Business Manager API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Connects external agency platforms to programmatically manage ad accounts, product catalogs, and pixel trackers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Enterprise Commerce API. Integration points for Meta Business Suite, calling ad-creation and tracking pixel APIs programmatically.

### 45. Meta Marketing API SDK

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Programmatic SDKs in Python, Java, and PHP that allow developers to automate ad campaign creation, custom audience building, and real-time budget management.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Automated Ads SDK. Integrated using package managers (e.g., `pip install facebook-business`). Requires a Marketing API app access token and system user credentials.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. Login Alerts & Unrecognized Device Tracking

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Sends immediate email or push notifications when an account is accessed from a new browser or physical location.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Security Alert Logs. Enabled in Account Center > Password and Security > Login Alerts. Shows a map of active sessions.

### 47. Legacy Contact Designation

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Permits a user to choose a friend or family member to manage their account if it becomes memorialized after their passing.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Post-Mortem Profile Management. Configured under Settings > Account Center > Personal Details > Memorialization. Legacy contacts can pin posts.

### 48. Meta Account Center

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Central hub to manage password, two-factor authentication, ad preferences, and shared details across Facebook and Instagram.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Federated Meta Settings. Open Settings > Account Center. Links cross-platform security preferences in a unified dashboard.

### 49. App Password Generation

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Generates unique, one-time passwords for older platforms (such as Xbox or smart TVs) that do not support 2FA formats.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Security credentials. Configured under Account Center > Password and Security > App Passwords. Generates random strings for login.

### 50. Authorized Logins (Trusted Devices Management)

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Displays a history of recognized browsers and devices that are authorized to access the account without requiring a two-factor verification code, with options to revoke access.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Login Device Auditing. Access via Meta Account Center > Password and security > Two-factor authentication > Authorized logins. Select recognized devices to inspect details or tap the "X" button to log them out and require 2FA on their next attempt.
