# YouTube Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for YouTube, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. YouTube Shorts Editor

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: A mobile creation tool for recording, editing, and publishing short-form, vertical videos. It provides creators with a library of licensed audio tracks, speed adjustments, recording timers, and a green screen utility.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Mobile Video Creator. Open the YouTube mobile app, tap the "+" button in the center of the bottom navigation bar, select "Create a Short," and use the side panel controls to record and edit your video.

### 2. YouTube Video Editor

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: A browser-based video editing workspace built directly into the YouTube Studio dashboard. It allows creators to trim or cut video segments, blur sensitive elements (like faces or license plates), and add audio tracks from the royalty-free Audio Library after a video has been uploaded.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Browser-Based Editor. Log in to YouTube Studio, navigate to the "Content" tab, click on the thumbnail of the video you wish to edit, click "Editor" in the left sidebar, and apply your cuts or blur zones.

### 3. YouTube Auto-Generated Captions

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: An automated transcription system that uses machine learning to generate captions for uploaded videos. It automatically creates timed text tracks in multiple supported languages to increase video accessibility.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Automated Captions. Navigate to YouTube Studio > Content, click on your video, select "Subtitles" from the left menu, and choose to publish or edit the auto-generated caption track.

### 4. YouTube Chapter Markers

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Divides long-form videos into distinct, clickable sections on the playback progress bar. This helps viewers navigate to specific topics or highlights easily by referencing time-stamped headings.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Time-Stamed Navigation. Open the video details page in YouTube Studio, scroll to the description box, and type a list of timestamps starting with `00:00` followed by a space and the section title.

### 5. YouTube Cards & End Screens

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Clickable visual overlays added to videos to recommend other content, promote merchandise, or encourage channel subscriptions. Cards can appear at any point in the video, while End Screens are placed in the final 5 to 20 seconds.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Interactive Screen Overlays. Open your video details in YouTube Studio, click the "End Screen" or "Cards" option in the right-hand panel, select the elements you want to add, and set their timeline positions.

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. YouTube Search Filters

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Refines search queries using granular criteria to locate relevant videos, channels, playlists, or movies. Users can filter results by upload date, video duration, definition formats (such as 4K or HDR), and sort them by relevance or view count.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Query Refinement Tools. Type a search term into the main search bar, press enter, click the "Filters" button at the top of the search results page, and select your desired filtering options.

### 7. YouTube Subscriptions Feed

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A dedicated chronological feed that displays newly published videos solely from channels the user has subscribed to. This feed bypasses algorithmic recommendation systems, giving users a simple view of updates from their favorite creators.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chronological Content Feed. Tap the "Subscriptions" tab in the bottom menu of the mobile app, or click "Subscriptions" in the left sidebar of the desktop browser interface.

### 8. YouTube Explore Tab

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A discovery portal that aggregates trending videos and categorizes content into specific interest hubs. Users can browse dedicated sections for gaming, music, movies, fashion, news, and live broadcasts to find popular videos on the platform.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Topic Trend Directory. Click the "Explore" button in the left-hand navigation pane on desktop, or tap the compass icon in the top-left corner of the mobile app home screen.

### 9. YouTube Recommended Feed

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: The primary feed displayed on the homepage, driven by deep neural network algorithms. It customizes a feed of videos based on the user's viewing history, search history, active subscriptions, and general engagement habits.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Algorithmic Home Feed. Access the homepage by loading `youtube.com` in a browser or opening the mobile app, and scroll down to view personalized recommendations.

### 10. YouTube Watch Later Playlist

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A private default playlist where users can save videos they want to watch at a later time. The playlist syncs across all logged-in devices and allows users to easily clear watched content.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Private Video Queue. Hover over a video thumbnail and click the clock icon, or tap the three-dot menu next to a video and select "Save to Watch Later" to queue it.

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. YouTube Community Posts

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: An interactive feed on the channel homepage where creators can publish text updates, polls, images, and GIFs. This allows creators to communicate and poll their audience without uploading a full video.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Channel Feed Composer. Open YouTube Studio, click the "Create" button in the top right, select "Create post," choose your post type (text, poll, or image), write the content, and click "Post."

### 12. YouTube Channel Membership

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: A recurring monthly subscription model that allows fans to financially support creators in exchange for exclusive perks. Perks include custom loyalty badges, custom emojis for live chat, access to member-only videos, and private community posts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Fan Subscription Service. Visit a participating channel's homepage, click the "Join" button next to the subscribe button, select a membership tier, and complete the billing details.

### 13. YouTube Live Chat & Moderation

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: A real-time conversation feed that displays alongside live streams and video Premieres. Creators and designated moderators can manage the chat feed using filters, word blocklists, and user timeouts to maintain a safe environment.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Stream Moderation Center. Open the Live Control Room in YouTube Studio, navigate to chat settings, assign moderator roles to trusted users, and manage live messages using the chat panel controls.

### 14. YouTube Comments & Pinning

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: A discussion section located beneath videos where viewers can post feedback, ask questions, and interact with the creator. Creators can pin a single comment to the very top of the feed to highlight important updates or positive feedback.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Comment Section Control. Scroll below a video, click the three-dot menu next to a comment you want to highlight, and select "Pin" to lock it at the top of the comment feed.

### 15. YouTube Premiere Chat

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: A specialized viewing format that allows creators and viewers to watch a newly uploaded video together in real time. It features a synchronized countdown and a live chat room that fosters community engagement during the initial broadcast.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Synced Broadcast Room. Upload a video in YouTube Studio, select "Premiere" under the visibility options, set a date and time, and join the live chat room when the broadcast countdown begins.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. YouTube Creator Studio Inbox

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: A centralized communication dashboard in YouTube Studio designed for reviewing and managing viewer interactions. It compiles and filters comments, replies, and mentions across all channel videos for streamlined responses.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Interaction Management Hub. Log in to YouTube Studio, click "Comments" in the left-hand navigation menu, and use the filter tabs (such as "I haven't responded" or "Held for review") to reply to messages.

### 17. YouTube Share to Chat

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: A sharing shortcut that compiles video links and previews to send directly to contacts. While native direct messaging inside YouTube has been deprecated, this system integrates with mobile device share sheets to forward links to messaging applications.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Video Link Sharing. Tap the "Share" button beneath any video player on mobile, select a messaging application from the system tray, choose a contact, and send the message.

### 18. YouTube Live Q&A Sessions

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: An interactive tool for live broadcasts that allows creators to host organized question-and-answer sessions. Viewers submit questions in a dedicated feed, and the creator can select and pin questions to the top of the chat for all viewers to see.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Interactive Q&A Panel. During a live stream in the Live Control Room, click the "+" icon in the chat panel, select "Start a Q&A," and pin submitted questions to the stream display.

### 19. YouTube Mention Tags

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows creators to tag other channels in video titles, descriptions, or community posts using the "@" symbol. Tagged channels receive a notification in their YouTube Studio dashboard, making collaborations and shoutouts easier.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Channel Tagging System. When writing a video title or description in YouTube Studio, type the "@" character followed immediately by the name of the channel you want to tag, and select it from the autocomplete list.

### 20. YouTube Live Chat Super Stickers

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Animated digital stickers that viewers can purchase to send during live streams and Premieres. The stickers display as bright animations in the live chat feed, helping the viewer's message stand out from normal text.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Animated Gifting Stream. Open a live chat window during a broadcast, tap the dollar sign icon, select "Super Sticker," choose an animation, and complete the purchase.

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. YouTube Partner Program (YPP)

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: The foundational monetization framework that allows eligible channels to earn revenue from advertisements and YouTube Premium views. Creators must meet specific subscriber and watch time thresholds to apply.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Monetization Program Application. Open YouTube Studio, click the "Earn" tab in the left-hand sidebar, monitor your eligibility progress, and click "Apply Now" once requirements are met.

### 22. YouTube Super Chat

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A monetization tool that allows viewers to buy highlighted messages in live chat streams. The message is pinned to the top of the feed for a set period, depending on the purchase amount, ensuring the creator can see it.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Highlighted Chat Purchase. Tap the dollar sign icon in a live stream's chat panel, select "Super Chat," choose your payment tier, write your custom message, and tap send to highlight it.

### 23. YouTube Shopping Integration

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows creators to connect official store platforms (like Shopify or Spring) to their channel. It displays a scrollable shelf of products below videos and lets creators tag items in videos and live streams.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Product Shelf Linkage. Go to YouTube Studio > Earn > Shopping, click "Connect Store," select your e-commerce platform, and manage product tags for your videos using the interface.

### 24. YouTube Super Thanks

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A tipping feature that allows viewers to show appreciation for individual uploaded videos or Shorts. When a tip is sent, the viewer's comment is posted with a distinct, colored background that displays the tip amount.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Post-Upload Tipping. Click the "Thanks" button located in the toolbar below a video player, select a tip amount, enter payment details, and publish your highlighted comment.

### 25. YouTube BrandConnect

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A matchmaking service within YouTube Studio that connects creators with brands for sponsored video campaigns. The tool manages campaign details and calculates audience value using YouTube's internal data.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Sponsorship Matchmaker. Navigate to YouTube Studio > Earn, select "BrandConnect" from the top menu, complete your creator profile, set your pricing preferences, and view brand deal offers.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. YouTube Studio Analytics

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A comprehensive reporting suite providing detailed statistics on channel performance. It tracks metrics such as total views, watch time, subscriber trends, and estimated revenue over customizable date ranges.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Performance Metrics Panel. Log in to YouTube Studio, click "Analytics" in the left navigation menu, and toggle between the "Overview," "Content," "Audience," and "Revenue" tabs.

### 27. YouTube Audience Retention Graph

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A line graph that visualizes how long viewers stay engaged throughout a specific video. It pinpoints exact seconds where viewers drop off, skip ahead, or rewatch sections, helping creators improve video pacing.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Engagement Timeline Analyzer. Go to YouTube Studio > Analytics, select a video, click "Engagement," scroll down to the "Audience retention" section, and click on the graph to analyze playback behavior.

### 28. YouTube Research Tab

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: An audience search analyzer built into YouTube Studio that displays trending search terms and search volumes. It highlights content gaps where viewers are searching for topics that do not have many high-quality videos.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Topic Demand Finder. Open YouTube Studio, click "Analytics," select the "Research" tab from the top menu, and enter search terms into the query field to review popularity.

### 29. YouTube Content ID System

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: An automated copyright detection system that scans newly uploaded videos against a database of audio and visual content submitted by copyright owners. It alerts owners of matches and applies automated actions based on their preferences.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Copyright Rights Matching. Managed via the Content ID dashboard for approved media companies. Rights holders configure rules to track, monetize, or block matching user uploads.

### 30. YouTube Page Access Roles

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Allows channel owners to invite team members to help manage the channel with granular permissions. Access roles include Manager, Editor, Editor (Limited), Subtitle Editor, and Viewer to secure channel credentials.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Collaborative Team Permissions. Open YouTube Studio, click "Settings" in the bottom-left corner, select "Permissions," click "Invite," enter the email address of your team member, select their role, and send the invitation.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. YouTube Restricted Mode

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: An optional setting that filters out potentially mature or inappropriate content from search results and feeds. It uses signals like video metadata, community flagging, and age-restrictions to identify and hide sensitive videos.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Mature Content Filter. Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner of the interface, scroll to the bottom of the pop-up menu, select "Restricted Mode," and toggle it to "On."

### 32. YouTube Custom Video Visibility

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Controls who can view an uploaded video by setting its visibility state. Creators can choose Public (visible to everyone), Unlisted (accessible only via link), or Private (visible only to the owner and selected accounts).
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Video Privacy Toggles. In YouTube Studio, navigate to the Content tab, hover over a video's visibility column, click the dropdown menu, select "Public," "Unlisted," or "Private," and save.

### 33. YouTube Comment Blocklist

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: An automated comment moderation tool that holds comments containing blocked words or links for review. Creators can customize a list of words, phrases, and usernames that are blocked from appearing publicly.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Text Filter Settings. Open YouTube Studio, go to Settings > Community, scroll down to the "Blocked Words" field, enter your list of terms, check "Block links," and click "Save."

### 34. YouTube Kids Account Restrictions

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Parental control settings that restrict content access to a kid-safe version of YouTube. Parents can select age tiers (Preschool, Younger, Older), manually approve allowed videos, and set daily viewing limits.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Child Account Setup. Open the YouTube app settings, select "Parental Settings," click on your child's profile, select "Content Settings," and adjust the age levels or approved video lists.

### 35. YouTube SafeSearch

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Integrates search engine filtering to block explicit content, sexual imagery, and extreme violence from search results. This helps ensure that video searches return family-friendly listings.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Search Moderation Toggle. Accessed via connected Google Account settings under the "Search Settings" panel, where users can toggle the SafeSearch filter on or off.

## Category 8: Notifications & Time Management

### 36. YouTube Notification Bell

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Allows viewers to customize how they receive push and email notifications for channels they subscribe to. Viewers can set preferences to "All" (every upload), "Personalized" (based on viewing habits), or "None."
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Subscription Alert Selector. Navigate to a creator's channel page, click the bell icon next to the "Subscribed" button, and select your preferred notification frequency from the dropdown menu.

### 37. YouTube Scheduled Video Release

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Allows creators to schedule an uploaded video to transition from private to public status at a future date and time. This helps creators coordinate video releases with specific times when their audience is most active.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Video Publishing Scheduler. When uploading a video in YouTube Studio, go to the "Visibility" step, select "Schedule," choose your timezone, date, and time, and click "Schedule."

### 38. YouTube Pause Watch History

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Suspends the tracking of videos watched by the user, preventing newly viewed videos from influencing homepage recommendations. This is useful when searching for topics you do not want added to your profile.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Tracking Control Panel. Open the left menu on YouTube, click "History," then select "Pause watch history" from the right-hand panel on desktop, or access "Manage all history" on mobile.

### 39. YouTube Take a Break Reminder

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: An in-app utility that prompts users to stop watching videos after a specified period of continuous usage. The reminder displays as a full-screen alert that pauses video playback.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Screen Time Limit. Open the mobile app, tap your profile icon > Settings > General, select "Remind me to take a break," and set the reminder frequency (e.g., every 1 hour).

### 40. YouTube Bedtime Reminder

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: A time-management feature that alerts users when it is time to close the app at night. Users can set the reminder to display immediately at the scheduled hour or wait until the current video finishes.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Nighttime Usage Alert. Navigate to Settings > General in the mobile app, tap "Remind me when it's bedtime," set your start and end hours, and choose your display preferences.

## Category 9: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 41. YouTube Data API v3

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A programmatic REST API that allows developers to search videos, retrieve channel statistics, upload video files, and manage playlists. This interface supports integration of YouTube's core content catalog into external applications.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: REST Data API. Register a project in the Google Cloud Console, enable the YouTube Data API v3, generate API keys or OAuth credentials, and make HTTP requests to the API endpoints (e.g., `https://www.googleapis.com/youtube/v3/videos`).

### 42. YouTube Player API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: An iframe-based JavaScript API that allows developers to embed a YouTube video player directly into their websites. It provides programmatic controls to play, pause, seek, adjust volume, and listen to player state changes using custom code.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Client-Side Video Player. Include the YouTube Iframe Player API script in your website's HTML, create a placeholder element, and initialize the player object using JavaScript.

### 43. YouTube Live Streaming API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A specialized component of the Data API that allows developers to schedule live broadcasts, manage chat feeds, and monitor stream health programmatically. This enables external streaming software to automate stream configurations.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Programmatic Live Controller. Authenticate with OAuth2 credentials, and call live resource endpoints (e.g., `https://www.googleapis.com/youtube/v3/liveBroadcasts`) to create and bind video streams.

### 44. YouTube Analytics API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Retrieves custom reports on channel performance, video engagement, and viewer demographics. This API allows creators and enterprise partners to build automated reports and dashboards.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Analytics Data Retrieval. Set up OAuth2 user authentication, and execute HTTP queries against the Analytics endpoint (`https://youtubeanalytics.googleapis.com/v2/reports`) specifying parameters such as dimensions, metrics, and dates.

### 45. YouTube Reporting API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Designed for enterprise channels and content owners to download bulk datasets containing system performance and monetization metrics. It delivers pre-generated CSV reports containing raw data for deep analytical processing.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Bulk Data Pipeline. Set up OAuth credentials, call the API endpoint (`https://youtubereporting.googleapis.com/v1/jobs`) to create reporting jobs, and download generated data files using the returned URLs.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. YouTube Google Account Recovery

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Configures backup recovery details to restore account access if a password is forgotten or the account is compromised. Users link backup email addresses and phone numbers to receive secure verification codes.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Access Recovery Settings. Tap your profile icon, select "Manage your Google Account," navigate to the "Security" tab, and add credentials under the "Ways we can verify it's you" section.

### 47. YouTube Account Linkage

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Connects third-party gaming, streaming, or smart home accounts to a YouTube profile. Linking accounts allows users to earn in-game rewards during sponsored streams and simplifies logins on external devices.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Third-Party Integrations. Navigate to YouTube Settings in a browser or mobile app, select "Connected apps" from the menu, choose the target platform, and complete the authorization flow.

### 48. YouTube Premium Membership Settings

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: A subscription management portal where users can manage billing methods, toggle auto-renew settings, and view membership benefits. It also allows users to configure offline download quality settings for mobile devices.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Subscription Billing Manager. Open the mobile app, tap your profile icon, select "Purchases and memberships," and click on your Premium membership plan to modify details.

### 49. YouTube Two-Step Verification

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Secures login sessions by requiring a second authentication factor in addition to a password. Authentication options include security keys, Google prompt notifications, or SMS verification codes.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Two-Factor Login Setup. Go to your Google Account settings, select "Security," tap "2-Step Verification" under the "Signing in to Google" section, and configure your preferred verification method.

### 50. YouTube Account Data Download

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows users to download a comprehensive archive of their YouTube activity, including uploaded videos, search history, watch history, comment logs, and playlists. This ensures data transparency and account portability.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Activity Data Export. Navigate to the Google Takeout dashboard (`takeout.google.com`), select "YouTube and YouTube Music" from the product list, select the files you want to download, choose your archive format, and request the download link.
