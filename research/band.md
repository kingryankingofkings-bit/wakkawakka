# Band Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for Band, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. Post Rich Text Editor

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows users to write community posts with styled text including bold, italics, underlines, bulleted lists, and inline hyperlinks.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Text Editing. Click the post composer field on the group main board, select text to modify, and use the formatting toolbar overlay to apply formatting.

### 2. Polling Tool with Multi-Choice & Anonymous voting

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Enables group members or admins to create interactive polls that support multiple selections, user-added choices, and anonymous voting.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Community Polling. In the post composer, click the "Poll" icon, write the question, add options, and check the toggles for "Allow multiple choices" or "Anonymous voting."

### 3. To-Do List and Task Assigner

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows group members to create checkboxes for specific tasks, set deadlines, and assign them to group members.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Task Management. Click the "+" button in the post composer, select "To-Do," enter the task title, set a due date on the calendar picker, and select members to assign the task to.

### 4. Attachment Uploader (PDF, Doc, ZIP)

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Enables uploading and sharing of productivity and binary files directly within posts and chat rooms, supporting large file attachments.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: File Sharing. Tap the paperclip icon in the post composer or chat input box, browse local device storage to choose a PDF, document, or archive file up to 2GB, and upload.

### 5. Image Markup / Annotation Tool

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Provides built-in drawing and annotation tools to edit images, add labels, insert arrows, and crop before publishing.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Image Annotations. Upload a photo in the post composer, tap the image thumbnail, select the edit/markup icon, use the brush or text tools to highlight specific details, and tap "Done."

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. Hash-tag Directory & Filters

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Automatically catalogs posts using hashtags and provides a directory to view all group content associated with specific topics.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Tag Navigation. Click on any hyperlinked hashtag (e.g., `#announcement`) in a post to open a feed of posts sharing that tag, or browse the group's hashtag index.

### 7. Band Directory & Public Search

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Allows users to explore and join public Band groups categorized by interests, hobby types, or search terms.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Directory Discovery. Navigate to the "Discover" tab, browse top-level categories (e.g., Sports, Gaming, Parents), or enter search terms to locate open public groups.

### 8. Pinned Post Section (Notice board)

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Highlights important announcements by pinning up to 10 posts at the top of the Band board for easy visibility by all members.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Pinned Notices. Click the drop-down menu on a published post, select "Pin as notice," and the post will appear in the pinned announcement carousel at the top of the group feed.

### 9. Global Search within Band

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Allows users to run keyword searches across all posts, comments, chat histories, uploaded files, and photos within a specific Band.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Contextual Search. Tap the search icon at the top of the Band, input a search term, and use the filter tabs (Posts, Chats, Files, Photos) to narrow down results.

### 10. Shared Album Media Browser

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Organizes all uploaded photos and videos in a dedicated, structured gallery interface featuring folder organization.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Media Folders. Click the "Albums" tab in the Band menu, create custom folders, and browse uploaded media sorted by upload date or contributing member.

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. RSVP Event Calendar

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: A shared calendar where admins can schedule events, collect RSVPs, and manage attendee lists.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Calendar RSVPs. Select the "Calendar" tab, tap an event date, click RSVP, and select "Attending," "Maybe," or "Not Attending" to update your status.

### 12. Member Profiles & Bio Cards

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Custom user profile cards within each Band, showing band-specific roles, bios, contact details, and activity histories.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Community Directory. Access the member directory list from the side menu, tap any user's profile card, and view their band-specific introduction and past posts.

### 13. Band Level / Roles Administration

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows creators to designate member roles (Admin, Co-admin, Member) and customize group permissions granularly.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Permission Governance. Go to Band Settings > Member settings > Change Member Role, select a user, and assign administrative permissions.

### 14. Birthday Calendar & Auto-congratulations

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Aggregates member birthdays in a group calendar and automatically publishes celebratory notices on the Band board.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Milestone Reminders. Enable the "Show member birthdays" setting in the group calendar. The platform will auto-generate a birthday congratulatory post when the date is reached.

### 15. Shout-outs & Badges

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Permits admins and members to award recognition badges or custom digital stickers to highlight exceptional member contributions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Peer Recognition. Tap the comment section of a post, click the sticker menu, select from the custom badge pack (e.g., "Star Contributor"), and post the sticker.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. Group Chat Rooms (Open & Private)

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows members of a Band to open dedicated, real-time group chat threads for quick messaging separate from the main board.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Live Chatrooms. Go to the Chat tab, click the "+" icon, select the members you want to chat with, select "Group Chat," and enter a room name.

### 17. Voice & Video Group Calling

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Built-in VoIP voice and video calls within chat rooms, allowing multiple members to join and share screens.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Group Calls. Open any chat room, tap the call phone icon in the header, choose "Voice Call" or "Video Call," and invite active members.

### 18. Voice Messages

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows users to record and send short audio voice clips directly into a chat window.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Push-to-Record. Press and hold the microphone icon next to the chat text box, speak your message, and release to send the audio recording.

### 19. Message Read Receipts (Detailed view)

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Tracks who has opened chat messages, displaying a count and a list of specific readers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Receipt Auditing. Tap the numeric "Read" indicator next to any sent message bubble in a chat to open a drawer displaying the names of members who have viewed it.

### 20. DM Ban/Ignore list

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows users to mute or block individual group members from sending direct message requests.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chat Blocking. Open a chat room with a specific member, tap the menu list icon, and select "Mute notifications" or "Block member" to halt further communications.

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. Paid Member Dues & Stripe Connect

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Restricts access to a Band to paying subscribers, managing recurring membership fee collection.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Membership Subscription Gate. Go to Band Settings > Subscription dues, set the price and billing frequency, click "Connect with Stripe," and complete stripe authorization.

### 22. In-App Sticker Store

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Offers premium digital sticker and emoji packs for purchase to customize messaging and board replies.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Digital Storefront. Click the emoticon icon in the composer, tap the "+" store shortcut, select a sticker pack, and complete the purchase using Google Play or Apple App Store billing.

### 23. Group Merch / Print-on-Demand Integration

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Integrates with custom merchandising platforms to let groups design and sell custom clothing or gear to members.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Merch Linking. Navigate to the Band Business Center, click "Create Store," upload a group logo graphic, choose items to print, and publish the shop link on the notice board.

### 24. Ad-Free Group Subscription

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows Band admins to pay a platform fee that removes all third-party advertisements from their Band for all members.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Ad Removal. Navigate to Band Settings > "Remove Advertisements," select the monthly or annual subscription tier, and enter payment credentials.

### 25. Sponsored Post Placement

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Enables organizations to run sponsored banner ads targeted to public interest Band directories.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Ad Manager. Go to Band Ad Console, set your daily ad budget, upload image or text ad creatives, select interest targets, and launch.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. Band Member Activity Reports

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Provides creators with visual charts summarizing daily active users, new membership signups, and board engagement levels.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Group Analytics. Navigate to Band Settings > Activity Stats to view analytics dashboards showing member login and activity trends.

### 27. File Storage Quota Analytics

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Tracks the storage quota consumed by uploaded group attachments, photos, and files.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Storage Auditing. Go to Band Settings > Manage Storage to view a detailed breakdown of total disk space used out of the group's quota.

### 28. Sign-up Sheets (Duty Roster Creator)

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Creates interactive volunteer sign-up sheets that allow members to lock in specific time slots or duties.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Roster Coordination. Click the "+" composer tool, select "Sign-up Sheet," input a title, add separate shift times or items needed, and publish to collect signups.

### 29. Poll Results Export

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Generates a detailed CSV file mapping poll options to the names of members who voted for them.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Poll Auditing. Open a completed poll on the board, click the options menu, select "Export to CSV," and save the spreadsheet file.

### 30. Public Band SEO Analytics

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Tracks web search traffic, click-through rates, and Google indexing statistics for public Bands.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Search Optimization. Access SEO Tools in Band Settings, viewing impressions, page ranks, and search query keywords that lead users to the public page.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. Custom Admission Questions

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Prompts applicants with customized screening questions that they must answer before admins approve their join request.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Onboarding Gate. Go to Band Settings > Join settings > Admission Questions, write up to three short-answer questions, and review answers under the "Requests" dashboard.

### 32. Band Invite Code / QR Code Access

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Restricts group joining to secure invitation links, numerical invite codes, or scanned QR codes.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Invite Controls. Click "Invite," choose to generate an invite link or QR code, set the link's expiration duration (e.g., 24 hours), and distribute.

### 33. Word Filtering (Auto-moderation)

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Automatically reviews board posts and comments, blocking those that contain specified keywords.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Content Censorship. Go to Band Settings > Moderation > Word Filter, type keywords to ban, and toggle "Auto-delete matches."

### 34. Hide Member List

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Allows admins to hide the member directory from regular members to protect privacy.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Member Directory Privacy. Go to Band Settings > Privacy settings, and toggle "Hide member list from regular members" to active.

### 35. Report & Kick/Ban Member Tool

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Enables admins to permanently eject disruptive members and ban them from rejoin attempts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Community Expulsion. Tap the member directory list, click the target member, select "Kick out," choose a violation category, and check the "Ban permanently" box.

## Category 8: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 36. Band Open API (REST)

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A developer API providing access to post announcements, read boards, and fetch member details programmatically.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Web Service Integration. Register an app on `developers.band.us` to acquire token keys, making REST HTTP requests to write to specific Band endpoints.

### 37. External Calendar Sync (iCal/Google)

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Syncs Band calendar events to external personal calendar software.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Calendar Sync. Open Calendar settings, click "Export Calendar Feed," copy the iCal URL, and paste it into Google Calendar or Outlook settings.

### 38. Google Drive & Dropbox File Linker

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Connects cloud storage platforms to select and link remote documents within post updates.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Cloud Storage Link. In the post composer, click the cloud icon, authenticate your Google Drive or Dropbox login, select the file, and insert the download link.

### 39. Microsoft Teams / Slack Integration Bridge

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Relays Band announcements to internal enterprise chat rooms.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Webhook Relays. Copy the incoming webhook URL from your Slack channel settings, navigate to Band Settings > Integrations, and paste it to configure auto-relaying.

### 40. Smart TV Casting

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Streams group video feeds, live webcasts, or photo galleries onto smart TVs.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Media Casting. Open a video post or photo within the mobile app, tap the Chromecast or AirPlay icon in the player container, and choose the output TV device.

## Category 9: Notifications & Time Management

### 41. Urgent Notice Alerts

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Sends a push alert that overrides normal muting preferences for emergency communications.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Emergency Broadcast. When creating a notice post, check the box "Set as Urgent Notice" before publishing. The post will ring on all member phones even if muted.

### 42. Per-Band Notification Profiles

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Permits configuring distinct notification rules and sound alerts for different Bands.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Custom Alert Behavior. Click Band Settings > Notifications, and customize separate toggles for posts, comments, chats, and events.

### 43. Snooze Group Chats

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Silences notifications from active chat rooms temporarily without exiting the chat.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Temporary Muting. Open the chat room, click the settings cog or menu, tap the bell icon, and select a snooze duration (e.g., "Mute for 1 hour" or "Mute for 8 hours").

### 44. Daily Digest Emails

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Consolidates unread group activity into a single daily notification summary email.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Email Digest. Navigate to Account settings > Email settings, and check "Send daily digest of unread board posts."

### 45. Do Not Disturb Schedule

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Auto-blocks all incoming push alerts during specified daily hours (e.g., sleeping hours).
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Sleep Schedules. Open App Settings > Notifications > Do Not Disturb, set the start time (e.g., 10:00 PM) and end time (e.g., 7:00 AM), and toggle on.

---

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. Multi-Account Login Switcher

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows logging into multiple Band profiles on a single mobile device and switching profiles.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Switching. Open the main side menu, tap your profile avatar dropdown, select "Add Account," log in, and tap avatars to switch.

### 47. Biometric App Lock (Face ID/Fingerprint)

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Locks the Band app behind Face ID, fingerprint, or PIN lock screen security.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: App Access Security. Navigate to Settings > Security > App Lock, toggle on, select your preferred biometric method, and configure the auto-lock timeout.

### 48. Primary Phone Number verification

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Links the profile to a validated cellular number via SMS one-time password verification.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Phone Verification. Open Settings > Account details > Phone number, enter your number, input the 6-digit confirmation code received by SMS, and save.

### 49. Connected Device Management

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Lists all active devices and browsers logged into the account, with options to force remote logout.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Session Auditing. Navigate to Settings > Security > Logged-in Devices, review active sessions, and tap "Log out" next to unrecognized devices.

### 50. GDPR Data Retention Settings

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows users to configure how long their personal logs, messages, and deleted posts are retained in platform databases.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Data Expiration. Open Settings > Privacy > Data retention, select the auto-delete time limit for deleted posts or chats (e.g., 3 months, 6 months, or 1 year), and click "Apply."
