# Facebook Messenger Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for Facebook Messenger, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. Messenger Stories
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Shares photos and short videos that remain visible to friends for 24 hours, supporting overlays, text, and music.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Ephemeral Stories. Tap "Add to Story" in the People or Stories tab, capture or select media, add stickers, and share.

### 2. AR Camera Effects
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Applies augmented reality filters, face distortions, and virtual backgrounds to photos, videos, and live video calls.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Augmented Reality. Open the camera in Messenger or start a video call, then tap the effects/smiley icon to browse and apply filters.

### 3. Soundmojis
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Sends emojis that play a short, matching sound clip in the chat window when tapped.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Expression. Tap the emoji button in a chat, select the sound wave icon, choose a Soundmoji to preview, and tap send.

### 4. Boomerang Loops
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Records short, back-and-forth looping video clips using the in-app camera.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Looping Video. Open the Messenger camera, select the "Boomerang" mode from the sidebar, press and hold record, and send.

### 5. Word Effects
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Triggers screen-filling floating emoji animations when specific custom key phrases are sent in a chat.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Triggered Animations. Tap the chat header info page, select "Word Effects," define a keyword or phrase, select a corresponding emoji, and save.

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. Active Contacts Tab
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Displays a list of Facebook friends and contacts who are currently online or were recently active.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Presence List. Tap the "People" tab in the bottom navigation bar and select "Active" to view a real-time list of online friends with green status indicators.

### 7. Marketplace Chats Index
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Organizes buyer and seller conversations from Facebook Marketplace in a dedicated sub-folder.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: E-commerce Folder. Select the menu drawer in Messenger, tap "Marketplace" to view all active product negotiation and purchase chats.

### 8. Keyword Chat Search
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Finds specific text messages and keyword matches across all historical chats or within a single chat thread.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Text Queries. Tap the search bar at the top of the chat list, type your search query, or tap a chat header and select "Search in Conversation."

### 9. Archived Conversations
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Hides conversations from the main chats feed by moving them into an archived archive folder.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chat Archiving. Swipe left on a chat thread in the chats list and select "Archive" to hide it. Access it anytime via the menu drawer under "Archive."

### 10. Message Requests Filter
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Filters messages sent by users who are not connected with the recipient, categorizing them as spam or request inquiries.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Request Inbox. Tap the menu drawer in Messenger, select "Message Requests" to view pending messages, and choose to "Accept" or "Delete" them.

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. Group Chats
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Combines multiple users into a single chat room for text chat, media sharing, and group audio/video calling.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Group Conversations. Tap the compose pencil icon at the top right, toggle "Create a group chat," select contacts, name the group, and tap "Create."

### 12. Broadcast Channels
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Public message threads where creators can broadcast announcements, updates, and polls to their subscribers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: One-to-Many Channels. Creators set up a broadcast channel from their page or profile info page, invite subscribers, and post updates.

### 13. Watch Together Co-Viewing
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows group call participants to watch videos, Facebook watch shows, and movies together in real-time sync.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Media Co-watching. During a Messenger video call, swipe up to access the menu, tap "Watch Together," and search or select a video.

### 14. Co-AR Call Effects
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Enables video call participants to apply matching or interactive AR filters that react to each other simultaneously.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Joint Filters. During a video call, select the Effects icon, choose "Group Effects," and select an effect for all participants to interact with.

### 15. Shared Group Albums
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows group chat members to upload, view, and organize photos and videos into shared albums inside the group.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Collaborative Media. Inside a group chat info page, tap "Shared Media" or "Albums," tap "Create Album," name it, and add media files.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. Voice Messaging Waves
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Sends voice notes with visual sound wave previews that let recipients scrub through audio durations.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Notes. Press and hold the microphone icon in a chat to record, swipe up to lock, and tap send to share the wave preview.

### 17. Group Video Calls
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Launches high-definition video calls hosting multiple participants with grid layouts and interactive call options.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Video Meetings. Open a group chat thread, tap the video camera icon at the top right to start a video call, notifying all group members.

### 18. Video Screen Sharing
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Broadcasts the caller's mobile or desktop screen display to other participants during a video call.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Live Screen Sharing. Swipe up on the control panel during a video call, tap "Share your screen," confirm the permission, and browse your device.

### 19. Forwarding Limits
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Limits forwarding a message to a maximum of 5 chats at a time to prevent the spread of spam and misinformation.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Spam Prevention. Select a message, tap the "Forward" button, and select up to 5 individual chats or groups before the platform blocks additional targets.

### 20. Swipe to Reply
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Quotes a specific message in a chat by swiping right on it, allowing structured, inline conversations.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Threaded Replies. Swipe right on any message in a chat thread to attach it as a quote above your message composer.

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. Peer-to-Peer Payments
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows users in supported regions to send and receive money directly through chat using a linked debit card or PayPal account.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Direct Payments. Tap the "+" attachment icon in a chat, select "Payments," enter the dollar amount, verify authentication, and send.

### 22. Sponsored Inbox Messages
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Displays targeted promotional ads from businesses directly within the Messenger chat list alongside user conversations.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Inline Ads. Managed via Meta Ads Manager. Businesses target demographics, and the ad displays in the user's inbox; tapping it opens a chat with the business.

### 23. Click-to-Messenger Ads
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Ad formats on Facebook or Instagram that open a Messenger chat window with a pre-populated greeting when clicked.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Ad Routing. Set up in Meta Ads Manager with "Messenger" selected as the target messaging app, linking social media campaigns to direct chat flows.

### 24. In-Chat Business Checkout
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Enables businesses to send payment requests and product links for checkout directly within the chat window.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Conversational Commerce. A customer clicks a product card sent by a business, completes shipping/payment details in the overlay, and buys.

### 25. Subscription Message Alerts
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows businesses to send recurring automated notifications, updates, and promotions to users who have opted into subscription alerts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Automated Alerts. Configure via the Messenger Platform API, setting up custom subscription flows and scheduling broadcast lists.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. Business Page Inbox
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A unified inbox that allows page administrators to manage customer conversations from both Facebook and Instagram.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Multi-Page Inbox. Access via Meta Business Suite or the Messenger app by switching the active profile to the Facebook Page.

### 27. Automated Instant Responses
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Automatically responds to customer messages with predefined welcome greetings or away messages during non-business hours.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Automated Replies. Set up in Meta Business Suite > Inbox > Automations. Customize triggers, active times, and reply templates.

### 28. Response Time Insights
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Displays metrics on how quickly a page responds to customer inquiries, showcasing the response badge on the profile.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Responsiveness Metrics. View in Meta Business Suite Inbox settings. Highlights "Typically replies in minutes" or "hours" based on live chat history.

### 29. Customer Lead Templates
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Creates interactive questionnaires within the chat window to qualify leads and gather customer contact information automatically.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chat Lead Generation. Configure in Meta Ads Manager or Page settings under Automated Chat, setting custom fields (e.g. email, phone number).

### 30. Conversation Labels
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Organizes customer chats by applying custom labels like "New Customer," "Pending Payment," or "Order Shipped."
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chat Tagging. Open a conversation in Meta Business Suite Inbox, tap the label icon in the details panel, select or create a label, and apply.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. Encrypted Chat Threads
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Provides end-to-end encrypted chats where all messages, media, and calls are stored securely on-device.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: E2EE Chats. Swipe up on an active chat to enter Vanish Mode, or start a new chat and toggle the lock icon to initiate secure end-to-end encryption.

### 32. Messenger App Lock
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Secures access to the Messenger app using biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) or the device passcode.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Biometric Protection. Go to Settings > Privacy & Safety > App Lock, toggle "Require Face ID" (or fingerprint), and select the lock timing.

### 33. Granular Block Options
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Allows users to block someone only on Messenger (messages and calls) while keeping them as a friend on Facebook.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Contact Blocking. Tap a contact's photo in chat, select "Block," and choose "Block messages and calls" or "Block on Facebook."

### 34. In-Chat Safety Notices
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Displays safety tips and educational banners when a user receives a message from a suspicious account or stranger.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Threat Mitigation. Messenger automatically displays banner notices at the top of chats containing links to report or block accounts behaving suspiciously.

### 35. Restricted Accounts Mute
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Mutes a contact's notifications and hides their chat from the active chat list without letting them know they are restricted.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Restriction. Tap a contact's name, select "Restrict" from the privacy options. The thread is moved out of sight and read receipts are disabled.

## Category 8: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 36. Messenger Platform API
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Connects external backend servers to Messenger to send and receive text, templates, and quick replies.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Developer Platform. Register a Meta Developer App, link a Facebook Page, set up Webhooks, and obtain access tokens to make API requests.

### 37. Webview Chat Integration
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Allows developers to open custom HTML/CSS/JS web pages inside a modal window directly within the Messenger app.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Interactive Modals. Configure a URL button in a generic template to open with the `messenger_extensions` SDK enabled for context sharing.

### 38. Bot-to-Human Handover Protocol
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Passes control of a conversation from an automated chatbot to a live customer service agent seamlessly.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Session Handover. Use the Handover Protocol API to send a request to transfer the control from the Primary Receiver bot to the Secondary Receiver inbox.

### 39. Website Chat Plugin
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Embeds a Messenger chat window on external websites, letting visitors chat with the business using their Facebook account.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Web Embed. In Meta Business Suite, configure the Chat Plugin, whitelist the website's domain, copy the generated code snippet, and paste it into the HTML.

### 40. Business Persona API
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Allows developers to display custom names and avatars for customer service agents rather than the generic Page name.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Agent Personalization. Create a Persona object via the API, and include the `persona_id` in the JSON payload of outbound messages.

## Category 9: Notifications & Time Management

### 41. Custom Chat Themes
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Changes the chat bubble colors, gradients, and background wallpaper for individual chat threads.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Visual Themes. Open a chat, tap the contact or group name at the top, select "Theme," and pick a theme preset (e.g. Pride, Ocean, Custom gradients).

### 42. Floating Chat Heads
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Displays round chat bubbles on top of other mobile applications (Android only) for quick messaging access.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Multi-tasking Bubbles. Go to Settings, toggle "Chat Heads" to on, and allow the "Display over other apps" permission in Android settings.

### 43. Multi-Option Thread Muting
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Mutes alerts for specific chat threads for pre-defined durations (15 minutes, 1 hour, 8 hours, 24 hours, or Until I turn it back on).
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Alert Muter. Long-press a chat thread in the chats list, tap "Mute," select the mute duration, and confirm.

### 44. Hide Notification Previews
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Hides the sender's name and message content in push notifications, displaying only "1 new message" or similar.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Notification Privacy. Go to Settings > Notifications & Sounds, and toggle off "Show previews" to secure incoming alerts.

### 45. Do Not Disturb Toggle
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Temporarily pauses all Messenger notifications for a set duration, letting users focus without distractions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Mute All Notifications. Go to Settings > Notifications & Sounds, tap "Do Not Disturb," and select the pause duration.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. Meta Accounts Center Sync
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Syncs settings, profiles, and login details across Meta platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Messenger).
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Sync. Go to Settings > Accounts Center to link Meta accounts, coordinate profile pictures, and sync active statuses.

### 47. Active Status Indicator
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Toggles the green dot status display that shows contacts when the user is online or active.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Online Status. Go to Settings > Active Status, and toggle off "Show when you're active" to hide presence details.

### 48. Multi-Device Login Sync
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Synchronizes chat threads, settings, and read statuses across all logged-in devices in real-time.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Device Sync. Authenticate via Meta Accounts Center on new devices to automatically sync and access chat threads securely.

### 49. Delete Chat History
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Permanently deletes all messages and media in a specific chat thread from the user's account.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Thread Deletion. Swipe left on a conversation in the chats list, tap the three-dot "More" button, select "Delete," and confirm.

### 50. E-Secure Backup PIN
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Secures end-to-end encrypted chat backups in cloud storage using a user-defined 6-digit PIN.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Secure Recovery. In Chat settings > End-to-end encrypted chats > Secure storage, select "Create 6-digit PIN" and authenticate to enable cloud backups.
