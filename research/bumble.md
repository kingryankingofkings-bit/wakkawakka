# Bumble Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for Bumble, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. Profile Prompts & Icebreakers
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows users to answer pre-written questions (e.g., "My ideal Sunday is...") that display directly on their profile card to start conversations.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Bio Customization. In the profile editor, tap "Add prompt," select a prompt from the catalog, type your response in the text field, and save to publish.

### 2. Bumble Badges (Lifestyle, Interests, Work)
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Simple icon indicators that users can add to their profiles to quickly display height, zodiac sign, exercise frequency, relationship goals, or education levels.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Demographic Badges. Edit profile, scroll down to the "My Info" or "Life habits" section, select specific Badges (e.g., "Non-smoker," "Active"), and tap save.

### 3. Photo Verification Tool
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: A verification feature where the platform requests users to take a real-time photo copying a specific pose, certifying their identity and granting a blue checkmark badge.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Identity Verification. Tap the verification badge link in the profile settings, emulate the prompted hand pose in the camera view, snap the photo, and submit for moderator review.

### 4. Spotify Favorite Artists Link
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Integrates with a user's Spotify account to show their top played musical artists directly at the bottom of their Bumble profile page.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Music Integration. In the edit profile page, scroll down to the Spotify block, click "Connect Spotify," enter your Spotify account credentials to authorize, and select which top artists to display.

### 5. Bumble Bizz / BFF Bio Sections
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Provides distinct profile text editors and formatting options specifically tailored to professional networking (Bizz) or friendship seeking (BFF).
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Mode-Specific Profiles. Switch the app mode to BFF or Bizz via the side menu, click "Edit Profile," and fill out fields designed for work histories or friendly interests rather than romantic ones.

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. Swiping Profile Discovery Feed
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: The core swiping card interface that allows users to discover local profiles, swiping right to indicate interest or left to pass.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Swiping UI. On the main feed, swipe a user's profile card to the right to register a like or swipe it to the left to reject.

### 7. Advanced Search Filters (Premium Filters)
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Allows users to filter potential matches based on specific criteria such as political leanings, height, relationship intent, or education.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Filter Preferences. Click the swipe settings icon in the top right of the swipe screen, click "Advanced Filters," select your preferences, and toggle on "Apply filters."

### 8. Incognito Mode (Incognito Swiping)
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Hides a user's profile from the public swiping queue, showing their card only to users they have actively swiped right on.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Stealth Discovery. Navigate to Premium Settings, scroll down to "Incognito Mode," and toggle the switch to active to browse profiles privately.

### 9. Travel Mode
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Allows premium users to temporarily change their swipe location coordinates to another city, matching with users there before arriving.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Location Spoofer. Go to Settings > Travel Mode, search for and select your target city, and the discovery feed will refresh to show users in that location.

### 10. Bumble BFF & Bizz Modes Toggle
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Allows users to toggle their discovery feed among dating matches, friendly connections, or professional networking opportunities.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Mode Selector. Tap the mode header at the top center of the swiping screen, and select "Dating," "BFF," or "Bizz" from the drop-down menu.

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. Women-Initiate-First Match Control
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Enforces a double-opt-in rule where only female users can send the first message after a heterosexual match, with a 24-hour expiration window.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Matching Logic. Once a match is made in Dating mode, a 24-hour countdown timer begins. The female user must send a message to open the chat window, otherwise the match expires.

### 12. Opening Moves
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows users to set a default prompt question that matches can respond to immediately to open the conversation.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chat Openers. Go to Chat Settings > Opening Move, select a pre-written question or type a custom one, and it will be presented to matches to answer.

### 13. Extend Match Timer
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows users to add 24 hours to the countdown timer of an expiring match to keep the match from dissolving.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Expiration Extensions. Open the active match queue, click on the profile icon of the match that has less than 24 hours remaining, and click the "Extend" button.

### 14. Compliments Tool
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Enables users to send a short message to a potential match before an official match has occurred, attaching it to a specific profile card element.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Pre-Match Communication. Tap the yellow star icon on someone's profile prompt response or photo, type a message up to 150 characters, and send.

### 15. Speed Dating (Blind Chat Event)
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: A scheduled real-time event where users are paired to chat for three minutes without seeing each other's photos, revealing profiles only if both agree.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Blind Swiping. Join the event during designated hours via the Explore tab, chat with the paired user, and tap the "Match" button to view photos if the match is mutual.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. In-App Video & Voice Calling
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Enables matches to start video calls or voice chats directly inside the chat interface without sharing personal contact numbers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: In-App Calls. Open an active match chat window, tap the phone receiver icon for voice or the camera icon for video in the top right, and wait for connection.

### 17. Voice Notes in Chat
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows users to record and send voice messages directly into a match chat thread.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chat Voice Recording. Inside the chat window, tap and hold the microphone icon next to the text input box, record your audio message, and release to send.

### 18. Interactive Question Game
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: An interactive game built into the chat console where both matched users must answer the same card-style question before their answers are revealed.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Messaging Icebreakers. Inside a chat, tap the question card icon next to the input box, select a card, type your answer, and wait for your match to answer to reveal both responses.

### 19. Reaction Emojis & Stickers
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Permits users to apply emoji reactions directly to specific message bubbles in their chats.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Quick Reactions. Double-tap a received message bubble in the chat thread, and select one of the floating emojis (e.g., heart, laugh, thumbs up) to apply a reaction.

### 20. Photo & GIF Messenger
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Enables matched users to share images from their camera rolls or search and send animated GIFs.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Multimedia Chat. Tap the image icon to select a local photo, or tap the GIF button to search the Giphy directory and send files within the chat console.

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. Bumble Premium/Boost Subscriptions
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Paid recurring subscription packages that unlock premium features like unlimited swipes, backtracking, and advanced search filters.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Tiered Premium Services. Tap the profile silhouette icon, select "Upgrade to Premium," select a billing frequency (e.g., weekly, monthly, or lifetime), and complete checkout.

### 22. Spotlight Feature
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A premium purchase that highlights a user's profile card to the front of local discovery queues for 30 minutes.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Boost. Tap the Spotlight icon on the swiping screen, click "Use Spotlight," and your profile is prioritized. A timer displays on screen showing the remaining boost.

### 23. SuperSwipe Feature
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A paid interaction that lets another user know you are highly interested in them before they swipe on your card.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Premium Swiping. Tap the heart-shaped SuperSwipe icon on a user's profile card to swipe right with high priority, sending them a notification when your profile appears.

### 24. Bumble Coins In-App Currency
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: An in-app virtual currency that users can purchase and spend to activate Spotlights and SuperSwipes.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: App Wallet. Go to your profile settings, tap "Bumble Coins," select a coin package, and purchase using the mobile store payment framework.

### 25. Backtrack Feature (Premium)
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows premium users to undo an accidental left swipe (rejection) and return the profile to the screen.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Undo Swipes. If you swipe left accidentally, shake your phone or tap the undo arrow in the top left corner of the screen to reload the profile.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. "Beeline" Profile Views (Who Liked Me)
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Displays a list of profiles that have already swiped right on your card, allowing for instant matching.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Inbound Swipes list. Tap the chat bubble icon to view the match queue, click the gold-circled profile photo previewing who liked you, and browse the list of fans.

### 27. Match Rate Analytics (My Stats request)
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Provides users with a detailed downloadable summary of their profile performance, including swipe numbers and match rates.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: User Performance Data. Go to Settings > Contact Support > Request Data, and submit a ticket to receive a CSV file detailing your swipe history and statistics.

### 28. Profile Strength Meter
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: An interactive tracker displaying profile completeness and suggesting changes to optimize match rates.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Optimization Dashboard. Open the edit profile screen, observe the completeness percentage meter, and tap the recommended actions (e.g., "Add 3 badges to increase views").

### 29. Swipe/Match Peak Time Alerts
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Monitors local swipe volumes and notifies users when activity levels are high in their area.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Activity Alerts. The platform sends push notifications stating "Swiping is 3x higher now! Activate Spotlight to stand out."

### 30. Professional Resume Linker (Bumble Bizz)
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Allows Bizz mode users to link their professional resumes or sync their LinkedIn profiles to their bio cards.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Career Portfolios. In Bizz Mode, open the profile editor, scroll to the professional details, select "Link LinkedIn" or click "Upload Resume," and upload a PDF file.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. Snooze Mode (Mental Health Break)
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Pauses profile visibility in discovery feeds without deleting matches, chats, or profile details.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Temporary Deactivation. Go to Settings > Snooze, select a snooze duration (e.g., 24 hours, 72 hours, or indefinitely), and optionally select an away status message.

### 32. Private Detector (Automatic NSFW Blurring)
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Automatically detects and blurs explicit or NSFW photos sent in chats, prompting users with a safety warning before viewing.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Content Censorship. The platform analyzes incoming images. If a violation is flagged, it blurs the image, displays a warning dialog, and offers buttons to view or report.

### 33. Report and Block Tool
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Allows users to block and report profiles for harassment, fake photos, or behavior violating the terms of service.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Block & Report UI. Scroll to the bottom of the user's profile or click the three dots in a chat window, select "Block & Report," specify the reason, and submit.

### 34. Safety Center Hub
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: A dedicated in-app resource directory providing security guidelines, support helplines, and reporting instructions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Security Resources. Click your profile avatar, tap "Safety Center," and browse directories covering online security, dating safety tips, and mental health resources.

### 35. Unmatch Tool
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Allows users to dissolve a match, permanently deleting the chat history and removing profiles from both sides' feeds.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Match Dissolution. In the chat interface, tap the three dots in the top right corner, click "Unmatch," select a reason, and confirm to erase the conversation.

## Category 8: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 36. Instagram Feed Integration
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Connects with Instagram to display a grid of the user's latest 24 Instagram posts directly on their Bumble profile page.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Instagram Feed Sync. In the profile editor, click "Connect Instagram," log in on the redirect screen, authorize access, and select to display.

### 37. Spotify Top Tracks Integration
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Integrates with Spotify to let users choose a specific song to highlight as their profile "Anthem," playable by visitors.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Snippet Integration. Connect your Spotify account under profile editing, search for a track in the "My Anthem" bar, select it, and a 30-second play button will display on your profile.

### 38. Bumble Web Client Platform
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A web-based client that lets users swipe, manage matches, and message from desktop browsers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Desktop Swiping. Go to `bumble.com/app` in a browser, log in via phone number or Apple/Facebook credentials, and access the swiping interface.

### 39. Apple Health / Fitness Link
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Syncs activity metrics from Apple Health to automatically assign lifestyle badges (e.g., "Active").
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Health Integration. Go to Account Settings > Connections, authorize Apple Health sync permissions, and select specific activity metrics to showcase.

### 40. Google Sign-In API integration
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Connects Google authentication to allow secure, one-tap login and registration.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Login Integrations. On the app login screen, select "Continue with Google," choose your Google account in the OAuth popup, and log in securely.

## Category 9: Notifications & Time Management

### 41. Match Expiring Notifications
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Sends urgent push notifications to users when a match is within 1 hour of expiring to prompt messaging.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Expiration Alerts. If a match has 1 hour left on its timer, the system automatically sends a push notification stating "Your match with [Name] is about to expire! Say hello now."

### 42. Daily Swiping Limit Cap
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Restricts the number of right swipes a user can perform in a 24-hour period on the free tier, showing a countdown timer until reset.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Usage Limit. Once the daily limit of likes is reached, the app displays a full-screen block card with a countdown timer until the next swipe batch is ready.

### 43. Bumble BFF Event Notifications
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Sends push notifications and alerts when local mixers or BFF community events are scheduled in the user's area.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Local Alerts. Turn on "BFF Events" notifications in settings to receive push updates when local organizers host friendly events.

### 44. Message Read Receipts Toggle
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Allows users to control whether their matches can see when they have read direct messages.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Read Status Control. Go to Settings > Privacy settings > Read Receipts, and toggle the switch on or off.

### 45. Email Match Digests
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Compiles recent match numbers and profile views into a weekly email summary digest.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Email Summaries. Go to Settings > Email notifications, scroll down to the "Match digests" option, and toggle it to active.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. Phone Authentication with OTP
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows users to register and log in securely using their mobile number and a verification SMS code.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: OTP Verification. Enter your phone number on the login screen, receive a 6-digit verification code via SMS, and input the code to access the account.

### 47. Federated Apple ID Login
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Integrates Apple ID login allowing iOS users to log in securely using Face ID or Touch ID authentication.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: iOS Federated Login. Click "Log in with Apple" on the welcome screen, authenticate with Apple biometrics, and authorize profile mapping.

### 48. Account Deactivation (Pause Account)
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows users to temporarily hide their profile from the swipe queue while preserving active conversations.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Discovery Muting. Go to Settings > Account, and toggle off "Show me on Bumble" to withdraw your profile from queues while maintaining active chats.

### 49. Request GDPR Account Data
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Permits users to request a complete archive download of their profile data, history, and matches.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Data Archiving. Go to Settings > Privacy settings > Request data, and the platform will email a link to download your ZIP data archive within 30 days.

### 50. Account Permanent Deletion
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: A self-serve option to permanently delete a user profile and delete all account history from the platform databases.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Deletion. Go to Settings > Delete Account, type the word "DELETE" in the confirmation field, and click "Confirm" to delete all records.
