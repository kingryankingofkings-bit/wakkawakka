# X/Twitter Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for X/Twitter, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. Long-form Posts (Articles)
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows users to write posts containing up to 25,000 characters, supporting rich text formatting such as bold, italics, bulleted lists, and inline media embeds.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Rich Text Articles. Available to Premium and Premium+ subscribers; composed using the standard post editor on web or mobile, which automatically switches to long-form mode once the 280-character threshold is crossed.

### 2. Drafts Saving & Scheduling
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Enables users to write posts and schedule them to be published at a specific future date and time, or save them as drafts for editing later.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Post Scheduler. Accessed via the calendar icon in the post composer on the web client. Drafts are synchronized locally on mobile devices and accessible via the "Drafts" folder.

### 3. Interactive Polls
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows creators to embed a poll within a post, offering up to four response options and a customizable voting duration.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Interactive Poll Creator. Triggered by clicking the bar graph icon in the post composer. Voting durations range from 5 minutes to 7 days. Results display in real-time.

### 4. Post Threading (Thread Composer)
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Chains multiple posts together sequentially, allowing creators to tell longer stories or present multi-part ideas in a structured, connected format.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Multi-Post Composer. Accessed by clicking the "+" button within the active post composer to add another post to the thread. The entire thread can be published simultaneously.

### 5. Image & Video Editing (In-App)
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Provides built-in filters, cropping tools, sticker overlays, and video trimming tools before publishing media.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Media Editor. Triggered automatically after uploading a photo or video in the post composer. Users can select aspect ratios and trim videos to a maximum of 2 minutes and 20 seconds (or longer for Premium users).

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. Pinned Custom Lists
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Allows users to pin custom curated feeds of specific accounts as swipeable tabs on their home screen, letting them view niche feeds easily.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Custom Curated Feeds. Created under the "Lists" tab in the navigation bar. Pin up to 5 lists, which will display alongside the "For You" and "Following" tabs.

### 7. Bookmarks Collections
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Enables users to save posts privately, categorizing them into custom folders for easy discovery and retrieval.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Private Folders. Tap the share icon on any post, select "Bookmark." Premium users can organize bookmarks into folders by tapping "New Folder" inside the Bookmarks tab.

### 8. Topics to Follow
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Feeds the algorithm recommendations by allowing users to follow specific interest topics (e.g., Gaming, Technology) rather than just individual accounts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Interest-Based Algorithmic Filters. Managed in Settings > Privacy and safety > Content you see > Topics. Users can follow, unfollow, or block suggested topics.

### 9. Trending Summarizations (Grok News)
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Displays real-time summaries of current news events and trending hashtags using AI compilation.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: AI News Compilation. Appears at the top of the search feed under the "Explore" tab when clicking on a trending news keyword. Summarizes key posts.

### 10. Advanced Search Operators
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Allows users to perform precise searches using operators like `from:`, `to:`, `filter:links`, and dates to filter chronological results.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Granular Search Queries. Type query syntax (e.g., `from:X corp since:2026-01-01`) in the top search bar, or click "Advanced search" next to search results.

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. X Communities
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Dedicated, semi-private spaces centered around specific topics where users can post content seen primarily by community members.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Niche Sub-Forums. Found via the Communities tab. Community owners can set privacy to "Open," "Restricted," or "Private," and configure unique community moderation rules.

### 12. Pinned Posts in Communities
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Enables moderators and administrators of an X Community to lock a specific post at the top of the community feed.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Community Announcements. Accessed by clicking the three-dot icon on any post within the community feed and selecting "Pin to Community."

### 13. Spaces (Live Audio)
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Hosts or joins live, interactive audio conversations with other users on the platform.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Live Audio Streams. Started by holding the "+" icon on mobile and selecting "Spaces." Hosts can assign up to 2 co-hosts, invite 10 speakers, and record the broadcast.

### 14. Scheduled Spaces
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Enables hosts to schedule a live audio Space in advance, allowing followers to subscribe to launch notifications and generating a unique shareable link.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Event Planning. Press and hold the "+" icon in the bottom-right of the mobile app, tap the Spaces icon, select "Schedule for later," choose a date and time, and share the generated scheduling link on the timeline.

### 15. Affiliation Badge (Verified Organizations)
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Displays a square logo icon of a parent company next to an employee's or affiliate's checkmark badge.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Corporate Branding. Managed via the Portal for Verified Organizations, where admin users invite sub-accounts to join, confirming their official affiliation.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. End-to-End Encrypted DMs
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Secures private direct messages between two eligible users using cryptographic encryption, ensuring that X cannot read the messages.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Cryptographic Chats. In the mobile or web app, open a DM chat with an eligible user (both must be verified or Premium subscribers), tap the settings icon, and toggle on "Encryption" to start a secure conversation thread.

### 17. DM Voice Messages
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows users to record and send audio messages within their private chat threads instead of typing text.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Snippets. Accessed by tapping and holding the microphone icon in the chat input bar on the mobile app. Voice recordings are limited to 140 seconds.

### 18. Group DM Administration
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows users to create group chats with up to 150 participants and manage group-wide settings.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Group Moderation. Group creators can add or remove members, change the group photo/name, and generate a shareable join link via the chat info screen.

### 19. DM Reactions
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Enables users to quickly react to any individual direct message with a selection of emoji icons.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Message Reactions. Activated by double-tapping or long-pressing a received message, which displays a horizontal bar of emojis to apply.

### 20. Direct Message Filters & Requests
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Segregates incoming DMs from accounts you do not follow into a separate "Requests" folder, keeping the primary inbox clean.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Inbox Segmentation. Configured in Settings > Privacy and safety > Direct Messages, where users can choose to accept messages from "No one," "Only verified users," or "Everyone."

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. Location Spotlight
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Displays physical business coordinates, operating hours, contact telephone/email, and a miniature interactive map on the profile.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Location Maps. Configured through the "Edit Professional Profile" menu. Integrates with maps services to provide routing coordinates.

### 22. Shop Spotlight
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Showcases a carousel of up to five products with price tags directly at the top of a business profile.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: E-Commerce Showcase. Configured via the Professional Home dashboard by linking a product catalog from supported e-commerce partners (e.g., Shopify).

### 23. Link Spotlight
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Adds a prominent call-to-action button (e.g., "Book Now", "View Menu", "Shop Now") with an external hyperlink directly under the user's bio.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Custom CTA Buttons. Managed via the Profile Edit menu on professional accounts; provides standard CTA labels to improve click-through rates.

### 24. Creator Subscriptions
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows creators to lock specific posts, live spaces, and interactions behind a monthly subscription fee.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Subscriber Paywalls. Configured in the Monetization tab. Price points range from $2.99 to $9.99/month. Subscribed users receive a badge next to their names in replies.

### 25. Ad Revenue Sharing
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Distributes a portion of the advertisement revenue generated by ads placed in the reply sections of a creator's posts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Ads Revenue Share. Tap the profile icon on the top left, tap "Monetization," select "Ad Revenue Sharing," and tap "Join and Setup Payouts" if eligibility is met (requires Stripe account setup).

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. Post Analytics View
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Shows performance statistics for a specific post, detailing impressions, retweets, likes, detail expands, and profile clicks.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Performance Metrics. Accessible by tapping the bar chart icon located at the bottom-left of any individual post on mobile or desktop.

### 27. X Ads Manager Dashboard
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A mobile workspace for creators and professionals to view performance analytics, track growth benchmarks, and monitor monetization accounts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Creator Dashboard Hub. Tap "X Ads Manager Dashboard" on the side menu of the mobile application to access visual summaries of follower counts, impressions, and engagement rates over a 28-day range.

### 28. Media Studio Library
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: An advanced web-based dashboard at `studio.x.com` for scheduling videos, editing metadata, managing media libraries, and tracking video-specific analytics.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Media Manager. Access `studio.x.com` via a web browser, upload video assets to the library, set titles/descriptions, schedule publication times, and review video view retention graphs.

### 29. X Premium Analytics Portal
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A comprehensive analytics portal at `analytics.x.com` providing account-level summaries, monthly growth parameters, and follower interest trends.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Account Analytics. Log in to `analytics.x.com` on a web browser to review month-over-month summaries of top posts, total post impressions, profile visits, and mention counts.

### 30. X Ads Manager Insights
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: An advertising dashboard for tracking campaign reach, tracking pixels, conversion parameters, CPC, and demographic distributions.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Campaign Metrics Tracking. Log in to `ads.x.com`, select "Campaign Manager" to view detailed tables tracking ad impressions, spend, click-through rates (CTR), and custom tracking events.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. Reply Limits
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Restricts who can reply to a specific post, preventing unsolicited comments or targeted harassment.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Comment Gating. Set during post composition using the dropdown menu. Options are: "Everyone," "People you follow," or "Only people you mention."

### 32. Community Notes Contextual System
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Allows a community-driven moderation group to add helpful context notes to posts that contain misleading information.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Crowdsourced Fact-Checking. On any post containing a Community Note, tap the "Rate this note" button at the bottom of the note container. To apply to write notes, navigate to the web page x.com/i/communitynotes/signup in a browser, log in, verify eligibility, and tap "Join Community Notes."

### 33. Muted Words Filtering
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Hides posts containing specific keywords, phrases, hashtags, or handles from notifications and the timeline.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Keyword Muting. Set in Settings > Privacy and safety > Mute and block > Muted words. Users can choose duration (24 hours, 7 days, 30 days, or forever).

### 34. Hide Replies
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Enables a post author to hide specific replies to their post, moving them to a hidden tab.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Post-Level Hiding. Accessible via the three-dot icon on any reply to the user's post. Moves it to a separate, clickable "Hidden replies" page.

### 35. Private Account (Protected Posts)
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Gated posting where only approved followers can view or interact with your posts, preventing public search indexation.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audience Privacy Settings. Navigate to Settings > Privacy and safety > Audience and tagging, and toggle on "Protect your posts."

## Category 8: Notifications & Time Management

### 36. Quality Filter for Notifications
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Automatically filters out duplicate notifications or posts that appear to be automated spam.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Spam Muting. Managed in Notifications > Filters > Quality Filter. Toggling this on uses platform heuristics to silence low-quality activity.

### 37. Mute Notifications Settings
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Mutes notifications from accounts based on specific criteria (such as new accounts, or accounts that lack a profile picture).
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Advanced Alert Muting. Navigate to Settings > Notifications > Preferences > Mute Notifications, and check boxes next to specific criteria (e.g., "Those who don't follow you").

### 38. Email Digest Controls
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Customize frequency and categories of email reports, limiting updates to daily or weekly intervals.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Email Frequencies. Managed under Settings > Notifications > Preferences > Email notifications. Toggle off specific reports or set digests to weekly.

### 39. Smart Push Notifications
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Employs machine learning to send push notifications for highlights and posts you are highly likely to engage with.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Algorithmic Push Alerts. Configured under Settings > Notifications > Preferences > Push Notifications. Users toggle "Highlights" or "For You" notifications.

### 40. Notification Sound Customization
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Allows mobile app users to toggle or change the sound profiles for different alert categories (such as DMs vs mentions).
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Alert Tone Settings. Accessible through Settings > Notifications > Preferences, linking to system-level sound settings for the application.

## Category 9: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 41. X API Developer Access (v2)
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Programmatic access to post, search, and map tweets using developer plans.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: RESTful API Access. Developers create accounts on `developer.x.com` to configure projects, generate access tokens, and access endpoint paths.

### 42. OAuth 2.0 with PKCE Authorization Flow
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Standard security protocol allowing third-party developers to authenticate X users securely with granular scope permissions (read, write, DMs) without exposing user credentials.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Programmatic Login. Managed via `developer.x.com`. Developers configure their client application settings to enable OAuth 2.0, define redirect URIs, select appropriate scopes (e.g., `tweet.read`, `users.read`), and implement the PKCE challenge-verifier exchange.

### 43. X Webhooks (Account Activity API)
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Provides real-time notifications of user account activities (such as direct messages, tweets, retweets, and likes) to external developer servers via HTTPS webhooks.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Event Listening. Configured under `developer.x.com` under the Account Activity API dashboard. Developers register a secure HTTPS webhook URL and perform a CRC (Challenge-Response Challenge) handshake to verify the endpoint.

### 44. X Ads API
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Programmatic access to manage ad campaigns, target audiences, and retrieve campaign performance metrics.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Campaign Ad Automation. Developers obtain API approval, use OAuth access tokens, and send HTTP requests to endpoints like `/2/ads_accounts/{id}/campaigns` to manage ad spend.

### 45. X API v2 DMs Endpoint
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Programmatic endpoint allowing developers to send, receive, and manage direct messages and group conversations.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: DM API Integrations. Calls `POST /2/dm_conversations/with/{participant_id}/messages` using OAuth 2.0 user-context tokens to send direct messages programmatically.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. Advanced Security (Two-Factor Authentication)
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Adds an extra layer of protection by requiring a secondary verification code during account login.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: X Multi-Factor Settings. Configured in Settings > Security and account access > Security > Two-Factor Authentication. Supports security keys, authenticator apps, and SMS.

### 47. Hardware Key Authentication
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Supports physical USB or NFC security keys (e.g., YubiKeys) for hardware-based two-factor authentication.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Strong FIDO2 Credentials. Registered in the 2FA settings menu. Users tap their physical hardware key during login to authenticate.

### 48. X Archive Download
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Request a downloadable ZIP file containing complete account data, post history, media, and search history.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: GDPR Data Export. Settings > Your account > Download an archive of your data. Requires password re-entry and sends download link within 24-48 hours.

### 49. Account Deactivation Loop
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Initiates the deactivation process, which deletes all data permanently after a 30-day recovery window.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Deletion. Accessed in Settings > Your Account > Deactivate your account. Verify password to queue account for permanent deletion.

### 50. Social Sign-In OAuth
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Log in or register new accounts using Google or Apple credentials via secure OAuth protocols.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Federated Sign-On. On the login screen, click "Sign in with Google" or "Sign in with Apple" to skip password entry.
