# Instagram Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for Instagram, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. Reels Templates
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows users to reuse the exact timing, audio sync, and clip transitions of an existing, successful Reel.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Creative Templates. Tap the "Use template" prompt on any template-enabled Reel in the feed, select your own photos or video clips from your device's camera roll, and the app will automatically crop and sync them to the audio's beats.

### 2. Dual Camera Capture
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Enables capturing photos or videos using the front and rear cameras of the device simultaneously to capture action and reaction.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Multi-Camera Layout. Open the Stories or Reels camera, tap the down arrow on the left-hand toolbar to expand camera options, select "Dual," and tap the record button.

### 3. Grid Preview
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Allows creators to preview how a draft post or Reel will fit visually into their existing profile grid layout before publishing it.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Grid Layout. On the final upload screen of a draft post, tap "Show preview" to see a mock-up of your profile grid with the new post inserted, then drag and crop to adjust visual alignment.

### 4. Adjustable Reels Playback/Recording Speed
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Modifies the recording speed of video clips relative to the backing audio, allowing slow-motion or fast-forward effects.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Speed Customization. Open the Reels camera, tap the speed icon (marked as "1x" by default) on the left panel, and choose speed settings (0.3x, 0.5x, 1x, 2x, 3x, or 4x) before recording.

### 5. Carousel Posts
- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Post up to 10 photos or videos in a single swipeable feed post, allowing multi-media story collections.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Swipeable Media Carousel. Select multiple photos from the device library when uploading a feed post, arrange their order, and add separate filters or tags to each image.

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. "Not Interested" Feedback
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Instructs the Explore page and feed algorithms to stop recommending content similar to a selected post.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Algorithmic Preference Control. Tap on an unwanted post in the Explore tab, tap the three dots in the upper-right corner of the post, and select "Not Interested."

### 7. Interactive Map Search
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A map-based search tool showing geotagged posts and popular businesses near a selected location.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Geolocation Indexing. Tap the search icon, enter a city or neighborhood (e.g., "Downtown Seattle"), tap the search results, and tap the map icon to see top-rated public posts geotagged there.

### 8. Audio Page Search
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Groups all Reels using a specific audio track or sound, allowing creators to assess trends and save audio for future use.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Trending Audio Directory. Tap the scrolling audio track name at the bottom of any Reel to open the audio's dedicated page. Tap "Save audio" to store it in your creative vault.

### 9. Favorites Feed Filter
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Changes the main home feed to display only chronological posts from a pre-selected list of favorite accounts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Custom Home Feed Filters. Tap the "Instagram" logo in the top-left corner of the Home screen, and select "Favorites" from the dropdown list. Manage the list under Settings > Favorites.

### 10. Story Highlights
- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: Pins expired Stories to folders on the user's profile page, bypassing the default 24-hour disappearance rule.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Content Archives. Navigate to profile, tap "+" under "Story Highlights," select archived Stories from history, enter a folder name, choose a cover photo, and tap "Add."

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. Collaborative Posts (Collabs)
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows a single feed post or Reel to be co-authored by up to 4 accounts (1 owner + 3 collaborators), sharing likes, views, and comments across profiles.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Shared Reach Collaborations. On the final post creation screen, tap "Tag people," select "Invite collaborators," search for up to three partner accounts, and publish. The post is shared to their feeds and profile grids once they accept.

### 12. "Add Yours" Interactive Prompts
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Creates public, interactive chains in Stories where other users can respond to a prompt with their own Stories.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Public Story Chains. In the Story editing interface, tap stickers, select the "Add Yours" sticker, type a prompt (e.g., "Show your workspace"), and publish.

### 13. Interactive Story Polls
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Embeds interactive, two-option voting stickers in Stories to collect real-time feedback from viewers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audience Poll Stickers. Select the "Poll" sticker in Stories, input a question, customize the two answer fields, and share. Swipe up to view vote tallies.

### 14. Broadcast Channels
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: One-to-many messaging tool for creators to share text, voice notes, photos, and polls with fans.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Read-Only Creator Feeds. Go to DM inbox, tap the compose icon, select "Create broadcast channel," name it, and invite followers.

### 15. Live Rooms (Up to 4 Users)
- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Host live video broadcasts with up to three guests simultaneously in split-screen format.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Multi-Host Broadcasting. Open the Live camera, tap the "Rooms" icon, select the guests you want to invite, and start the broadcast to merge audiences.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. Silent Messages (`/silent`)
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Delivers a direct message without triggering a push notification or sound on the recipient's device.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Silent Message Delivery. Open a DM thread, type `/silent` at the very beginning of your message text followed by your content, and tap send.

### 17. Vanish Mode
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Deletes all messages, photos, and links within a direct message thread as soon as the recipient reads them and both users close the chat.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Ephemeral Private Chats. Open an active DM conversation, swipe up from the bottom of the screen until the "Release to turn on Vanish Mode" animation completes.

### 18. Emoji Message Reactions
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows users to quickly react to any individual direct message with a specific emoji instead of typing a text response.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Quick Reactions. Double-tap a message bubble to quickly apply a heart reaction, or long-press the message bubble to select any emoji from the drawer.

### 19. Hands-Free DM Voice Notes
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Records and sends voice messages in chats without requiring the user to hold down the microphone button.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Voice Note Recording. Open a DM, press and hold the microphone icon, swipe up toward the padlock icon to lock the recording, and tap send when finished.

### 20. Saved Replies (Quick Replies)
- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Allows professional and creator accounts to assign keyboard shortcuts to pre-written message templates.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Business Text Templates. Go to Settings > Creator/Business > Saved Replies, tap "+", enter a shortcut (e.g., "info"), and type the full message.

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. Feed & Reels Product Tagging
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Embeds interactive product links directly onto items shown in organic feed posts, Reels, or Stories.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Clickable Product Tags. During final upload, tap "Tag products," tap the photo/video, search for the item from your connected catalog, and select it to overlay a shopping tag.

### 22. Profile Storefront (Instagram Shop)
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Displays a dedicated shopping tab on a business's profile, hosting custom collections and catalog items.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Native Catalog Storefronts. Sync product catalog via Meta Commerce Manager, go to Settings > Business > Shopping, and enable the Shop feature.

### 23. In-App Checkout
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows customers to purchase tagged items directly inside Instagram using stored Meta Pay credentials.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Direct Checkout Processing. Viewers tap a tagged product, select "Add to bag" and "Checkout on Instagram," select shipping/billing options, and complete order.

### 24. Shopping in DMs
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Allows business owners to send custom shoppable invoices and product details directly inside DM threads.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Chat Invoice Processing. In a DM, tap the blue "+" icon in the DM toolbar, select "Product," choose an item from the synced catalog, and send it.

### 25. Instagram Subscriptions
- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Enables creators to charge a monthly fee in exchange for exclusive content, badges, and communication channels.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Instagram Creator Paywalls. Eligible creators go to Instagram Professional Dashboard > Subscriptions, set a monthly price, and publish subscription-only content.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. Instagram Professional Dashboard
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A mobile dashboard for creators and business accounts to track overall performance, access creator tools, and review educational materials.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Creator Performance Hub. Tap "Instagram Professional Dashboard" at the top of your profile page on the mobile app to access overall account reach, engagement trends, and monetization eligibility.

### 27. Instagram Post Insights
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Provides detailed reach, impression, and engagement analytics for individual posts, reels, or stories.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Individual Post Metrics. Tap the "View insights" link directly underneath a published post on your profile to see breakdowns of reach (followers vs non-followers), profile visits, and likes/shares.

### 28. In-App Content Scheduling
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Allows professional accounts to schedule feed posts and Reels up to 75 days in advance directly in the composer interface.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Instagram Scheduling Engine. In the post composer, scroll to the bottom, tap "Advanced Settings," toggle on "Schedule this post," and select the target date and time.

### 29. Audience Demographics Analytics
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Displays charts tracking follower growth and demographic information such as age ranges, gender, and top cities/countries.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Follower Growth Insights. Tap "Instagram Professional Dashboard," select "Total followers," and scroll to see charts detailing active times, top locations, and demographic splits.

### 30. Reels Play Insights
- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Details play counts, total watch time, average watch time, and initial vs repeat view rates for Reels.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Video Performance Metrics. Tap "View insights" on an individual Reel, and scroll down to the "Reels plays" section to view total plays, watch time, and retention curves.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. Close Friends List
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Restricts access to specific Stories and feed posts to a custom-curated subset of followers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Selective Audience Sharing. Go to Profile > Menu > Close Friends, check the boxes next to the followers you wish to include, and share Stories with the green Close Friends button.

### 32. Hidden Words Filtering
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Automatically hides comments and message requests that contain specific offensive keywords, phrases, or emojis defined by Meta or the user.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Content Muting Lists. Go to Settings > Privacy > Hidden Words. Toggle "Hide comments" and "Hide message requests" to active, and input custom phrases.

### 33. Limits Control
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Temporarily blocks or restricts comments and DMs from accounts that do not follow you, or have only followed you recently.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Viral Moderation Safety. Go to Settings > Privacy > Limits, select the group you want to limit, and set a duration limit (e.g., 3 days).

### 34. Restrict Account Option
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Quietly limits a problematic user's ability to interact with your profile without blocking them or notifying them.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Soft-Blocking Controls. Open the target user's profile, tap the three dots in the upper right, and select "Restrict." Their comments will only be visible to themselves.

### 35. Account Blocking with Ban Evasion Prevention
- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Blocks a user and preemptively blocks any new profiles they create under their IP or device footprint.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Hard Account Ban. Tap the three dots on a user's profile, select "Block," and choose the option "Block [username] and new accounts they may create."

## Category 8: Notifications & Time Management

### 36. Quiet Mode
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Pause push notifications and auto-reply to DMs during selected daily periods.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Sleep & Break Scheduling. Settings > Notifications > Quiet Mode. Enable the mode and select your daily start and end times to silence all pings.

### 37. Notification Digests
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Group notification updates into a single daily summary instead of instant push alerts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Alert Frequency Control. Settings > Notifications > Notification Digests. Toggle on and select a time of day to receive your summary.

### 38. Daily Limit Reminders
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Prompt users with a full-screen alert once they hit their set daily app limit.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Usage Cap Limiters. Settings > Your Activity > Time Spent > Set daily limit. Select a duration (e.g., 45 minutes) to receive the alert.

### 39. "Take a Break" Reminders
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Prompts users to close the app after a chosen duration of continuous scrolling, such as 10, 20, or 30 minutes, to manage time spent.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Digital Well-being Reminders. Settings > Your Activity > Time Spent > Set reminder to take breaks. Choose a time interval (e.g., 20 minutes) and tap "Done."

### 40. Push Notification Category Mutes
- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Allows users to customize and toggle push notifications for specific activity categories like likes, comments, and broadcasts.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Custom Alert Filters. Settings > Notifications. Tap on "Posts, stories and comments" or "Direct messages" and toggle notification groups to "Off", "From profiles I follow", or "From everyone".

## Category 9: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 41. Instagram Graph API
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Allows business profiles to schedule posts, manage comments, and track metrics externally.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Developer Endpoint Access. Programmatic RESTful API endpoints authorized via Facebook Login tokens for developer accounts.

### 42. Instagram Webhooks (Graph API Subscriptions)
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Delivers real-time event notifications to external servers whenever comments, mentions, or stories are posted on a professional account.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Real-time Webhooks. Configured via the App Dashboard on the Meta Developer Portal by subscribing to the `instagram` topic and verifying webhook URL endpoints.

### 43. Instagram Basic Display API
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Allows external websites to retrieve public profile details, images, and video files from public Instagram profiles.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Media Read API. Set up app registration on the Meta Developers portal, configure OAuth redirect URLs, and call the user profile media endpoints.

### 44. Messenger API for Instagram
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Enables developers to programmatically receive and respond to user direct messages and comments using external messaging platforms and webhooks.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: DM Automation. Configured on the Meta Developer Portal by linking an Instagram Professional Account to a Facebook Page, activating the Messenger API, and registering webhooks for the `instagram_messaging` scopes.

### 45. Instagram Graph API Publishing Endpoint
- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Programmatic endpoint allowing developers to upload photo or video binaries and publish them automatically as feed posts or Reels.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Automated Media Publishing. Calls the `POST /{instagram-business-account-id}/media` endpoint to upload the container, followed by the `media_publish` endpoint to post it.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Enhances account security by requiring a secondary verification code from an external source when logging in.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Instagram Multi-Factor Settings. Go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication, tap "Get Started," and select Authenticator App, WhatsApp, or Text Message.

### 47. Login Activity & Device Management
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Monitors and lists all active sessions logged into your account, providing locations, device models, and remote logout options.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Device Session Log. Navigate to Settings > Security > Login Activity. Review active devices and tap "Log out" for unrecognized devices.

### 48. Login with Facebook (Meta Single Sign-On)
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Authenticate and log in using linked Meta/Facebook account credentials.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Single Sign-On. On the login screen, click "Log in with Facebook" to utilize Meta's single-sign-on OAuth pipeline.

### 49. Download Your Information
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Request a downloadable archive file containing all your profile history, chat messages, uploaded media, and profile metrics.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: GDPR Data Portability. Settings > Account Center > Your Information and Permissions > Download Your Information. Request download links via email.

### 50. Account Status Dashboard
- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Provides a clear view of any policy infractions, copyright notices, or community guideline violations active on your profile.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Compliance Checking. Settings > Account Status. Check the indicators showing if your content can be recommended or if you have violated guidelines.
