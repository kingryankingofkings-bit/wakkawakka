# Snapchat Feature Research

This document compiles the feature research for Snapchat, categorized into the reconciled 10-category taxonomy. Each category contains exactly 5 unique, specific, and actionable features, totaling 50 features.

---

## Category 1: Content Creation & Editing

### 1. Snapchat Lenses

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Applies real-time augmented reality filters to faces or environments through the camera interface. This allows users to morph their appearance, play interactive mini-games, or project digital objects into their physical space.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Augmented Reality Overlays. Swipe left or right on the main camera screen to cycle through active Lenses, then tap or press and hold the capture button to take a photo or record a video.

### 2. Snapchat Filters

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Overlay artistic designs, location-specific geofilters, current time, temperature, or speed metrics onto captured photos or videos. These filters dynamically change based on physical location and real-time environmental context.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Graphic Overlays. After capturing a photo or video snap, swipe horizontally across the screen to preview and apply available filters.

### 3. Snapchat Sounds

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Adds licensed music tracks or custom audio recordings to snaps prior to sharing. Users can browse a vast library of trending songs or create their own sound clips to overlay on video content.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Overlay Editor. Tap the music note icon on the right-hand toolbar of the camera screen, browse or search for a track, select the specific audio segment, and add it to the snap.

### 4. Snapchat 3D Bitmojis in Snaps

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Places a personalized three-dimensional avatar of the user into the camera view using augmented reality. The avatar can be animated to perform dances, express emotions, or react to the environment in real time.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Avatar AR Projection. Open the Snapchat camera, tap the screen to activate the Lens carousel, search for your Bitmoji Lens, and drag the avatar to position it in your physical environment.

### 5. Snapchat Director Mode

- **Category**: Content Creation & Editing
- **Description**: Accesses a suite of professional video editing tools directly within the Snapchat app interface. Features include green screen overlays, dual-camera capture, speed controls, and a precise multi-clip trimmer.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Advanced Video Editor. Tap the video camera icon labeled "Director Mode" on the camera toolbar, select your desired tool like "Green Screen" or "Dual," and begin recording multi-clip content.

## Category 2: Content Discovery & Search

### 6. Snapchat Spotlight

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A dedicated, algorithmically driven feed that showcases short-form, vertical videos submitted by the community. Users can scroll endlessly to discover trending challenges, comedy sketches, and creative content from other creators.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Short-Form Video Feed. Tap the play button icon in the bottom-right corner of the main app navigation bar to open the Spotlight feed, then swipe up to browse videos.

### 7. Snapchat Map (Snap Map)

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: An interactive geographical map that displays the location of friends who choose to share their coordinates. It also aggregates public snaps onto a heatmap, allowing users to discover events and stories happening nearby or around the world.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Location-Based Discovery. Swipe down on the main camera screen to open the Snap Map, pinch to zoom, and tap on heat spots to view public stories submitted from that location.

### 8. Snapchat Discover

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A content hub featuring curated stories, shows, and articles from verified publishers, media companies, and popular creators. The layout uses tile-based previews that update daily with localized news, pop culture, and entertainment content.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Publisher Story Hub. Swipe left from the main camera screen past the chat tab, or tap the double-story icon at the bottom of the screen to browse the Discover directory.

### 9. Snapchat Scan

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: An intelligent visual search utility that uses the camera to identify objects in the physical world. It can recognize plants, scan food labels for nutritional info, solve math equations, or identify songs playing in the background via Shazam.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Visual Search Utility. Point the camera at an object, document, or QR code, then press and hold the center screen to activate the Scan utility and display information cards.

### 10. Snapchat Search Bar

- **Category**: Content Discovery & Search
- **Description**: A global search utility at the top of the interface used to find accounts, lenses, publisher shows, and trending topics. It provides instant suggestions as the user types and displays categorized results for quick navigation.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Global Navigation Search. Tap the magnifying glass icon in the top-left corner of the camera or chat screen, type your query, and tap on a user, Lens, or show from the search results.

## Category 3: Interpersonal & Community Engagement

### 11. Snapchat Streaks

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Tracks the consecutive number of days two friends have sent direct photo or video snaps to each other. This visual counter encourages daily communication and displays a flame emoji next to the contact's name.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Gamified Engagement Tracker. Send a photo or video snap to a friend, and ensure they send one back within 24 hours to initiate and maintain the streak counter next to their chat profile.

### 12. Snapchat Stories (My Story)

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Allows users to post chronological photo and video updates that are visible to their friends or the public. The compiled content remains viewable for exactly 24 hours before automatically expiring.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Ephemeral Status Updates. Capture a snap, tap the "Send To" button in the bottom right, select "My Story," and tap the send arrow to publish the snap.

### 13. Snapchat Public Profiles

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Dedicated profile spaces for creators, brands, and public figures to showcase their public content. These profiles allow users to add a bio, website link, subscriber count, and pin permanent stories called highlights.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Creator Profile Management. Tap your profile icon, scroll to the "Public Profile" section, tap "Create Public Profile," and edit your settings to display subscriber counts and pinned highlights.

### 14. Snapchat Group Chats

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Shared conversation spaces supporting up to 100 participants for messaging, media sharing, and group calls. Group chats allow users to send snaps that are delivered to all members simultaneously and support live voice or video conferencing.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Multi-User Chat Spaces. Tap the chat icon, click the pen-and-paper button in the bottom right, select the friends you want to add, and tap "Chat with Group" to begin the conversation.

### 15. Snapchat Bitmoji Outfits & Customization

- **Category**: Interpersonal & Community Engagement
- **Description**: Enables users to design and modify a digital avatar that represents them across the platform. Users can customize facial features, hair, and select clothing items from designer brands to update their look in chats and on the Snap Map.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Digital Avatar Editor. Tap your profile icon in the top-left corner, select your Bitmoji avatar, tap "Change Outfit" or "Edit Bitmoji," select your items, and tap "Save" to apply.

## Category 4: Direct Messaging & Communication

### 16. Snapchat Ephemeral Chat Messages

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Text, voice, and media messages sent in direct threads that are deleted by default after viewing. Users can tap on messages to save them in the chat history, which highlights them in grey.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Self-Deleting Direct Chat. Open a chat thread, type your message, and tap send. The message will disappear from the thread once the recipient opens and exits the chat.

### 17. Snapchat Live Video Calling

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Initiates real-time, face-to-face video conversations with individual contacts or groups. The video calling interface allows participants to apply active AR Lenses and filters during the live call.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Real-Time Video Calls. Open a chat thread, click the call button (camera symbol) in the header bar, and swipe to apply AR Lenses.

### 18. Snapchat Voice Notes

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Sends recorded voice messages directly within chat threads. This is useful for hands-free communication, displaying a waveform in the chat bubble that recipients can play at their convenience.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audio Chat Messages. Open a chat thread, press and hold the microphone icon next to the text entry box, speak your message, and release the icon to send.

### 19. Snapchat Chat Reactions

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Reacts to individual chat messages in a thread using specialized Bitmoji stickers or standard emojis. This allows users to quickly express feelings like laughter, shock, or approval without typing a text reply.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Emoji/Bitmoji Reactions. Long-press on a chat bubble in a conversation thread, then select an emoji or a customized Bitmoji sticker from the pop-up reaction bar.

### 20. Snapchat Map Location Sharing

- **Category**: Direct Messaging & Communication
- **Description**: Shares your live real-time location or static coordinates with a friend directly inside a chat thread. This allows friends to track your arrival or find where you are on the Snap Map.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Real-time Coordinate Sharing. Open a chat thread, tap the location pin icon or the "+" icon next to the chat bar, select "Share My Live Location" or "Send My Current Location," and choose the duration.

## Category 5: Monetization & E-Commerce

### 21. Snapchat Spotlight Challenges

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Direct monetization programs where creators can submit short vertical videos to specific topic challenges. Users compete for cash prizes by producing highly engaging content using featured sounds, lenses, or topics.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Creator Cash Challenges. Navigate to the Spotlight feed, tap the trending sound or lens name, select "Submit to Challenge" if available, capture your video, and submit.

### 22. Snapchat Store

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: An in-app digital marketplace where users can purchase official Snapchat hardware, brand merchandise, or exclusive virtual assets. Transactions are completed within the application using stored payment methods.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Digital In-App Shop. Tap your profile icon, scroll to the "Snap Store" or "Shop" category, select a product or digital item, enter shipping/payment details, and complete the checkout.

### 23. Snapchat AR Shopping Lenses

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: Interactive try-on experiences that allow users to virtually test cosmetics, eyewear, and clothing using the camera. These lenses feature product details and a direct "Shop" button that links to checkout pages.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Virtual Try-On Commerce. Open the camera, search for shopping lenses in the lens explorer, apply the lens to view the product on your face or body, and tap the "Shop" button to make a purchase.

### 24. Snapchat Creator Marketplace

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A business platform that connects verified Snapchat creators with brands looking to collaborate on sponsored marketing campaigns. Brands can search for creators based on audience demographics, vertical specialties, and location.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Creator Sponsorship Network. Access the Creator Marketplace portal on a web browser at `creators.snapchat.com`, log in with your creator account, update your portfolio, and view brand inquiries.

### 25. Snapchat Tokens

- **Category**: Monetization & E-Commerce
- **Description**: A virtual in-app currency that users can purchase with real money to gift to creators or buy digital items. Gifting tokens translates to financial rewards for creators, fostering an ecosystem of viewer support.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Digital Gifting Currency. Tap your profile icon, go to the Token Shop, select a token package, purchase it via app store billing, and tap the gift icon on a creator's public story to send tokens.

## Category 6: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools

### 26. Snapchat Insights for Creators

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A comprehensive analytics dashboard that provides data on story views, view time, subscriber growth, and audience demographics. This tool helps creators understand what content resonates best with their viewers.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Profile Analytics Dashboard. Tap your public profile, select "Insights," and toggle between views to track metrics such as reach, age splits, and top geographic locations over a 7-day or 28-day period.

### 27. Snapchat Ads Manager

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A self-serve web application that enables businesses to create, target, and run advertisement campaigns across Snapchat. It supports formats like single image/video ads, collection ads, and story ads with budget optimization.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Advertising Campaign Portal. Open a web browser, navigate to `ads.snapchat.com`, log in with your business account, select "Create Campaign," set your budget, define target demographics, and upload ad creatives.

### 28. Snapchat Ads Manager Audience Insights

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A research tool within Ads Manager that helps businesses identify and analyze Snapchat audience segments. It displays detailed information on user behaviors, purchase intent, device usage, and interests.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Audience Research Hub. Log in to Ads Manager, select "Audience Insights" from the main menu, apply target filters (location, age, gender), and view visual reports on demographic profiles and lifestyle categories.

### 29. Snapchat Lens Web Builder

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: A web-based creation platform designed for brands to build custom augmented reality lenses without writing code. It offers template libraries, simple customization fields, and immediate publishing options for ad campaigns.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Browser Lens Creator. Go to `lensbuilder.snapchat.com` on a desktop web browser, select a pre-made template, upload custom logos or 2D assets, set active parameters, and submit the lens for review.

### 30. Snapchat Brand Profiles

- **Category**: Analytics, Business & Creator Tools
- **Description**: Permanent, customizable homepages for businesses to showcase their brand assets, active AR lenses, highlights, and connected stores. This serves as a central landing page for organic Snapchat traffic.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Business Landing Page. Managed via Meta Business Manager or Snapchat Business Suite. Access your brand profile settings to customize logos, link shopping catalogs, and pin highlights.

## Category 7: Privacy, Security & Safety

### 31. Snapchat My Eyes Only

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: A secure, password-protected folder within the Memories section of the app. It allows users to hide sensitive snaps from their main gallery, requiring a separate 4-digit PIN or passcode to view them.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Private Media Vault. Swipe up from the camera screen to open Memories, tap "Select" in the top-right, choose snaps, tap the "More" option, select "Move to My Eyes Only," and set up your passcode.

### 32. Snapchat Ghost Mode

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: A privacy setting that hides a user's location from all friends on the Snap Map. When activated, the user's Bitmoji disappears from the map, but the user can still view the locations of friends who share their coordinates.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Location Sharing Opt-Out. Swipe down on the camera screen to open the Snap Map, tap the gear icon in the top-right corner, and toggle on the "Ghost Mode" switch.

### 33. Snapchat Quick Add Privacy

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: A settings toggle that controls whether a user's account is suggested to others in the "Quick Add" section. Disabling this prevents acquaintances or strangers from finding the account via mutual connections.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Suggestion Control Settings. Tap your profile icon, tap the gear icon to open Settings, scroll down to "Privacy Controls," select "See Me in Quick Add," and uncheck the box.

### 34. Snapchat Custom Story Privacy

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: Enables users to create private or custom stories where they manually choose which friends can view and add content. This provides absolute control over who participates in shared digital scrapbooks.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Restricted Audience Stories. Tap your profile icon, select "+ New Story," tap "Custom Story" or "Private Story," choose the allowed friends from your contacts list, name the story, and tap "Create Story."

### 35. Snapchat In-App Reporting

- **Category**: Privacy, Security & Safety
- **Description**: A reporting tool that allows users to submit objectionable content or abusive profiles directly to safety teams. The system categorizes reports (harassment, spam, self-harm) to expedite review and action.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Content Moderation Reports. Press and hold on the offending snap, story tile, or username in chat, tap the flag icon or "Report" button, select the reason for the report, and submit.

## Category 8: Notifications & Time Management

### 36. Snapchat Notification Customization

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Allows users to manage the frequency and type of alerts they receive on their mobile device. Users can toggle notifications for snaps, chat messages, story updates, and friend suggestions independently.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Alert Frequency Settings. Tap your profile icon, click the gear icon for Settings, select "Notifications," and check or uncheck individual alert categories to match your preference.

### 37. Snapchat Custom Story Expiration

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Allows Snapchat+ subscribers to customize the duration of their stories, changing them from the standard 24-hour window. Stories can be set to expire in 1 hour, 4 hours, 12 hours, 2 days, or up to 1 week.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Flexible Expiration Timers. Create a snap, select "My Story," tap the gear icon next to it, choose your desired expiration limit, and publish the snap.

### 38. Snapchat Sleep Mode

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Integrates with system-level focus modes or in-app mute features to pause all push alerts during sleeping hours. When active, incoming snaps and messages are received silently without waking the screen.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Silent Hours Scheduler. Open Settings > Notifications, tap "Quiet Hours" or "Do Not Disturb," and schedule the start and end times to mute alerts automatically.

### 39. Snapchat Story Notification Muting

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Mutes story alerts for specific friends who post updates frequently. This allows users to stay connected without getting constantly interrupted by push alerts whenever that contact posts a new snap.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Selective Alert Muting. Tap and hold your friend's profile name in the chat feed, select "Story Settings," and toggle off "Story Notifications."

### 40. Snapchat Chat Expiration Controls

- **Category**: Notifications & Time Management
- **Description**: Configures whether chat messages in a direct thread are deleted immediately after viewing or 24 hours after they are read. This gives users flexibility in managing their conversation archives.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Message Retention Toggles. Long-press on a friend's chat header in your feed, select "Chat Settings," tap "Delete Chats...", and choose between "After Viewing" and "24 Hours after Viewing."

## Category 9: Developer APIs & Integrations

### 41. Snapchat Camera Kit SDK

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A software development kit that allows developers to integrate Snapchat's camera features and augmented reality lenses directly into their own mobile applications. This extends Snapchat's AR ecosystem to external platforms.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Mobile AR Integration. Developers access the Snap Developer Portal, download the Camera Kit SDK for iOS/Android, and initialize it within their app codebase using API keys.

### 42. Snapchat Login Kit

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: An identity integration SDK that allows developers to add secure "Log in with Snapchat" functionality to external websites and applications. It simplifies user registration by retrieving the user's display name and Bitmoji avatar.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: OAuth Federated Identity. Register your application on the Snap Developer Portal, configure OAuth redirect URLs, and import the Login Kit SDK into your client application.

### 43. Snapchat Creative Kit

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A developer utility that enables external apps to pass media, links, or dynamic stickers directly to the Snapchat camera. This lets users share third-party app experiences, such as music tracks or achievements, as a custom snap sticker.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Shared Media Pipeline. Developers implement the Creative Kit library in their app and configure a button that calls the Snapchat share intent with custom payload assets.

### 44. Snapchat Bitmoji Kit

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: Allows developers to import and display users' personalized Bitmoji avatars inside third-party mobile applications and web tools. This creates cross-platform personalized profiles using Snapchat's avatar ecosystem.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Avatar Sync SDK. Download the Bitmoji Kit from the Snap Developer Portal, link it to your app's login flow, and request permission to display the user's custom sticker pack.

### 45. Snapchat Marketing API

- **Category**: Developer APIs & Integrations
- **Description**: A programmatic REST API that allows developers and agencies to manage ad campaigns, automate ad creation, and update budgets. It enables direct programmatic access to extract performance metrics from Snap's advertising database.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: REST Campaign Endpoint. Integrate OAuth2 tokens, and execute HTTP requests to the Marketing API endpoints to manage ad structures.

## Category 10: Account Settings & Authentication

### 46. Snapchat Two-Factor Authentication

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Adds an extra layer of security to the login process by requiring a verification code in addition to a password. Users can receive codes via SMS or generate them using an external authenticator application.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Two-Step Verification. Tap your profile icon, tap the gear icon for Settings, select "Two-Factor Authentication," tap "Continue," and choose "SMS Verification" or "Authenticator App."

### 47. Snapchat Username Modification

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Allows users to change their public Snapchat username once per year. This updates how friends search for the user without losing their friend list, streaks, or Memories.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Account Identifier Settings. Navigate to Settings, tap "Username," select "Change Username," enter the new name, verify availability, and enter your password to confirm.

### 48. Snapchat Account Portability

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Compiles a secure digital folder containing the user's complete Snap history, including chat messages, geofilter usage, account logs, and saved Memories. This enables users to retrieve their multimedia content off the platform.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Memories and Media Downloader. Visit `accounts.snapchat.com` on a web browser, select "My Data", choose whether to include Memories, and submit a download request.

### 49. Snapchat Connected Devices

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Displays a list of all mobile devices and web browsers that are currently authorized to access the Snapchat account. Users can review device names, login dates, and revoke authorization for unfamiliar devices.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Session Device Auditing. Go to Settings > Two-Factor Authentication > Forget Devices, select the device you want to remove, and tap the delete "X" to log it out immediately.

### 50. Snapchat Account Deletion Request

- **Category**: Account Settings & Authentication
- **Description**: Initiates a request to permanently deactivate and delete a Snapchat account. The account is immediately deactivated for 30 days, during which the user can log in to cancel the deletion before it becomes permanent.
- **Sub-category / Actionable Details**: Account Deactivation Portal. On the web, visit `accounts.snapchat.com` and select "Delete My Account," or scroll to the bottom of the in-app Settings menu and select "Delete Account."
